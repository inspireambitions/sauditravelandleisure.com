Visa and Entry Requirements

Visa on Arrival: How It Works for Canadians

Canadians arriving in Dubai are eligible for a free 30-day tourist visa stamped in their passport upon landing. It is straightforward: arrive at immigration, present your passport (with at least six months validity recommended), and wait for the stamp. What the 30-Day Visa Allows

  • Single-entry entry for 30 days from arrival.
  • Possible one-time extension (fees apply) for a further 30 days.
  • Suitable for short vacations or business trips under one month.

Why Many Canadians Choose a Pre-Approved Visa

A pre-approved visa, obtained online before departure, removes uncertainty and lets you tailor the length and entry flexibility of your stay. Pre-approved options commonly include 30-day and 60-day tourist visas, and multiple-entry visas for travelers who plan to move between emirates or neighboring countries.

Advantages:

  • Sufficient funds for your stay.
