Entry Requirements: Visas, Passport Rules, and Immigration

Who Needs a Visa to Enter Dubai?

Dubaiâs entry rules vary significantly by nationality, residency status, and travel purpose. The UAE offers multiple pathways for visitors:

  • Transit visas: Short-term 48-hour and 96-hour transit visas are available for eligible passengers connecting through Dubai who want to leave the airport during a layover.

Laws, Customs, and Cultural Etiquette

Dubai is comparatively liberal by Gulf standards but remains a Muslim society with laws reflecting conservative values. When in public:
