StepГўВЂВ‘ByГўВЂВ‘Step: How To Apply For An Account On A Visit Visa

Common Problems and How To Avoid Them

Solution: Ask the bank what additional documents will lift the restriction and provide them promptly. If the bank requires a local delivery address for a debit card, arrange temporary accommodation or use a business partnerвЂ™s address with prior permission.

Fees, Limits, and Practical Costs You Should Expect
