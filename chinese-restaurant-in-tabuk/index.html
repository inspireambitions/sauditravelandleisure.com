Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Tabuk You Must Try

Updated September 13, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Tabuk, located in the northwestern part of Saudi Arabia, is known for its rich history, growing economy, and vibrant cultural scene. Alongside traditional Saudi cuisine, international food is becoming increasingly popular here — and Chinese cuisine is at the top of the list. From sizzling stir-fries to aromatic noodle soups, Tabuk has some excellent restaurants serving authentic Chinese flavors.

In this guide, we highlight the 10 best Chinese restaurants in Tabuk, covering everything from upscale dining spots to casual eateries.

What Makes a Good Chinese Restaurant in Tabuk?

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Tabuk

1. Dragon City Chinese Restaurant

Dragon City is a popular spot in Tabuk, known for flavorful stir-fried rice, sizzling noodles, and delicious seafood dishes. The family-friendly setup and consistent taste make it a local favorite.

2. Baytoti

A well-known chain across Saudi Arabia, Baytoti delivers authentic Chinese flavors at affordable prices. Their sweet-and-sour chicken, chow mein, and fried rice are top picks.

3. China Town Restaurant

China Town offers a cozy dining environment with a diverse menu. Known for dumplings, hot soups, and Szechuan chicken, it’s a great spot for casual meals with friends and family.

4. P.F. Chang’s

For an upscale Chinese dining experience in Tabuk, P.F. Chang’s is a go-to choice. Famous for its Mongolian beef and dynamite shrimp, it’s ideal for celebrations, business dinners, and date nights.

5. Golden Chopsticks

Golden Chopsticks brings bold flavors and generous portions. Popular dishes include fried noodles, spicy beef, and vegetable stir-fries. The simple décor and fair prices make it a must-try.

6. Red Lantern Restaurant

The Red Lantern specializes in both Chinese and pan-Asian dishes. Guests enjoy fried rice, wonton soups, and crispy chicken. Its warm atmosphere makes it perfect for family outings.

7. Hua Ting Chinese Restaurant

Hua Ting stands out for its authentic Szechuan cuisine. From mapo tofu to chili chicken, it’s a top pick for spice lovers in Tabuk.

8. Lucky Panda

Lucky Panda is a casual eatery serving quick and affordable Chinese meals. Their fried rice, noodle bowls, and dumplings are popular with students and young professionals.

9. China Garden

China Garden combines stylish interiors with a broad range of dishes, including sweet-and-sour chicken, shrimp fried rice, and dim sum. It’s a versatile restaurant for families and groups.

10. Noodle House

As the name suggests, Noodle House specializes in noodle-based dishes and soups. With affordable prices and a casual vibe, it’s perfect for quick lunches or light dinners.

Comparison Table Of Best Chinese Restaurants in Tabuk

Final Words

Tabuk’s food scene is expanding, and Chinese cuisine is at the forefront of this growth. Whether you’re in the mood for affordable comfort food at Lucky Panda, authentic spicy dishes at Hua Ting, or a premium dining experience at P.F. Chang’s, Tabuk has something for every palate and budget. Try out these top 10 Chinese restaurants in Tabuk and enjoy an authentic taste of China without leaving the city!
