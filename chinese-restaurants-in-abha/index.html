10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Abha You Must Try

Updated September 11, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Abha, the jewel of the Asir region, is famous for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and vibrant culture. But did you know it also has a growing food scene that includes international cuisines like Chinese? From sizzling stir-fries to savory dumplings, Abha’s Chinese restaurants are delighting locals and tourists alike. Whether you’re looking for a casual meal or a more elegant dining experience, we’ve rounded up the 10 best Chinese restaurants in Abha that will satisfy your cravings.

What Makes a Good Chinese Restaurant in Abha

When choosing a Chinese restaurant in Abha, consider the following:

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Abha

1. Alkarkand Restaurant

Alkarkand is known for its fresh seafood and classic Chinese dishes. With cozy interiors and a welcoming staff, it’s a go-to for both residents and travelers. Favorites include stir-fried vegetables, sweet-and-sour chicken, and seafood platters, making it perfect for a relaxed family dinner.

2. Maharajah Indian & Chinese Restaurant

While Maharajah primarily serves Indian cuisine, its Chinese dishes are surprisingly authentic and flavorful. The menu offers unique fusion dishes where Indian spices meet traditional Chinese recipes, creating a memorable taste experience. Ideal for those looking for variety in one meal.

3. Taj Royal Restaurant

Located in Abha City Center, Taj Royal offers a wide-ranging menu including Chinese favorites. Its modern decor, attentive service, and clean setting make it a popular choice for family gatherings. Try their dumplings, stir-fried noodles, and specialty sauces for a satisfying meal.

4. Hajir Shahrani Restaurant

Hajir Shahrani provides a casual and affordable dining experience. While it specializes in Asian dishes, its Chinese menu items—like fried rice, stir-fried chicken, and chow mein—are flavorful and well-loved by locals. A great spot for a relaxed lunch or dinner.

5. Baytoti

Baytoti has a reputation across Saudi Arabia for delivering reliable and tasty Chinese food. In Abha, its branch serves classic dishes like sweet-and-sour chicken, egg fried rice, and noodles. Quick service and consistent quality make it perfect for everyday dining.

6. Asuka

Asuka focuses on Japanese cuisine but includes a selection of Chinese dishes. Its minimalist interiors and focus on fresh, quality ingredients make it a hit among those looking for Asian flavors beyond just sushi. Try the stir-fried noodles and vegetable dishes.

7. Raj Indian & Chinese Restaurant

Raj offers a fusion menu combining Indian spices with traditional Chinese dishes. It’s a favorite for groups wanting variety, offering dishes like chicken chow mein, sweet-and-sour tofu, and spicy stir-fried vegetables in a vibrant, casual setting.

8. Rusticana

Rusticana is known for its smoked meats but also serves a range of Chinese dishes. The rustic ambiance, friendly service, and generous portions make it ideal for diners who want a mix of international flavors in one place.

9. Soho Square Restaurant

Soho Square offers an extensive menu that includes Chinese favorites alongside other international cuisines. Modern interiors and attentive service make it perfect for both casual dining and special occasions. Popular dishes include kung pao chicken and stir-fried seafood noodles.

10. Al Baik

Primarily famous for its fried chicken, Al Baik also features Chinese-inspired items like spicy noodles and fried rice. Its fast service and consistent quality make it a convenient option for a quick, satisfying meal.

Comparison Table Of Best Chinese Restaurants in Abha

Final Words

Abha’s Chinese dining scene is full of variety, from cozy casual spots to modern restaurants offering unique fusion menus. Whether you’re craving traditional dishes like fried rice and dumplings or adventurous fusion creations, these 10 restaurants are sure to satisfy your cravings. Explore them all and discover your personal favorite!

FAQ About Chinese Restaurants in Abha

1. Is there authentic Chinese food in Abha?

Yes. Restaurants like Alkarkand and Baytoti serve authentic flavors, while smaller eateries often provide surprisingly genuine dishes.

2. Which restaurant is best for dim sum?

Taj Royal and Alkarkand are popular choices for fresh dim sum.

3. Are there vegetarian options in these restaurants?

Most Chinese restaurants in Abha offer vegetable stir-fries, tofu dishes, and mixed vegetable noodles.

4. How much does a typical Chinese meal cost in Abha?

Casual meals cost around SAR 20–40, while upscale dining ranges from SAR 60–120 per person.

5. Do I need to reserve a table?

For popular spots like Taj Royal, reservations are recommended, especially on weekends. Smaller casual restaurants usually accept walk-ins.
