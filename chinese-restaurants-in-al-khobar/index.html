10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Al Khobar You Must Try

Updated September 11, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Khobar, a vibrant city in Saudi Arabia’s Eastern Province, is known for its lively waterfront, modern malls, and cosmopolitan dining scene. Among the many international cuisines available, Chinese food has carved a strong presence. From flavorful stir-fries to delicate dim sum, Al Khobar offers a wide variety of Chinese restaurants for locals and tourists alike. In this guide, we highlight the 10 top Chinese restaurants in Al Khobar that offer unforgettable flavors, inviting atmospheres, and great value.

What Makes a Great Chinese Restaurant in Al Khobar

When choosing a Chinese restaurant, here are the key factors to consider:

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Al Khobar

1. Dragon City Chinese Restaurant

Dragon City is a top choice for authentic Chinese flavors in Al Khobar. Its menu includes stir-fried seafood, sweet-and-sour chicken, and fried rice. The modern and family-friendly ambiance makes it perfect for gatherings or casual dinners.

2. Baytoti

Baytoti is a reliable chain known across Saudi Arabia for its classic Chinese dishes. In Al Khobar, it offers favorites like chow mein, dumplings, and stir-fried vegetables. The casual atmosphere and consistent quality make it ideal for everyday meals.

3. P.F. Chang’s

For an upscale dining experience, P.F. Chang’s delivers with stylish interiors, attentive staff, and beautifully presented dishes. Popular items include Mongolian beef, Kung Pao shrimp, and hand-rolled dumplings, making it a great option for special occasions or business dinners.

4. China Garden

Located near Al Khobar Corniche, China Garden combines elegant décor with a wide Chinese menu. Dim sum, hot and sour soup, and seafood stir-fries are highly recommended. The restaurant’s clean and spacious setting is suitable for families and groups.

5. Hua Ting Chinese Restaurant

Hua Ting is known for bold Szechuan flavors, including spicy chicken, mapo tofu, and garlic noodles. The vibrant atmosphere and flavorful dishes make it a favorite for spice lovers looking for a casual dining experience.

6. Lucky Panda

Lucky Panda offers quick, delicious Chinese meals ideal for lunch or casual dinners. Favorites include fried rice, chow mein, and noodle bowls. The restaurant is affordable and popular among students and office workers in Al Khobar.

7. Red Dragon

Red Dragon is known for generous portions and a wide variety of Chinese dishes. Diners recommend seafood combinations, sweet-and-sour chicken, and stir-fried noodles. The comfortable setting makes it suitable for both family meals and friendly gatherings.

8. Sichuan Delight

Sichuan Delight specializes in authentic Szechuan cuisine. Its menu features bold, spicy dishes with aromatic flavors. Casual and cozy, it’s ideal for diners who enjoy intense, flavorful Chinese meals.

9. Golden Wok

Golden Wok is praised for fresh ingredients and consistently good food. The menu offers favorites like dumplings, fried rice, and stir-fried vegetables. Its modern, casual atmosphere appeals to families and small groups alike.

10. Noodle House

Noodle House focuses on noodle-centric dishes, soups, and stir-fries. Affordable and casual, it’s a go-to for those looking for a quick and satisfying Chinese meal. Efficient service and simple décor make it perfect for takeout or dine-in.

Comparison Table Of Best Chinese Restaurants in Al Khobar

Final Words

Al Khobar offers a rich and diverse Chinese dining scene, ranging from upscale restaurants like P.F. Chang’s to casual favorites like Lucky Panda or Noodle House. Whether you’re craving classic fried rice, spicy Szechuan dishes, or dim sum, these 10 restaurants provide an excellent culinary journey. Explore them to find your new favorite Chinese flavors in Al Khobar!

FAQ About Chinese Restaurants in Al Khobar

1. Are there authentic Chinese restaurants in Al Khobar?

Yes. Restaurants like Dragon City, Hua Ting, and Sichuan Delight offer authentic Chinese flavors.

2. Which restaurant is best for dim sum in Al Khobar?

China Garden is widely recommended for its fresh and flavorful dim sum.

3. Are vegetarian options available?

Most Chinese restaurants provide vegetable stir-fries, tofu dishes, and fried rice suitable for vegetarians.

4. How much does a typical meal cost?

Casual meals range from SAR 20–50, while upscale restaurants may charge SAR 80–150 per person.

5. Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are recommended for popular restaurants like P.F. Chang’s and China Garden, especially on weekends. Smaller casual restaurants usually accept walk-ins.
