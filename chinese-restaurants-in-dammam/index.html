10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Dammam You Must Try

Updated September 11, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Dammam, the heart of the Eastern Province, is not just a hub for business and culture—it’s also home to a growing international food scene. Chinese cuisine has become a favorite among locals and visitors alike, offering everything from spicy stir-fries to comforting noodle soups. Whether you’re looking for a fine dining experience or a quick casual bite, Dammam has something to satisfy every craving. Here’s a guide to the 10 top Chinese restaurants in Dammam that you should check out.

What Makes a Great Chinese Restaurant in Dammam

When selecting a Chinese restaurant in Dammam, consider the following factors:

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Dammam

1. Dragon City Chinese Restaurant

Dragon City is a popular choice for authentic Chinese cuisine. Known for its sizzling dishes and seafood platters, the restaurant offers both casual and semi-formal dining. Favorites include sweet-and-sour chicken, chow mein, and Peking-style duck. Its modern interior makes it a great spot for family dinners or small gatherings.

2. Baytoti

Baytoti is a well-known chain across Saudi Arabia, and its Dammam branch is no exception. Offering classic Chinese dishes like fried rice, dumplings, and stir-fried vegetables, Baytoti is perfect for quick and reliable meals. The casual environment and reasonable prices make it ideal for everyday dining.

3. China Garden

Located in central Dammam, China Garden combines elegant décor with a broad Chinese menu. Diners love their dim sum, seafood stir-fries, and hot and sour soup. The restaurant provides a comfortable, family-friendly setting and attentive service, making it a great choice for special occasions.

4. P.F. Chang’s

For those seeking an upscale experience, P.F. Chang’s in Dammam offers a refined take on Chinese cuisine. The stylish interiors, professional staff, and beautifully presented dishes like Mongolian beef and Kung Pao shrimp make it ideal for celebrations or business dinners.

5. Hua Ting Chinese Restaurant

Hua Ting is a hidden gem known for authentic Szechuan flavors. Its menu includes spicy chicken, mapo tofu, and stir-fried noodles. Casual yet vibrant, it’s perfect for diners who enjoy bold and flavorful dishes.

6. Lucky Panda

Lucky Panda specializes in quick, tasty Chinese meals. Its menu features fried rice, chow mein, and noodle bowls that are perfect for lunch breaks or casual dinners. Affordable prices and speedy service make it popular among students and office workers.

7. Red Dragon

Red Dragon offers a wide variety of Chinese dishes with generous portion sizes. Diners recommend their seafood combinations and sweet-and-sour chicken. With comfortable seating and a casual atmosphere, it’s an excellent spot for families or friends.

8. Sichuan Delight

Sichuan Delight is a must-visit for spice lovers. True to its name, the restaurant focuses on authentic Szechuan recipes, offering dishes with bold flavors and aromatic spices. It’s casual and cozy, making it perfect for a flavorful, casual dining experience.

9. Golden Wok

Golden Wok is known for fresh ingredients and consistent quality. The menu covers a broad range of Chinese favorites, including fried rice, dumplings, stir-fried vegetables, and seafood dishes. The ambiance is casual but modern, suitable for families and small groups.

10. Noodle House

Noodle House focuses on noodle-based dishes, soups, and stir-fried favorites. It’s casual, affordable, and popular among those looking for a quick yet satisfying Chinese meal. Its simple décor and efficient service make it a convenient choice.

Comparison Table of Best Chinese Restaurants in Dammam

Final Words

Dammam’s Chinese restaurants offer something for everyone—from refined fine dining at P.F. Chang’s to the quick, flavorful meals at Lucky Panda or Noodle House. Whether you want spicy Szechuan dishes, seafood platters, or classic fried rice, these 10 restaurants are the perfect places to satisfy your cravings. Explore them all and discover your favorite Chinese flavors in Dammam!
