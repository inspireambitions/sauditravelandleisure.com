10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Jazan You Should Try

Updated September 13, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jazan, a beautiful coastal city in southwestern Saudi Arabia, is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and growing modern lifestyle. While the city is famous for its local seafood and Arabian flavors, Chinese cuisine has gained a strong presence here. From sizzling stir-fries to dumplings and hearty noodle soups, Jazan offers several dining spots where you can enjoy authentic Chinese flavors.

If you’re looking for the best Chinese restaurants in Jazan, this guide highlights the top 10 spots you must explore.

What Makes a Chinese Restaurant in Jazan Special?

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Jazan

1. Dragon House Restaurant

Dragon House is one of Jazan’s most popular Chinese restaurants, serving sizzling noodles, fried rice, and crispy chicken dishes. The spacious setting makes it ideal for families and large groups.

2. Baytoti

A well-loved Chinese chain across Saudi Arabia, Baytoti has a branch in Jazan offering flavorful sweet-and-sour chicken, Mongolian beef, and chow mein. It’s a reliable choice for quick, tasty meals.

3. China Town Jazan

China Town brings authentic flavors with its dumplings, fried rice, and soups. The cozy atmosphere makes it perfect for casual dining with friends or colleagues.

4. Golden Wok

Golden Wok is known for its wok-fried noodles and seafood specialties. The combination of generous portions and fair pricing makes it a favorite among locals.

5. Red Lotus Restaurant

Red Lotus blends traditional Chinese recipes with a modern dining experience. Signature dishes include kung pao chicken, wonton soup, and spicy shrimp.

6. P.F. Chang’s (Jazan)

For a premium Chinese dining experience, P.F. Chang’s is an excellent choice. Famous for its dynamite shrimp and Mongolian beef, it’s ideal for celebrations and special occasions.

7. Lucky Panda Express

A budget-friendly eatery, Lucky Panda Express offers quick meals such as fried noodles, spring rolls, and dumplings. Popular with students and young professionals, it’s a convenient spot for fast yet delicious food.

8. Mandarin Restaurant

Mandarin Restaurant focuses on authentic Chinese cuisine with a wide menu including beef stir-fry, sweet corn soup, and crispy duck. Its elegant setup makes it perfect for families.

9. Asian Corner

Asian Corner serves a mix of Asian cuisines but shines with its Chinese options like hot soups, stir-fried rice, and chili chicken. It’s a great place for variety seekers.

10. China Garden

China Garden is a stylish dining spot with an extensive menu, offering everything from dim sum to seafood platters. It’s especially popular for group gatherings and business dinners.

Quick Comparison Table Of Best Chinese Restaurants in Jazan

Final Words

Jazan is more than just a cultural and coastal hub — it’s also a rising destination for international cuisine. From affordable eateries like Lucky Panda Express to premium dining experiences at P.F. Chang’s, the city caters to every kind of food lover. If you’re craving authentic Chinese flavors, these 10 best Chinese restaurants in Jazan are definitely worth adding to your food bucket list.
