10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Jubail You Must Try

Updated September 11, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jubail, a bustling industrial city in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia, is not just a center for business—it’s also developing a vibrant food scene. Among the many international cuisines available, Chinese food has become a popular choice for locals and visitors alike. From hearty stir-fries to delicate dim sum, Jubail offers a wide range of Chinese dining experiences. In this guide, we highlight the 10 best Chinese restaurants in Jubail where you can enjoy authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and welcoming atmospheres.

What Makes a Great Chinese Restaurant in Jubail

When selecting a Chinese restaurant, consider the following key aspects:

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants in Jubail

1. Dragon City Chinese Restaurant

Dragon City is one of the most popular Chinese restaurants in Jubail. Its menu includes fried rice, sweet-and-sour chicken, seafood stir-fries, and dumplings. The modern interior and friendly service make it perfect for family meals or casual dining.

2. Baytoti

Baytoti is a well-known chain across Saudi Arabia, offering reliable and consistent Chinese dishes. Favorites include chow mein, fried rice, and vegetable stir-fries. Its casual environment makes it ideal for quick lunches or relaxed dinners.

3. China Garden

China Garden combines elegant décor with a broad selection of Chinese dishes. Diners enjoy dim sum, hot-and-sour soup, and stir-fried seafood. The restaurant provides a family-friendly and comfortable atmosphere, making it perfect for gatherings.

4. P.F. Chang’s

P.F. Chang’s in Jubail offers a premium Chinese dining experience. Stylish interiors, attentive staff, and beautifully presented dishes like Mongolian beef and Kung Pao shrimp make it suitable for special occasions, date nights, or business dinners.

5. Hua Ting Chinese Restaurant

Hua Ting specializes in Szechuan and Cantonese cuisine. Popular dishes include spicy chicken, mapo tofu, and stir-fried noodles. The vibrant and casual environment makes it ideal for diners seeking bold flavors.

6. Lucky Panda

Lucky Panda offers quick, affordable, and flavorful Chinese meals. From fried rice and chow mein to noodle bowls, it is a favorite for students, office workers, and casual diners looking for a tasty meal in Jubail.

7. Red Dragon

Red Dragon is known for its generous portions and wide variety of dishes. Highlights include seafood platters, sweet-and-sour chicken, and stir-fried vegetables. Its cozy and comfortable setting makes it perfect for family meals and group outings.

8. Sichuan Delight

Sichuan Delight focuses on authentic Szechuan cuisine with bold and spicy flavors. Casual and cozy, it is perfect for diners who enjoy traditional Chinese spices and aromatic dishes.

9. Golden Wok

Golden Wok serves fresh, flavorful Chinese dishes including dumplings, fried rice, and stir-fried vegetables. Its modern yet casual ambiance appeals to families and small groups seeking a relaxed dining experience.

10. Noodle House

Noodle House specializes in noodle-based dishes, soups, and stir-fries. Affordable and casual, it is ideal for a quick, satisfying Chinese meal. The simple décor and efficient service make it perfect for both dine-in and takeout.

Comparison Table Of Best Chinese Restaurants in Jubail

Final Words

Jubail’s Chinese restaurants offer a diverse culinary experience, from upscale dining at P.F. Chang’s to casual favorites like Lucky Panda or Noodle House. Whether you’re craving spicy Szechuan dishes, fresh dim sum, or classic fried rice, these 10 restaurants have something for everyone. Explore them all and discover your favorite Chinese flavors in Jubail!
