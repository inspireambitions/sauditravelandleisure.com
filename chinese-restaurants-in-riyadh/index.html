Top 10 Best Chinese Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh has a diverse dining scene. Chinese cuisine is very popular among locals and visitors.

From dim sum and hot pots to Szechuan dishes, Riyadh offers both casual and fine dining options for Chinese food lovers.

Here are the 10 top Chinese restaurants in Riyadh to try in 2025

1. Hualan

Hualan is a premium Chinese restaurant offering modern Chinese cuisine. Guests enjoy Peking duck, dim sum, and seafood dishes. The elegant décor and quiet atmosphere make it ideal for romantic dinners and business meals.

2. Mirage Chinese Restaurant

Mirage is known for its authentic Chinese flavors. Signature dishes include hot pots, dumplings, and Szechuan chicken. The restaurant has a family-friendly environment and traditional Chinese interior designs.

3. China Garden

China Garden serves a wide variety of Chinese dishes including seafood, noodles, and stir-fried dishes. The casual setting is perfect for families and groups. The restaurant is praised for its authentic taste and portion sizes.

Riyadh’s food scene is diverse. From aromatic curries at the best Indian restaurants in Riyadh to hand-crafted noodles in Chinese kitchens, the city has it all

4. P.F. Chang’s Riyadh

P.F. Chang’s is an international Chinese-American chain. It is famous for lettuce wraps, fried rice, and Kung Pao chicken. The modern ambiance and professional service make it suitable for business lunches and casual dining.

5. Zen China

Zen China offers contemporary Chinese cuisine with a focus on presentation and flavors. Guests enjoy dim sum, soups, and seafood dishes. It is a great spot for fine dining and special occasions.

6. Dragon Palace

Dragon Palace specializes in traditional Chinese dishes. Its menu includes sweet and sour chicken, dumplings, and Szechuan beef. The casual dining environment is suitable for families and group gatherings.

7. Red Dragon

Red Dragon is popular for its hot pots and Szechuan cuisine. Guests enjoy spicy dishes, noodles, and seafood. The stylish décor and vibrant atmosphere make it ideal for group dinners and celebrations.

8. Golden Dragon

Golden Dragon offers authentic Cantonese and Szechuan dishes. Signature dishes include roasted duck, seafood, and dim sum. The restaurant is casual yet elegant, perfect for both family meals and business lunches.

9. Dragon City

Dragon City is known for its traditional Chinese flavors. Guests enjoy stir-fried vegetables, noodles, and dumplings. It is a family-friendly restaurant with a cozy atmosphere.

10. Panda Express Riyadh

Panda Express is a casual Chinese-American chain. It offers quick meals like orange chicken, chow mein, and fried rice. The restaurant is perfect for casual dining and quick takeaways.

If you feel like switching flavors, you can also try the top Italian restaurants in Riyadh, famous for pizza, pasta, and fine dining

FAQs About Chinese Restaurants in Riyadh

Are Chinese restaurants in Riyadh expensive?

Prices vary. Fine dining options like Hualan are more expensive, while casual restaurants like Dragon City are affordable.

Can I find authentic Chinese flavors?

Yes. Most restaurants, including Mirage and China Garden, offer authentic Chinese cuisine.

Are there family-friendly Chinese restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes. Dragon Palace, China Garden, and Dragon City are ideal for families.

Do restaurants offer delivery or takeaway?

Many, including Panda Express and Zen China, provide delivery and takeaway options.

Are Chinese restaurants safe for tourists?

Yes. Hygiene standards in Riyadh are high, making them safe for all visitors.

Final Words

Riyadh offers a wide range of Chinese dining experiences. From fine dining at Hualan and Zen China to casual options like Dragon City and Panda Express, there is something for everyone.

These 10 Chinese restaurants in Riyadh are perfect for families, friends, and visitors craving authentic Chinese flavors.
