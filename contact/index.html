Email the editorial team

The fastest way to reach us is info@sauditravelandleisure.com. Use this address for corrections, destination suggestions, collaborations, and questions about a guide.

What to include

  • The page URL if you are reporting an update or correction.
  • Your suggested fix and, when possible, the source or first-hand context behind it.
  • A clear note if your message is editorial, partnership-related, or about a factual correction.

Editorial response

We prioritise factual corrections and updates to high-intent Saudi planning guides. If your note changes what a traveller should do in practice, we will move it up the queue.
