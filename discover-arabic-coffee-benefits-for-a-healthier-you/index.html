The History of Arabic Coffee in Saudi Arabia

The story of Saudi Arabian coffee starts in Yemen. A respected man named Sheikh Jaber Al-Bakri found the good side of coffee beans in the eighth century. In Saudi Arabia, people used coffee as a way to stay alert and as a warm welcome for guests.
