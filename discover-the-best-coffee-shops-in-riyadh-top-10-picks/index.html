Discover the Best Coffee Shops in Riyadh: Top 10 Picks

Updated August 8, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Top 10 Best Coffee Shops in Riyadh for Coffee Lovers

Key Highlights

  • Riyadh is brimming with a mix of contemporary and traditional coffee shops, offering specialty coffee and unique experiences for every coffee enthusiast.
  • The city’s café scene features chic interiors, Instagrammable spaces, and locally roasted beans, making it a must-visit destination in Saudi Arabia.
  • From Brew92’s expert espresso to Elixir Bunn’s artisanal blends, there’s something to delight every palate.
  • Cosy settings, rooftop views, and vegan-friendly options add to Riyadh’s hospitality and charm.
  • Whether you seek a quiet spot for remote work or a trendy place to socialise, Riyadh’s coffee shops are unmatched in variety and quality.

Introduction

Riyadh is a vibrant hub where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee wafts through the air, enticing both locals and visitors alike, including those coming from Dubai. This city’s coffee culture showcases unique spaces where traditional Arabian hospitality blends seamlessly with modern café trends. Along Uthman Ibn Affan Road, various establishments serve specialty coffee sourced from artisanal roasters, providing more than just a caffeine fix. Whether you seek a cozy nook or an Instagram-worthy backdrop, Riyadh's best coffee shops promise an unforgettable experience for every coffee lover.

Discover the Top 10 Best Coffee Shops in Riyadh for Coffee Lovers

Exploring Riyadh reveals a vibrant coffee scene that delights enthusiasts with its variety and flavor. Each café embodies the essence of local culture, offering unique blends and specialty roasts that elevate the coffee experience. From artisanal brews to cozy settings, these top picks present opportunities to indulge in exquisite flavors, such as saffron-infused lattes and rich espresso delights. Whether seeking a perfect spot for relaxation or a trendy backdrop for Instagram, these coffee shops are essential destinations for every coffee lover in the city.

1. Brew92 – Specialty Roasts and Chic Ambiance

At Brew92, the allure of specialty coffee is complemented by an inviting atmosphere that captivates patrons. Nestled along Uthman Ibn Affan Road, this coffee shop excels in sourcing artisanal beans, presenting a rich palette of flavors from creamy espresso to fragrant saffron delight. The chic ambiance, adorned with modern decor, provides a perfect backdrop for social gatherings or a serene workspace. Each sip transports coffee lovers to a world where quality meets creativity, making it a must-visit spot in Riyadh.

2. Elixir Bunn Coffee Roasters – Artisanal Beans and Cosy Setting

At Elixir Bunn Coffee Roasters, the artistry of coffee-making takes center stage. Situated in the vibrant landscape of Riyadh, this charming café specializes in artisanal beans sourced from the finest growers, offering a rich espresso experience. Its cozy setting invites visitors to unwind, whether savoring a delicate saffron-infused brew or enjoying a slice of cinnamon-spiced cake. The attention to hospitality is as remarkable as the brews themselves, making it a must-visit for any coffee aficionado in Saudi Arabia.

3. Half Million – Trendy Hotspot with Instagrammable Interiors

Half Million stands out as a chic destination, perfect for capturing aesthetically pleasing moments. Located along Uthman Ibn Affan Road, this coffee shop offers an inviting ambiance with its stylish interiors featuring stunning art and design. Specialty coffee lovers can enjoy expertly crafted espressos, enriched with notes of saffron and cinnamon, while the beautifully plated pastries complement the experience. Its vibrant atmosphere encourages creativity and connection, making it a favored spot for both remote work and leisurely meet-ups among friends.

4. Urth Caffé – Organic Coffee and Delectable Pastries

A haven for health-conscious coffee enthusiasts, Urth Caffé stands out with its commitment to organic coffee sourced from sustainable farms. The aromatic blends, enriched with hints of cocoa and saffron, invite patrons to indulge in a guilt-free experience. This inviting café, nestled on Uthman Ibn Affan Road, pairs its specialty brews with an array of delectable pastries that are as visually appealing as they are delicious. The cozy atmosphere enhances the overall enjoyment, making it a beloved spot in Riyadh's coffee culture.

5. Over Under – Ideal for Remote Work and Relaxation

Nestled along Uthman Ibn Affan Road in Makkah Al Mukarramah, Over Under offers a tranquil escape for remote workers. The space boasts an inviting atmosphere, ideal for focusing on tasks while sipping on expertly brewed specialty coffee. Enjoy a diverse menu, featuring aromatic espresso drinks paired with sweet treats. The cozy seating encourages leisurely conversations, making it a perfect spot to unwind. Whether you're checking emails or catching up with a friend, Over Under exemplifies the art of coffee hospitality in Riyadh.

Conclusion

Riyadh’s vibrant coffee culture offers an enchanting experience for enthusiasts and casual sippers alike. Each establishment, from Brew92 with its specialty roasts to the cozy atmosphere of Elixir Bunn, presents a unique charm reflective of Saudi hospitality. Travelers exploring Uthman Ibn Affan Road will find their taste buds tantalized by rich espresso blends, fragrant cinnamon-infused beverages, and delightful pastries. Whether in search of a remote work nook or an Instagram-worthy backdrop, these coffee shops truly define Riyadh’s identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which coffee shops in Riyadh are best for studying or working remotely?

For those seeking a productive environment, coffee shops like Over Under offer a relaxed atmosphere perfect for remote work. Meanwhile, Brew92 provides a chic ambiance, making both ideal spots for studying or working while enjoying a great cup of coffee.

Are there any Riyadh cafes serving traditional Arabic coffee and desserts?

Yes, Riyadh boasts several cafes that specialize in traditional Arabic coffee and desserts. Places like "Cafe Zahrat Al Qasr" and "Al-Muqha" offer authentic experiences with rich Arabic brews paired with classic sweets, providing a warm atmosphere for coffee lovers to enjoy cultural delights.

What are some budget-friendly coffee shops in Riyadh popular among students?

Some budget-friendly coffee shops popular among students in Riyadh include Brew92, known for its affordable specialty brews, and Over Under, which offers a comfortable workspace. Additionally, Half Million provides great coffee at reasonable prices, perfect for studying or socializing with friends.
