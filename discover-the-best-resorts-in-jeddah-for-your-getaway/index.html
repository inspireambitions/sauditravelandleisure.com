Discover the Best Resorts in Jeddah for Your Getaway

Updated August 7, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Resorts in Jeddah: Top Picks for Your Next Getaway

Key Highlights

  • Resorts in Jeddah give you stunning views of the Red Sea and easy ways to get to clean private beaches. These places are great for relaxation and water sports.
  • Many of the top resorts sit close to the Jeddah city center, so you have no trouble getting to local spots or finding transport.
  • You will get a taste of real luxury here. The resorts offer many kinds of accommodation, like private villas and suites.
  • The best resorts come with relaxing spa options and wellness packages, so you can unwind any time you want.
  • In the heart of Obhur, beach resorts have family fun, food from around the world, and lively beach clubs during the day.
  • When you make bookings at these resorts, you can expect great amenities, top dining, and a stay you will remember.

Jeddah is a beautiful city in Saudi Arabia by the sea. People know it for its amazing lifestyle and luxury. Many come here to enjoy lovely views of the Red Sea and a calm escape. The resorts in Jeddah be like an oasis where you can relax or find some fun. Each one is made to please all kinds of people, mixing old, traditional elements with new, modern amenities. Those who want some luxury with their family or a cozy spot for a special time can both find what they like. The shores of Jeddah offer a lot of choices and memories, with stunning views of the Red Sea and a relaxing setting.

Top Resorts in Jeddah for Your Next Getaway

A range of top places to stay waits for you in Jeddah. Here, you can enjoy both luxury and relaxation right by the Red Sea. These resorts offer something for everyone. You can find spots with family-friendly amenities, places made for romance, or busy beaches full of life. Guests can enjoy great food, with choices like authentic Turkish dishes or plates of Greek meze. Many resorts give you direct access to private beaches and relaxing spa services. With a strong focus on personal service, these places make sure every guest has a good time in this beautiful Saudi Arabian city.

1. Rixos Obhur Jeddah – Family-Friendly Luxury by the Red Sea

An oasis of family-friendly luxury is ready for you at Rixos Obhur Jeddah Resort, found right on the beautiful Red Sea. This beach resort has large residential units that mix traditional elements with all the modern comforts you may want. It's a great place to stay for people of any age. There is a wide selection of restaurants here. You can try authentic Turkish dishes or choose from international cuisine, so there is something for everyone.

Guests can enjoy many amenities, like a day beach club, a spa, and different activities to have fun or just relax. In this intimate setting, you will find both relaxation and adventure at the beach resort on the Red Sea.

2. Jeddah Hilton Hotel – Iconic Beachfront Retreat with All-Inclusive Packages

Known for its amazing views of the Red Sea, this beachfront hotel gives you a nice mix of luxury and relaxation. You can enjoy a range of international cuisine, which makes for a very good dining experience. The all-inclusive packages fit what many people need, so this place is great for families or for those looking to have a romantic trip. Some of the top amenities here include a spa where you can relax, plus direct beach access. The Jeddah Hilton Hotel really feels like an oasis in the heart of Obhur, giving you peace and all these great options right by the Red Sea.

3. Narcissus Resort & Spa Obhur – Romantic Escapes and Private Villas

Located along Jeddah’s Obhur Bay, this oasis gives you luxurious villas with beautiful views of the Red Sea. Each villa is perfect for couples who want a quiet and romantic place to stay. The private villas mix modern amenities with traditional elements. This gives you an intimate setting to relax and enjoy time together.

The resort has a spa where people can go to relax and feel good. You will find many dining choices at this place. They serve authentic Turkish dishes and also have international cuisine. It is a great spot in Jeddah for people who want a calm environment, good food, views of the Red Sea, and a touch of local style in every villa.

4. Mövenpick Resort Al Nawras – Popular Choice for Couples and Families

Nestled along Jeddah's beautiful coastline, Mövenpick Resort Al Nawras is the place to go for couples and families. This well-known beach resort gives you luxury rooms where you can enjoy amazing views of the Red Sea. It puts you close, within a few km, to many good amenities and fun things to do, giving you more reasons to stay in.

When it comes to food, you have a great dining experience here with a buffet concept. You can try authentic Turkish dishes as well as other international cuisine. This place has a very friendly lifestyle concept and family-friendly services. It offers an intimate setting where you can relax and have a good time. Whether you come with family or someone special, you will get a chance to make good memories and enjoy the oasis of relaxation that this luxury resort brings.

5. Makarem Annakheel Village – Best for Business and Leisure Travellers

Makarem Annakheel Village is a great place to stay in Saudi Arabia. It is close to the center of Jeddah and feels like an oasis. The accommodation is big and gives you space to relax after a long day. The village has good amenities for both work and relaxation. You can enjoy the day beach club, which is a good spot to unwind. There are also nice lounges for people who want to take it easy.

There is a wide selection of restaurants here. You can try authentic Turkish dishes as well as other international cuisine. This makes the dining experience enjoyable for everyone. No matter what type of food you like, you are sure to find something that fits your taste. This is the place to be for people who want comfort, relaxation, and good food in Jeddah.

What Sets Jeddah’s Resorts Apart?

Jeddah has many resorts that are well known for their top-notch amenities. You can enjoy beautiful pools, private beaches, and relaxing spas at these places. The resorts also offer fun things to do. You will find exciting water sports, many types of food to try, and some cultural activities as well. On top of that, these jeddah resorts are close to the city's main sights, which makes your stay even better.

Range of Facilities: Pools, Private Beaches, and Spas

There are many amenities to enjoy at resorts in Jeddah. These places make sure you feel relaxed during your stay. You will find beautiful pools, and some give you stunning views of the Red Sea. Some also have private beaches, so you can have your own calm and peaceful space. Many spas are there, too. They bring together traditional elements and new ways to help you rest and feel good. You can relax by the pool or choose to get a massage. Every little thing at these resorts is made to give you an oasis of comfort. This is so you get the most out of your relaxation and enjoy time in Jeddah with everything you need.

Unique Experiences: Water Sports, Dining, and Cultural Activities

Diving into the exciting experiences at resorts in Jeddah shows there is a lot to do. The Red Sea is perfect for water sports fans. You can go kite surfing, join in on snorkeling, and have fun out in the warm water. There are many places to eat, offering both international cuisine and authentic Turkish dishes. You can also enjoy plates of Greek meze. You get the chance to try many different flavors.

You will also find activities with traditional elements. These let you see the legacy of this historic trade route. Every time you visit, you get something new to remember. Your stay at one of these resorts in Jeddah will be full of good times and great memories.

Accessibility & Proximity to Jeddah’s Attractions

Many resorts in Jeddah are set up in great spots. They are close to the Jeddah city center, so guests can get to big places fast, including the Floating Mosque. This makes the whole stay better. You can drive for just a short time to reach vibrant markets, old sites, and beautiful waterfronts. If you stay close to the heart of Obhur, you will find it easy to try water sports or join in cultural activities without much bother.

These resorts give you the best of both worlds. You can relax and have some adventure too. They have top-notch amenities and let you find the special spots that locals know. This way, you will feel your trip is complete and full of good moments.

Conclusion

Experiencing the many luxurious resorts in Jeddah gives you a special way to enjoy the beauty of Saudi Arabia. Each place has its own unique amenities and the food there is great. You can try authentic Turkish dishes or enjoy other international cuisine. If you want relaxation by the Red Sea or want to do water sports, these beach resorts have something for everyone. The city of Jeddah, with its lively culture and the stunning landscapes around, makes you feel like you are in an oasis. This is a great spot for travel, whether it is for fun or for work. Book your stay to enjoy some time off in style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in an all-inclusive resort package in Jeddah?

An all-inclusive resort package in Jeddah usually covers your room, meals, and drinks. You also get to use the amenities, such as pools and private beaches. Many resorts in Jeddah give you fun things to do, such as shows, water sports, and spa services. This way, guests have a smooth and relaxing stay.

Which Jeddah resorts are best for couples seeking a romantic getaway?

For couples who want a romantic break in Jeddah, you can choose a place like Narcissus Resort & Spa Obhur. This resort gives you private villas, quiet rooms, and spa treatments that help you relax. Another good choice is the Mövenpick Resort Al Nawras. Here, you can enjoy luxury, beautiful views, and meals in a calm setting. These options provide the best blend of luxury and spa time in al Jeddah.

Do any resorts in Jeddah offer spa or wellness packages?

Many resorts in Jeddah offer spa and wellness packages. These are there for people who want some relaxation and to feel better. Some places, such as Narcissus Resort & Spa Obhur, have fancy treatments for you. Other places give a full wellness story with great views. This helps make sure that everyone who visits can have a good and memorable time.

What are the best places to visit in Jeddah?

Jeddah boasts stunning attractions like the historic Al-Balad district, the iconic King Fahd Fountain, and beautiful beaches such as Corniche. Visitor favorites also include the Red Sea Mall and the spectacular Jeddah Sculpture Museum. Each destination offers unique experiences, making Jeddah a perfect getaway for all travelers.
