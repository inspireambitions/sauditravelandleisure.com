Discover the Top 10 Must-Try Best Restaurants in Jeddah Right Now

Jeddah has a lot of places to eat, and there are always new ones and old favorites for people to try. People in Saudi Arabia come here for the wide range of food the city offers. You can find everything, from real Arabic and Indian cuisine to great Italian dishes. The places to eat also have different moods—some are simple, and some are fancy. To make a reservation at the most popular restaurants in Al Khobar, many venues now accept online bookings through their official websites or popular reservation platforms. It’s a good idea to check the restaurant’s website or call ahead to confirm if online reservations are available, as this ensures you secure your preferred dining time.

If you want to know about the top restaurants for Middle Eastern, Indian, or Italian cuisine, then this city has a lot to offer. Jeddah gives you special places and tasty dishes you will not forget. This guide will show you what to try and what makes each spot stand out, so your time eating in Jeddah is the best it can be.

1. Al Khodariyah — A Culinary Icon for Saudi and Middle Eastern Delicacies

When you walk into Al Khodariyah, you get to feel the rich history of Saudi Arabia and the best of Middle Eastern cuisine. This place is well-known because it brings real and great tastes to every dish. You will find lots of delicious foods on the menu, like juicy kababs, tasty rice meals, and colorful mezze platters. Each dish is made with the best local ingredients and with cooking ways that have been around for years.

The thing that makes Al Khodariyah special is how much they care about good food and top service. The place feels warm and welcoming but still has a nice, classy touch. It's a good spot whether you come with family or for a big moment. The look and feel of the restaurant blend today's comfort with hints of Middle Eastern style, so every meal feels more special.

If you want to enjoy real regional cuisine, you should try signature plates like Kabab Khashkhash and Oriental Hot Mezzah. Also, you need to check out the famous dessert buffet. It is the perfect way to finish your visit and makes your time there even better.

2. Shang Palace — Exquisite Chinese Fine Dining with Vibrant Ambience

Shang Palace is one of the best places for Chinese fine dining in Saudi Arabia. It is well-known for great cuisine and a fun, bright vibe. When you walk in, you feel the place is both stylish and warm. Every meal looks better with the elegant décor and good lighting.

The menu is full of Chinese food from old favorites to new dishes. You can try all kinds of dim sum, stir-fries, and warm noodle bowls. Each dish is made with care and skill. They often have well-known choices like Peking duck and seafood that is cooked with lots of taste. These show how much the kitchen cares about real Chinese food and high quality.

People think highly of Shang Palace because of the great service. This makes it a top spot for any special time. You can plan both small dinners and big parties here. The fine dining and happy energy make every visit feel special. If you want to enjoy top Chinese cuisine in Saudi Arabia, this is a very good place to go.

3. Il Gabbiano — Authentic Italian Flavours on Jeddah’s Waterfront

Il Gabbiano brings real Italian food to Saudi Arabia. You will find a true taste of Italy here, with wood-fired pizza, creamy risotto, and fresh pasta made with the best ingredients.

The big draw of Il Gabbiano is where it is. The restaurant sits by the waterfront, so you can enjoy great sea views while you eat. The mix of simple and stylish decor adds to the charm. It is a great place for special times with someone or for parties.

There are a few dishes you really should try. Go for the Margherita pizza, tasty Bolognese pasta, or the lively arugula salad. The staff is helpful, and the place has a warm feel that turns each meal into something special. With its mix of good cuisine and lovely views, Il Gabbiano is one of the best spots in Saudi Arabia for Italian food.

4. Toki — Contemporary Asian Cuisine in a Chic Setting

Toki is known for its modern Asian food and stylish look in Jeddah. When you walk in, you will see simple, clean lines and a cool design that go well with the food served. Toki’s menu lets you try many Asian dishes. You can choose from fresh sushi and sashimi, special fusion plates, and colorful salads.

People talk a lot about the warm feeling inside the restaurant. That is why many come here to celebrate, or just to have a nice and classy night out. The big windows let you enjoy beautiful city views, and the staff helps to make your meal smooth and easy.

Some of the top dishes here are the creative sushi rolls, tasty tempura, and rich main dishes made with Asian ideas. If you like true Japanese food or want to try something bold and new, Toki brings a real taste of Asia to the center of Saudi Arabia. If you love good cuisine in Saudi Arabia, you will find something to enjoy at Toki.

5. Shababik — Lebanese Specialities and Family-Friendly Atmosphere

Shababik is the place people go in Jeddah when they want to eat real Lebanese cuisine. The menu has many dishes from the region, like tasty mezze, grilled meats, fresh salads, and classic desserts. Every meal be cooked with care. The chefs use old recipes and the best ingredients to make sure you get the true flavors of Lebanon.

The place is known for being good for families. There is plenty of room to sit, warm lighting, and friendly staff. All this helps to make a space where people feel at home. It is a great place for groups to come together. You can stop by for a simple lunch, or you can also bring everyone for a happy and big dinner.

People talk a lot about the kababs, fresh hummus, and sweet kunafa. The way Shababik serves authentic cuisine, along with its cozy feel, makes it a top pick for people living in the city and for those just visiting.

6. Byblos — Fine Lebanese Dining with an Elegant Twist

Byblos is one of Jeddah’s favorite spots for those who love authentic Lebanese flavors but in a fine dining setting. The restaurant combines the rich traditions of Lebanon with modern culinary presentation, making each dish both flavorful and visually appealing.

The menu highlights classic mezze, perfectly grilled meats, and seafood specialties. Every dish is seasoned with fresh herbs and spices, bringing out the essence of Middle Eastern cuisine.

What makes Byblos stand out is its chic, elegant interior and warm atmosphere, perfect for family gatherings or business dinners. Signature dishes like the Mixed Grill Platter, Fattoush salad, and creamy Baba Ghanoush are must-tries. With attentive service and high-quality food, Byblos delivers a memorable dining experience.

7. Albaik — Jeddah’s Iconic Fast-Food Favorite

No list of Jeddah restaurants is complete without mentioning Albaik, the city’s most famous fast-food chain. Beloved by locals and tourists alike, Albaik is known for its crispy fried chicken, garlic sauce, and quick service.

The atmosphere here is casual and bustling, with long queues that show just how popular this spot is. Whether you’re grabbing a quick lunch or a late-night bite, Albaik always delivers consistency and flavor.

If you’re in Jeddah for the first time, Albaik is a must-visit. The Spicy Chicken, Shrimp Meal, and their signature Garlic Sauce are iconic dishes that truly define fast food in Saudi Arabia.

8. Al-Nakheel Restaurant — Traditional Arabian Dining with a View

Al-Nakheel Restaurant offers a unique experience with its outdoor terrace overlooking the Red Sea. It is one of the city’s most atmospheric dining spots, combining beautiful scenery with authentic Arabian cuisine.

The menu focuses on Middle Eastern and Gulf dishes, including lamb mandi, grilled seafood, and mezze platters. The highlight here is enjoying a meal under the open sky, with the sea breeze adding to the ambiance.

Families and groups especially enjoy Al-Nakheel for its spacious seating and traditional style. If you’re looking for a mix of culture, scenery, and delicious food, this is one of the best places to go in Jeddah.

9. Nozomi — Upscale Japanese Dining in a Modern Setting

For a trendy and upscale dining experience, Nozomi is one of Jeddah’s top Japanese restaurants. The atmosphere is sleek and modern, with dim lighting and stylish décor that makes it ideal for a sophisticated night out.

The menu focuses on contemporary Japanese cuisine, including sushi, sashimi, robata-grilled meats, and signature fusion dishes. Every plate is beautifully presented, highlighting both flavor and artistry.

Signature items include the Black Cod, Wagyu Beef, and creative sushi rolls. Nozomi is particularly popular among food enthusiasts who want more than just a meal—it’s a full fine-dining experience.

10. The Globe Roundabout Restaurant — International Cuisine with Iconic Views

Located inside the famous Globe Roundabout landmark, this restaurant offers both international and Saudi-inspired cuisine in one of the most unique dining settings in Jeddah. The panoramic views of the city make it a favorite spot for tourists and locals celebrating special occasions.

The menu blends global flavors, offering everything from grilled meats and pasta to regional specialties. Each dish is prepared with a fine balance of tradition and modern cooking styles.

The highlight here is undoubtedly the view—dining inside one of Jeddah’s architectural icons makes the experience unforgettable. Whether you go for a romantic dinner or a family outing, the Globe Roundabout Restaurant offers an extraordinary setting with delicious food to match.

Conclusion

Jeddah has a wide range of foods and places to try. You can find old favorites at Al Khodariyah or enjoy the view by the water at Il Gabbiano. Each spot on this list brings something special to the table. You will find good meals for big events or for a simple treat. These are the places you should visit to make your meal feel really special. Don’t miss your chance to try out some great flavors in the city. Book your table now and get ready for a meal you will remember.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the must-try dishes at these top restaurants in Jeddah?

Signature dishes at these places show off their own kind of cuisine. At Al Khodariyah, you can get Kabab Khashkhash and Oriental Hot Mezzah. Il Gabbiano is known for its wood-fired Margherita pizza. Shang Palace serves Peking duck. Toki has creative sushi rolls. If you go to Shababik, try the classic Lebanese mezze and kunafa. Each restaurant has something special that stands out from the rest.

Which restaurants in Jeddah are best for special occasions or romantic dinners?

Il Gabbiano sits by the water, and Shang Palace has an elegant look. Both of these places are great if you want a romantic dinner or if you plan a special celebration. Toki has a stylish feel, and Al Khodariyah brings a fine dining experience. These are all wonderful places in Saudi Arabia to enjoy good cuisine and a nice atmosphere for those moments you want to remember.

Are there any affordable or vegetarian-friendly options among Jeddah’s best restaurants?

Yes, the top places to eat in Jeddah like Shababik and Toki also have tasty options for people who eat vegetarian food. You will find some good prices too. Many places the families go offer many meals to fit all budgets and tastes. This way, everyone can get what they need or want to eat.

Best restaurants in Jeddah? (on a budget)

For budget-friendly dining in Jeddah, consider Al Baik for delicious fried chicken, or try Taza for flavorful grilled dishes. Another great option is the local favorite, Shawarma Factory, offering tasty wraps at reasonable prices. These eateries provide a delightful experience without straining your wallet while exploring the best restaurants in Jeddah.
