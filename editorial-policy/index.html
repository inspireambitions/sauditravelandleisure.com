Our scope

Saudi Travel and Leisure is now intentionally Saudi-first. We keep some legacy Gulf guides available for reference, but the primary indexed experience is focused on Saudi Arabia destinations, travel basics, transport, food, and cultural planning.

How guides are updated

We prioritise pages that affect real decisions: visas, airport transfers, transport, dress expectations, safety context, where to stay, and first-time planning. When a page no longer reflects the clearest version of a topic, we consolidate it rather than letting duplicate variants compete in search.

Sources and confidence

We prefer practical, decision-ready guidance over filler. That means clearer structure, fewer duplicate pages, stronger internal linking, and corrections when transport rules, destination access, or planning details change.

Corrections and archive handling

If a guide is no longer part of the primary Saudi focus, we keep it accessible as an archive reference and mark it accordingly. If a stronger page replaces a duplicate intent, we move that weaker page out of the index. Corrections can be sent to info@sauditravelandleisure.com.
