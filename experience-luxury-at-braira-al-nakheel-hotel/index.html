Discover Luxury at Braira Al Nakheel Hotel, Riyadh

Key Highlights

  • Braira Al Nakheel in Riyadh offers an impressive five-star luxury experience with elegant accommodation and modern amenities.
  • Positioned just a seven-minute stroll from Al Nakheel Mall, guests enjoy one of the largest shopping centres right on their doorstep.
  • The hotel features a beautiful indoor swimming pool, a well-equipped fitness centre, and a relaxing wellness centre for leisure and rejuvenation.
  • Delectable dining options are available, including a main restaurant, lounge bar, and coffee shop.
  • Free fast Wi-Fi and private parking ensure absolute convenience.
  • The location provides easy access to King Khalid International Airport, Riyadh train station, and major attractions such as World Sights Park Riyadh.

Introduction

If you are searching for fascinating accommodation in Riyadh, Braira Al Nakheel Hotel is a standout choice. Set opposite the renowned Al Nakheel Mall, this luxury destination captures the essence of comfort and style in the Saudi capital. Whether you are visiting for a business trip or a leisurely escape, the hotel blends modern design with top-tier service. Here, you discover a harmonious mix of contemporary amenities and authentic hospitality, making every stay at Braira Al Nakheel Hotel memorable from the very first moment.

Elegant Room Types and Accommodation Options

Every traveller finds a suitable space at Braira Al Nakheel Hotel, thanks to its variety of elegant room types. You can select from rooms with balconies, family-friendly layouts, or those with spacious dining areas and city views. While extra bed capacity is unavailable, each room comfortably accommodates adults and children, making it a fitting choice for families and business guests alike.

Rooms are thoughtfully equipped with essentials such as a fast free Wi-Fi connection, flat-screen satellite TV, minibar, and a convenient work desk. These details make a significant difference for business travellers needing a productive environment or anyone who values connectivity.

Compared to other hotels in Riyadh, Braira Al Nakheel offers competitive rates for its quality and location. The diverse accommodation options mean you can choose a space that suits your budget, without compromising on comfort or amenities. The lack of extra beds or cribs is balanced by roomy layouts and flexible arrangements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dining options and breakfast choices are available on-site?

Guests can look forward to a daily breakfast buffet at the main dining restaurant, featuring a variety of delicious international dishes. During Ramadan, both sahour and breakfast options are available. A cosy coffee shop is also on-site, serving beverages and light snacks throughout the day.

How do check-in, check-out, and cancellation policies work at Braira Al Nakheel Hotel?

Check-in is from 16:00 until 23:59, and check-out runs between 12:00 and 13:00. Cancellation and damage deposit policies vary depending on booking type, with certain rates requiring a credit card or cash deposit. Always review your reservation for specific terms.

What transport options and nearby attractions can guests enjoy?

Braira Al Nakheel Hotel is conveniently located near King Khalid International Airport and Riyadh train station. Guests can explore Al Nakheel Mall just steps away, or visit World Sights Park Riyadh, Alhokair Land, and Riyadh International Exhibition Center—all within a short drive.
