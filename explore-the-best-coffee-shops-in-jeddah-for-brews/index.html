Explore the Best Coffee Shops in Jeddah for Brews

Updated August 8, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Coffee Shops in Jeddah: A Guide to the Finest Brews

Key Highlights

  • Jeddah’s café culture is flourishing, blending Saudi Arabia’s love of coffee with international flair and design.
  • The best cafes in Jeddah cater to all tastes, from expertly brewed espresso to fresh pastries and gourmet brunch options.
  • Specialty roasters like Brew92 and Camel Step are essential stops for coffee enthusiasts seeking quality and innovation.
  • Many cafés in Jeddah offer unique atmospheres, whether you’re after trendy interiors, minimalist spaces, or family-friendly settings.
  • Excellent workspaces and late-night coffee spots mean you’ll always find a perfect place in Jeddah to relax or get productive.

Introduction

Jeddah, the vibrant coastal city in Saudi Arabia, is rapidly becoming a haven for café lovers seeking something special. With a flourishing café scene, the city merges tradition and modernity, offering everything from locally roasted espresso to exceptional pastries in settings that range from chic to homely. Whether you’re a resident or a visitor, you’ll quickly realise that a good café is never far away in Jeddah—each one reflecting the city’s creative energy and cosmopolitan spirit.

Discover the Finest Coffee Shops in Jeddah for Exceptional Brews

Searching for the best cafes in Jeddah? You’re spoilt for choice, as the city’s specialty coffee shops have gained a reputation for top-quality espresso, warm hospitality, and stylish ambiences. Whether you’re looking for a quiet corner for study or work, or a lively spot to meet friends, Jeddah’s café culture delivers.

Are you curious about unique or trendy coffee shops with an inviting atmosphere? From industrial-chic roasteries to minimalist hideaways, Jeddah offers a diverse selection of cafes that promise an unforgettable experience for every coffee lover. Let’s explore the standout venues across the city.

1. Brew92 – The Artisan Coffee Pioneer

Brew92 stands out as one of the best cafes in Jeddah and is widely celebrated among locals and visitors alike. This Saudi-born café and roastery is famous for its industrial yet welcoming vibe, featuring exposed brick walls, a custom coffee bar, and expert baristas passionate about every cup. Regulars flock here for V60 pour-overs and espresso-based drinks, which consistently impress with their depth of flavour.

What keeps Brew92 at the top of recent reviews is not just the coffee but its commitment to quality. The beans are roasted in-house, ensuring freshness and a robust taste profile in every sip. As a bonus, their menu includes mouthwatering pastries and light bites that pair perfectly with your drink.

If you’re seeking high-quality brews in Jeddah or want to experience the city’s artisan coffee scene, Brew92 is a must-visit. This café sets the standard for specialty coffee in Saudi Arabia.

2. Camel Step Café – Home-Grown Roastery with a Trendy Vibe

Camel Step Café is a home-grown Saudi Arabia brand that brings a fresh and modern approach to the café scene. Its welcoming atmosphere and trendy design attract a diverse crowd, from coffee enthusiasts to those seeking a relaxed vibe to unwind or catch up with friends. At Camel Step, every detail is thoughtfully curated—from the sourcing of the finest beans to the variety of brewing methods available.

Visitors can enjoy everything from espresso and pour-over to cold brew, each crafted for maximum flavour. The menu also includes delicious pastries and light bites, making it a favourite for both quick stops and leisurely visits. Some branches offer outdoor seating, allowing guests to enjoy the Jeddah weather.

If you’re after a distinct café experience in Jeddah, Camel Step delivers with its passion for coffee and lively yet approachable setting. It’s definitely one of the city’s must-visit independent cafés.

3. Urth Caffe – Brunch, Organic Coffee & Family-Friendly Ambience

Urth Caffe brings a slice of Californian cool to Jeddah, making it a standout choice for brunch lovers and families alike. The café’s menu is packed with wellness-inspired dishes, from Avocado Crush toast to Stuffed Banana & Nutella French Toast, all crafted with organic ingredients and presented beautifully. Its pastries add a tempting sweet touch to any visit.

Families are drawn to Urth Caffe for its spacious, nature-inspired interiors filled with greenery and natural light, creating a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. The baristas are dedicated to quality, offering everything from single-origin roasts to signature blends brewed to perfection.

For those searching for a child-friendly or family-oriented café in Jeddah, Urth Caffe checks every box. It’s a fantastic spot not only for coffee and brunch but also for a memorable outing with loved ones.

4. Myra Café – Minimalist Design Meets Specialty Coffee

Myra Café perfectly blends minimalist design with a dedication to specialty coffee in Al Jeddah, making it one of the most Instagrammable cafes in Jeddah. Guests are greeted with sleek, contemporary interiors and a calming colour palette, providing a tranquil backdrop for enjoying artisanal drinks and French-inspired pastries.

Led by acclaimed Pastry Chef Jonas Plangger, Myra offers a daily selection of cakes and viennoiseries, combining classic techniques with creative twists. The attention to quality extends to the drinks menu, from expertly brewed espresso to cold-pressed juices.

Whether you love capturing beautiful spaces or simply appreciate excellent coffee, Myra Café is a must-see spot in Jeddah. Its combination of visual appeal and speciality offerings makes it a favourite among both locals and visitors seeking a refined café experience.

5. Cup & Couch – Cosy Workspace and Instagrammable Interiors

Cup & Couch is the answer for anyone needing a comfortable workspace with a creative flair. This café is known for its inviting, Instagram-worthy interiors that blend style with utility, making it a popular choice for remote workers and students in Jeddah. Soft lighting, plush seating, and thoughtfully arranged tables create an atmosphere that’s as productive as it is cosy.

The menu at Cup & Couch features a well-curated selection of coffee options and light bites, ensuring you stay fuelled for both work and inspiration. Many regulars praise its relaxed vibe, which encourages conversation just as much as focused effort.

If your priority is finding a café in Jeddah with comfortable seating and a welcoming workspace, Cup & Couch stands out. It’s the ideal place for those wanting to balance productivity with a touch of style.

6. Black Potion – Late-Night Hangout with Creative Blends

Black Potion is a go-to destination for night owls and anyone searching for creative coffee blends in Jeddah. The café’s lively terrace and energetic vibe set the tone for memorable evenings, whether you’re after a bold cold brew or an inventive signature drink. Black Potion’s menu is designed to surprise and delight, offering both classic and unique options for adventurous palates.

Open late into the night, this café welcomes those looking for flexible hours and a fun social scene. The combination of outdoor seating and a dynamic crowd makes Black Potion a favourite for weekend gatherings and spontaneous meetups.

If you’re wondering where to find coffee shops in Jeddah that stay open late or offer unique blends, Black Potion should be at the top of your list. It’s the perfect spot to enjoy creative drinks and soak up the city’s vibrant energy after dark.

7. L’eto Café – Elegant Desserts and European-Style Coffee

L’eto Café brings European sophistication to Jeddah’s café scene, becoming a favourite for those who appreciate fine desserts and pastries alongside expertly made coffee. The café’s elegant décor and polished ambience make it a lovely setting for afternoon tea or a sweet treat with friends.

Patrons rave about L’eto’s menu, which features a tempting variety of cakes, tarts, and pastries—all crafted with premium ingredients and artistic presentation. The coffee selection is equally impressive, offering everything from smooth lattes to robust espressos prepared in true European style.

Wondering which cafés in Jeddah serve the best desserts and snacks with coffee? L’eto Café stands out for its dedication to quality, making every visit a memorable indulgence for the senses. It’s a must-visit spot for anyone looking to combine coffee culture with culinary artistry.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Jeddah is a haven for coffee enthusiasts, offering a diverse array of coffee shops that cater to every taste and preference. From the artisanal brews at Brew92 to the cozy ambiance of Cup & Couch, each café presents a unique experience that combines exceptional coffee with delightful atmospheres. Whether you’re looking for a quick caffeine fix or a comfortable workspace, this vibrant city has something for everyone. Don’t miss out on exploring these local gems; they not only serve fantastic brews but also foster a sense of community. So grab your friends, explore these cafés, and elevate your coffee experience in Jeddah today!

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most unique or must-visit independent coffee shops in Jeddah?

Some of the best cafes in Jeddah, such as Brew92, Camel Step, and Myra Café, are independently owned and offer a distinct vibe. These spots are celebrated for their specialty coffee, welcoming atmosphere, and innovative menus, making them essential stops for anyone exploring the local café scene in Saudi Arabia.

Which cafés in Jeddah offer the best desserts or snacks alongside coffee?

For top-notch pastries and desserts, head to cafés like L’eto Café, Myra Café, and Urth Caffe in Jeddah. These best cafes are known for their artisanal treats that pair beautifully with a quality espresso or specialty coffee, ensuring a satisfying and memorable visit.

Where can I find coffee shops in Jeddah that are open late or suitable for working/studying?

In Jeddah, Black Potion stands out for its late-night hours and vibrant vibe, while Cup & Couch is renowned for its comfortable workspace and café environment. Both are perfect for those needing a flexible schedule or a productive setting to work or study.
