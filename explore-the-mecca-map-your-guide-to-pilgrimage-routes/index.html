Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find the Qibla direction using a map centred on Mecca?

To find which way to face for prayer, get a map that is centred on Mecca. Look at your mosque or location on the map, then draw a straight line from there to the Kaaba. The Kaaba is in Masjid al-Haram. This line will show you the Qibla. That is the way you need to turn when you pray in your mosque.
