Ultimate Guide to Airline Luggage Size, Weight Limits, and Fees in Saudi Arabia

Knowing the rules about airline luggage size, the weight limits, and the fees is important for a smooth travel experience in Saudi Arabia. Most airlines have about the same size rules for carry-on bags. Usually, the biggest you can take is 55 cm x 40 cm x 20 cm, and it cannot weigh more than 7 kg. For checked luggage, the standard weight is about 23 kg for each bag. But the rules can be different with every airline. If your travel bag is too big or too heavy, you may have to pay extra fees. So, always check your airline’s rules before you pack your bag.
