Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Abha

Updated September 2, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Abha, the capital of the Asir region in Saudi Arabia, is known for its cool climate, green landscapes, and cultural richness. Beyond its natural beauty, Abha also has a growing food scene that attracts both locals and tourists. While traditional Saudi dishes are popular, French cuisine has also found its way here. From cozy cafés serving buttery croissants to fine dining spots offering classic French recipes, Abha has something for every French food lover.

Here are the 10 best French restaurants and cafés in Abha where you can enjoy authentic flavors and a memorable dining experience.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Abha

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant Abha

Paul is a world-famous French bakery and restaurant, and its Abha branch delivers the same high-quality experience. Known for freshly baked bread, delicate pastries, and light French meals, it’s a favorite spot for breakfast and evening tea. The interiors are elegant, creating a Parisian vibe in the heart of Abha.

Why people love it: Wide variety of French breads, family-friendly atmosphere, and excellent coffee.
Must-try dishes: Quiche Lorraine, butter croissant, and chocolate éclair.

2. Brioche Dorée Abha

Brioche Dorée is a modern French café chain that offers quick yet high-quality meals. In Abha, it is popular among young professionals and families looking for a casual French dining experience. The menu includes pastries, sandwiches, and light French desserts, making it ideal for a relaxed brunch.

Why people love it: Affordable prices, quick service, and authentic French pastries.
Must-try dishes: Almond croissant, chicken baguette, and café latte.

3. Le Croissant Abha

Le Croissant Café is a local favorite for those craving fresh croissants, baguettes, and desserts. The café is cozy and welcoming, perfect for a quiet morning breakfast or an afternoon coffee. Their variety of croissant fillings, both sweet and savory, makes it stand out from other cafés.

Why people love it: Freshly baked croissants daily, cozy seating, and family-friendly menu.
Must-try dishes: Cheese croissant, pistachio croissant, and cappuccino.

4. La Madeleine Café

La Madeleine is inspired by French country-style cooking. In Abha, it offers a warm and inviting space where guests can enjoy hearty soups, salads, and traditional French main courses. The café is well-suited for families and travelers looking for comfort food.

Why people love it: Homestyle French meals, large portions, and warm ambiance.
Must-try dishes: French onion soup, roast chicken with herbs, and crème brÃ»lée.

5. Café de Paris Abha

Café de Paris brings a little piece of Paris to Abha with its artistic décor and French-inspired menu. It is especially popular in the evenings when locals gather for coffee and dessert. Their desserts are highly praised, and many visitors come back for their signature hot chocolate.

Why people love it: Elegant design, rich desserts, and Parisian atmosphere.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, chocolate mousse, and Parisian hot chocolate.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant

Bon Appétit offers a wide mix of French and European dishes. With its spacious interiors and family-friendly environment, it’s a great choice for large gatherings. The restaurant serves everything from soups and salads to gourmet French dishes, making it one of the most versatile French-inspired restaurants in Abha.

Why people love it: Diverse menu, family dining options, and authentic flavors.
Must-try dishes: Ratatouille, grilled salmon with French herbs, and crème caramel.

7. Maison du Croissant Abha

Maison du Croissant specializes in freshly baked croissants, pastries, and cakes. It has quickly become a go-to destination for breakfast lovers in Abha. The café is simple yet charming, making it ideal for a relaxed start to the day.

Why people love it: Variety of croissant flavors, freshly baked every morning, and great coffee.
Must-try dishes: Pistachio croissant, chocolate croissant, and macchiato.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger Abha

This French-inspired bakery is dedicated to artisan bread-making. Known for its rustic breads, sandwiches, and French-style baked goods, it is a paradise for bread lovers. The bakery also offers takeaway options, making it convenient for those who want a quick yet quality meal.

Why people love it: Artisan-style breads, unique sandwiches, and authentic French recipes.
Must-try dishes: Baguette sandwich with French cheese, sourdough bread, and quiche.

9. Café Madeleine Abha

Different from La Madeleine, Café Madeleine is a boutique café known for its delicate French desserts and premium coffee. It’s a place where people enjoy calm evenings and celebrate special occasions with cakes and tarts.

Why people love it: Elegant pastries, peaceful atmosphere, and friendly staff.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

10. Relais Parisien Abha

Relais Parisien is a fine dining option in Abha that offers authentic French steaks, sauces, and gourmet meals. It’s more upscale compared to other French spots in the city, making it perfect for special occasions and romantic dinners.

Why people love it: Elegant fine dining, professional service, and authentic French recipes.
Must-try dishes: Steak au poivre, grilled chicken with béarnaise sauce, and crème brÃ»lée.

Final Thoughts

Abha is not only known for its mountains and cultural heritage but also for its growing culinary scene. French restaurants and cafés here give visitors a chance to experience the charm of Paris while enjoying the hospitality of Saudi Arabia. Whether you are looking for a light croissant breakfast, a cozy café experience, or a luxurious French dinner, these restaurants in Abha offer the perfect combination of taste and atmosphere.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Abha

1. Are French restaurants in Abha halal?

Yes, all French restaurants and cafés in Abha serve halal-certified food, ensuring they are suitable for locals and tourists alike.

2. What is the best French café for breakfast in Abha?

Paul Bakery & Restaurant and Brioche Dorée are among the top choices for French-style breakfasts.

3. Do French restaurants in Abha serve vegetarian food?

Yes, many French cafés and restaurants in Abha offer vegetarian options such as salads, cheese platters, and vegetable quiches.

4. Which French restaurant in Abha is best for fine dining?

Relais Parisien is one of the most popular choices for fine dining, offering authentic French steaks and gourmet meals.
