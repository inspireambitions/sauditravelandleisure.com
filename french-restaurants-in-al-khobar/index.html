10 Top Best French Restaurants in Al Khobar

Updated September 2, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Khobar, a bustling city in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia, is famous for its modern lifestyle, seaside views, and diverse culinary scene. Alongside traditional Saudi cuisine, French restaurants and cafés have become increasingly popular here. From artisan pastries and fresh croissants to fine dining with classic French dishes, Al Khobar offers something for every French food enthusiast.

Here are the 10 best French restaurants and cafés in Al Khobar that offer authentic French flavors with a memorable dining experience.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Al Khobar

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant Al Khobar

Paul is a renowned French bakery chain, and its Al Khobar branch provides a true Parisian experience. Known for freshly baked breads, croissants, and light French meals, Paul is a favorite for breakfast, brunch, and afternoon coffee.

Why people love it: Elegant interiors, family-friendly seating, and consistently fresh bakery items.
Must-try dishes: Quiche Lorraine, chocolate éclair, and butter croissant.

2. Brioche Dorée Al Khobar

This modern French café is perfect for a casual breakfast or light lunch. Brioche Dorée serves freshly baked pastries, sandwiches, and coffee in a cozy atmosphere, making it a popular spot for both professionals and families.

Why people love it: Quick service, authentic French pastries, and affordable pricing.
Must-try dishes: Almond croissant, chicken baguette, and café latte.

3. Le Croissant Café

Le Croissant Café specializes in freshly baked croissants, baguettes, and French-style desserts. Its relaxed and welcoming ambiance makes it ideal for morning coffee or an afternoon snack.

Why people love it: Variety of croissant fillings, fresh bakery items daily, and comfortable seating.
Must-try dishes: Pistachio croissant, cheese croissant, and cappuccino.

4. La Madeleine Al Khobar

La Madeleine brings French country-style cooking to Al Khobar. With hearty soups, fresh salads, and traditional French main courses, it’s perfect for families and tourists seeking comforting meals.

Why people love it: Homestyle French meals, large portions, and warm hospitality.
Must-try dishes: French onion soup, roasted chicken with herbs, and crème caramel.

5. Café de Paris Al Khobar

Café de Paris offers a stylish Parisian café experience with a focus on desserts and coffee. It’s a favorite evening spot for locals and tourists who want a calm environment and indulgent sweets.

Why people love it: Elegant décor, rich French desserts, and premium coffee.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, chocolate mousse, and Parisian hot chocolate.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant

Bon Appétit combines French and European cuisines, offering a wide-ranging menu for families and groups. The restaurant’s spacious interiors and authentic French flavors make it a great place for lunch or dinner.

Why people love it: Large menu, family-friendly dining, and high-quality French recipes.
Must-try dishes: Ratatouille, grilled salmon with lemon butter sauce, and crème brÃ»lée.

7. Maison du Croissant

Maison du Croissant is a French-inspired bakery renowned for its croissants and pastries. Visitors love the fresh-baked items and relaxed café setting, making it a go-to destination for breakfast or snacks.

Why people love it: Freshly baked croissants, cozy environment, and friendly staff.
Must-try dishes: Pistachio croissant, chocolate croissant, and espresso.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger Al Khobar

Specializing in artisan breads, sandwiches, and quiches, L’Artisan Boulanger brings traditional French baking to Al Khobar. It’s ideal for those who appreciate authentic, handmade breads.

Why people love it: Authentic French baking, artisan-style breads, and high-quality ingredients.
Must-try dishes: Baguette with French cheese, quiche Lorraine, and sourdough bread.

9. Relais Parisien Al Khobar

Relais Parisien is a fine dining French restaurant offering gourmet meals, classic French steaks, and an upscale dining experience. It’s perfect for special occasions, romantic dinners, or celebrations.

Why people love it: Elegant ambiance, professional service, and authentic French gourmet dishes.
Must-try dishes: Steak au poivre, chicken with béarnaise sauce, and crème brÃ»lée.

10. Madeleine Boutique Café

Madeleine Boutique Café is a boutique-style French café serving premium coffee, pastries, and desserts. Its peaceful environment and beautifully presented dishes make it ideal for a relaxing visit or special celebration.

Why people love it: Elegant desserts, quiet atmosphere, and friendly service.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

Final Thoughts

Al Khobar offers more than just shopping and seaside views—it has a growing culinary scene, including authentic French restaurants and cafés. Whether you’re looking for a light croissant breakfast, a cozy café experience, or a luxurious French dinner, these 10 French restaurants in Al Khobar provide delicious food, memorable ambiance, and a taste of Paris in Saudi Arabia.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Al Khobar

1. Are French restaurants in Al Khobar halal?

Yes, all French restaurants and cafés in Al Khobar serve halal-certified food.

2. Which French café is best for breakfast in Al Khobar?

Paul Bakery & Restaurant and Brioche Dorée are the top choices for authentic French breakfasts.

3. Do French restaurants in Al Khobar offer vegetarian options?

Yes, many restaurants provide vegetarian-friendly dishes like salads, quiches, and cheese platters.

4. What is the best French restaurant in Al Khobar for fine dining?

Relais Parisien is considered the best upscale French dining option in Al Khobar.
