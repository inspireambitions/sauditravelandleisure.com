Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Al Ula

Updated September 3, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Ula, one of Saudi Arabia’s most breathtaking destinations, is famous for its sandstone mountains, heritage sites, and unique desert landscapes. With the rise of tourism, especially during the Winter at Tantora festival and other cultural events, the food scene in Al Ula has expanded rapidly. Among the many international dining options, French cuisine has become especially popular, offering visitors a touch of elegance and authenticity.

Here are the 10 best French restaurants and cafés in Al Ula where you can enjoy delicious pastries, gourmet meals, and a taste of Paris in the desert.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Al Ula

1. Annabel’s Al Ula

Annabel’s is a luxury French-inspired restaurant known for its sophisticated atmosphere and fine dining. It brings a stylish European dining experience to the heart of Al Ula.

Why people love it: Elegant décor, romantic ambiance, and world-class French dishes.

2. Myazu Al Ula

Although Myazu is primarily known for Japanese fusion, it offers a French-influenced fine dining experience with beautifully presented dishes and premium service.

Why people love it: Gourmet dining, modern European influence, and exceptional presentation.
Must-try dishes: Wagyu beef with French butter sauce, truffle-infused starters, and mille-feuille.

3. Circolo Al Ula

Circolo is a modern French-Mediterranean concept that combines fresh ingredients with authentic French recipes. Its stylish design makes it perfect for evening dinners.

Why people love it: Chic Parisian vibe, creative French plates, and great service.
Must-try dishes: Ratatouille, grilled salmon with beurre blanc, and chocolate soufflé.

4. Villa One Al Ula

Villa One offers a boutique-style dining experience with a focus on French fine dining. It’s often chosen for anniversaries, special dinners, and luxury experiences.

Why people love it: Private atmosphere, gourmet menu, and high-end service.
Must-try dishes: Foie gras, steak au poivre, and tarte Tatin.

5. Le Bistro by Ashar Resort

Located within one of Al Ula’s luxury resorts, this French-style bistro serves authentic dishes and pastries in a relaxed yet refined setting.

Why people love it: Scenic views, fresh ingredients, and homely French cooking.
Must-try dishes: French baguette with cheese platter, quiche Lorraine, and croissants.

7. Café de Paris Al Ula

Café de Paris is a cozy French café where you can enjoy light meals, coffee, and desserts. It’s popular with tourists exploring the old town.

Why people love it: Relaxed ambiance, Parisian-style seating, and excellent desserts.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, hot chocolate, and éclairs.

8. La Table Al Ula

La Table offers French fine dining with a touch of local Arabian influence. It’s an excellent choice for food lovers who enjoy creative fusions.

Why people love it: High-end presentation, fusion dishes, and gourmet flavors.
Must-try dishes: Coq au vin, truffle risotto, and crème caramel.

9. Bon Appétit Café Al Ula

Bon Appétit Café is a casual spot offering French pastries, sandwiches, and coffee. It’s great for a quick stop during sightseeing tours.

Why people love it: Affordable French bites, family-friendly menu, and takeaway options.
Must-try dishes: Ham and cheese baguette, almond croissant, and cappuccino.

10. Madeleine Boutique Al Ula

Madeleine is known for its luxury cakes, French desserts, and high-quality coffee. It’s the perfect place for celebrations or enjoying a sweet evening treat.

Why people love it: Elegant dessert presentation, peaceful environment, and rich flavors.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

Final Thoughts

Al Ula is not only a historical and cultural hub but also a growing culinary destination. These 10 French restaurants and cafés in Al Ula combine elegance with authentic flavors, making them ideal for tourists and locals alike. From fine dining under the desert stars to casual cafés in the old town, you’ll find plenty of options to satisfy your craving for French food in this magical city.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Al Ula

1. Are French restaurants in Al Ula expensive?

Most French restaurants in Al Ula range from mid to upscale pricing, especially those inside resorts and luxury areas.

2. Do French restaurants in Al Ula serve halal food?

Yes, all restaurants in Al Ula serve halal-certified dishes, including French restaurants.

3. Which is the best French restaurant in Al Ula for fine dining?

Annabel’s and Villa One are among the top fine dining French experiences in Al Ula.

4. Can I find French cafés with pastries in Al Ula?

Yes, places like Bon Appétit Café and Madeleine Boutique offer fresh French pastries and desserts.
