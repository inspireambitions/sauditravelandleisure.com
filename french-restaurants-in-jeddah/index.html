10 Top Best French Restaurants in Jeddah

Updated September 2, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jeddah, the cultural hub of Saudi Arabia, is famous for its diverse food scene. While Middle Eastern and international cuisines dominate the city, French dining has carved out a special place. From elegant fine dining to cozy bistros, Jeddah offers plenty of options for those who crave French flavors.

In this guide, we’ve handpicked the 10 best French restaurants in Jeddah where you can enjoy everything from buttery croissants and fresh pastries to gourmet French entrées and fine wines.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Jeddah

1. Brioché

Brioché is a French-inspired café and restaurant serving freshly baked pastries, croissants, and French toast alongside classic mains. It’s perfect for breakfast or a light lunch.

2. Paul

A global favorite, Paul offers authentic French bakery items, including artisan bread, pastries, and quiches. Their soups and sandwiches make it a go-to spot for casual French dining in Jeddah.

3. Le Traiteur

Le Traiteur is known for its fine dining French menu, offering steak au poivre, duck confit, and a variety of seafood dishes. Its sophisticated setting makes it ideal for romantic dinners.

4. Boulangerie Et Patisserie

If you love French baking, Boulangerie Et Patisserie brings Paris to Jeddah with macarons, éclairs, and croissants that taste just like they’re from France.

5. Pierre Marcolini Café

A luxury chocolate and dessert café, Pierre Marcolini specializes in decadent French sweets, pastries, and artisan chocolates. A must-visit for dessert lovers.

6. Voila

Voila combines French café vibes with a modern twist. From crepes and salads to freshly brewed coffee and fine pastries, it’s a cozy spot for casual French dining.

7. The French Corner

As the name suggests, The French Corner focuses on authentic French flavors. Their onion soup, steak, and pastries make it a popular destination among locals.

9. Madeleine Patisserie

For authentic French patisserie, Madeleine is a top pick. They offer a wide selection of pastries, cakes, and specialty coffee, perfect for an indulgent afternoon treat.

10. La Cuisine

La Cuisine offers a blend of traditional French cooking with Mediterranean flair. Their menu includes ratatouille, roasted chicken, and a variety of pasta and risottos.

Final Words

French cuisine in Jeddah is thriving, offering a mix of fine dining, cozy cafés, and authentic bakeries. Whether you’re craving a buttery croissant, a classic French dessert, or a gourmet main course, these 10 best French restaurants in Jeddah guarantee a memorable dining experience.
