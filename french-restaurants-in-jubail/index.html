Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Jubail

Updated September 3, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jubail, one of Saudi Arabia’s most modern and industrial cities, is also home to a diverse food scene. While Middle Eastern cuisine is widely available, many residents and visitors also look for international flavors—especially French cuisine. Whether you’re in the mood for a buttery croissant, a fresh baguette sandwich, or a fine French dinner, Jubail offers several great options.

Here are the 10 best French restaurants and cafés in Jubail where you can enjoy authentic French flavors.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Jubail

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant Jubail

Paul is an international French bakery brand that brings authentic Parisian flavors to Jubail. It’s one of the most popular spots in the city for breakfast, brunch, and light dinners. The elegant setting and freshly baked breads make it a favorite among families and professionals.

Why people love it: Classic Parisian atmosphere, fresh bakery items, and wide menu variety.
Must-try dishes: Butter croissant, French onion soup, and chocolate éclair.

2. Brioche Dorée Jubail

Brioche Dorée is a casual French café with a menu full of sandwiches, pastries, and coffee. In Jubail, it has become a go-to place for people who want a quick yet authentic French bite.

Why people love it: Light French meals, cozy environment, and friendly service.
Must-try dishes: Almond croissant, chicken baguette, and cappuccino.

3. Le Croissant Café

Le Croissant Café in Jubail is loved for its freshly baked croissants with both sweet and savory options. It’s an excellent choice for breakfast or a light evening snack.

Why people love it: Wide variety of croissant flavors, affordable pricing, and family-friendly seating.
Must-try dishes: Cheese croissant, pistachio croissant, and latte.

4. La Madeleine Café

This café offers country-style French cooking with a homely touch. It’s popular with families and travelers who prefer hearty meals.

Why people love it: Comfort food, warm hospitality, and rustic French-inspired dishes.

5. Café de Paris Jubail

Café de Paris is a stylish French café where people gather to enjoy desserts, coffee, and a relaxed ambiance. It’s perfect for evening meetups and dessert lovers.

Why people love it: Parisian atmosphere, excellent desserts, and cozy seating.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, hot chocolate, and chocolate mousse.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant

Bon Appétit brings a mix of French and European flavors to Jubail. It is popular for group dining and family meals, thanks to its large menu variety.

Why people love it: Wide selection of dishes, family-friendly environment, and authentic taste.
Must-try dishes: Ratatouille, grilled salmon with butter sauce, and crème caramel.

7. Maison du Croissant

A specialty café focusing on croissants and French pastries, Maison du Croissant is loved by those who enjoy light, sweet breakfasts or evening snacks.

Why people love it: Fresh croissants daily, relaxing café design, and attentive service.
Must-try dishes: Pistachio croissant, chocolate croissant, and espresso.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger Jubail

This artisan bakery offers traditional French breads, sandwiches, and quiches. It’s perfect for those who enjoy authentic baked goods.

Why people love it: Artisan-style bread, authentic French recipes, and takeaway convenience.
Must-try dishes: French baguette, quiche Lorraine, and sourdough bread.

9. Relais Parisien Jubail

Relais Parisien is a fine dining French restaurant in Jubail that specializes in gourmet dishes and premium steaks. It’s an upscale option for those who want a more refined dining experience.

Why people love it: Elegant dining setup, authentic gourmet French dishes, and great service.
Must-try dishes: Steak au poivre, chicken with béarnaise sauce, and crème brÃ»lée.

10. Madeleine Boutique Café

This boutique café is known for its premium desserts, cakes, and high-quality coffee. It’s the perfect place for celebrations, special occasions, or a relaxing evening.

Why people love it: Elegant presentation, calm environment, and delicious French desserts.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

Final Thoughts

Jubail’s food culture reflects its international community, and French restaurants have become an important part of that. From fine dining to casual cafés, the city offers a wide range of French options for every occasion. Whether you want to start your day with a croissant and coffee or end it with a French steak dinner, these 10 French restaurants in Jubail will give you a true taste of France.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Jubail

1. Are French restaurants in Jubail halal?

Yes, all French restaurants and cafés in Jubail serve halal-certified food.

2. Which French café in Jubail is best for breakfast?

Paul Bakery & Restaurant and Brioche Dorée are among the most popular for French-style breakfasts.

3. Do French restaurants in Jubail offer vegetarian options?

Yes, many restaurants include vegetarian-friendly dishes like salads, vegetable quiches, and cheese platters.

4. What is the best French restaurant in Jubail for fine dining?

Relais Parisien is one of the best fine dining French restaurants in Jubail.
