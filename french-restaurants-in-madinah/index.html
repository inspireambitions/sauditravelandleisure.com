Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Madinah

Updated September 2, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Madinah is not only a spiritual city but also a place where different cuisines meet. While traditional Saudi and Middle Eastern foods dominate, French restaurants and cafés are also becoming popular here. From fresh croissants and pastries to classic French main courses, visitors can enjoy a taste of Paris right in Madinah.

Here are the 10 best French restaurants in Madinah you should try.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Madinah

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant

Paul is one of the most famous French bakery chains worldwide, and its branch in Madinah is loved for freshly baked bread, pastries, and authentic French meals.

Must-try: Croque monsieur and chocolate éclair.

2. Brioche Dorée

This modern French café in Madinah serves croissants, baguettes, light sandwiches, and great coffee. It’s perfect for breakfast or a quick snack.

Must-try: Almond croissant with cappuccino.

3. La Madeleine Café

Known for cozy French dining, La Madeleine offers fresh salads, hearty soups, and signature French chicken dishes.

Must-try: French onion soup and roast chicken.

4. Le Croissant Café

If you’re craving a buttery croissant or fresh baguette, Le Croissant Café is a favorite in Madinah. It’s great for a light breakfast before visiting the Prophet’s Mosque.

Must-try: Butter croissant with café latte.

5. Café de Paris

Café de Paris is loved for its desserts and warm French ambiance. It’s an excellent place for evening coffee or family gatherings.

Must-try: Mille-feuille and hot chocolate.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant

This French-inspired restaurant serves a mix of European and French dishes. It’s popular for families who want a full meal experience.

Must-try: Ratatouille and crème brÃ»lée.

7. Maison du Croissant

As the name suggests, Maison du Croissant is all about croissants and pastries. Their variety of flavors makes it a must-visit spot.

Must-try: Pistachio croissant.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger

A bakery specializing in rustic French breads and sandwiches. It’s a good option for those who love artisan-style baking.

Must-try: Fresh baguette with cheese.

9. Le Relais Parisien

For fine dining in Madinah, Le Relais Parisien offers authentic French steaks, sauces, and cheese platters.

Must-try: Steak au poivre with French fries.

10. Café Madeleine

A boutique-style café offering French pastries, cakes, and premium coffee. The relaxing environment makes it perfect for a quiet evening.

Must-try: Macarons and strawberry tart.

Final Thoughts

Madinah is home to more than just traditional food—it also offers a variety of French cafés and restaurants. Whether you’re looking for a quick croissant with coffee or a luxurious French dining experience, these places bring the flavors of France to Madinah.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Madinah

1. Are French restaurants in Madinah halal?

Yes, all French restaurants and cafés in Madinah serve halal-certified food, suitable for both locals and visitors.

2. Where can I find the best French breakfast in Madinah?

Paul Bakery & Restaurant and Brioche Dorée are among the top choices for authentic French breakfast options.

3. Do French restaurants in Madinah serve vegetarian food?

Yes, many offer vegetarian dishes such as vegetable quiche, salads, and cheese platters.
