10 Top Best French Restaurants in Makkah

Updated September 2, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Makkah is known for its spiritual atmosphere and rich cultural food scene. While traditional Saudi and Middle Eastern dishes dominate the city, French cuisine has also found its place here. From elegant fine dining to casual cafés serving freshly baked croissants, you can enjoy authentic French flavors without leaving Makkah.

In this guide, we share the 10 best French restaurants in Makkah where you can enjoy classic dishes, desserts, and cozy dining experiences.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Makkah

1. Albaik French Bistro

One of the most talked-about French-inspired spots in Makkah, Albaik French Bistro combines Saudi hospitality with French recipes. Their menu includes creamy soups, grilled meats, and signature French pastries.

Must-try: French onion soup and crème brÃ»lée.

2. Le Croissant Café

A perfect place for a light French breakfast in Makkah. The café serves freshly baked croissants, baguettes, and artisan coffee. It’s a great stop before heading to Masjid al-Haram.

Must-try: Butter croissant with hot chocolate.

3. Le Relais Parisien

If you are looking for fine dining, Le Relais Parisien offers a true French experience. The restaurant is known for its steak dishes, cheese platters, and authentic sauces.

Must-try: Steak au poivre with French fries.

4. Paul Bakery & Restaurant

Paul is a famous international French bakery chain, and its branch in Makkah doesn’t disappoint. It serves freshly baked bread, sandwiches, and French desserts.

Must-try: Chocolate éclair and quiche Lorraine.

5. Brioche Dorée

A modern French café with a variety of light meals and pastries. It’s a favorite for people who enjoy casual dining and quick bites.

Must-try: Almond croissant and café latte.

6. La Madeleine Makkah

This cozy spot brings French country-style cooking to the heart of Makkah. Their menu is filled with comfort food and baked goods.

Must-try: French roast chicken with herbs.

7. Café de Paris

Café de Paris is popular for its relaxed ambiance and authentic French desserts. It’s a perfect place to enjoy sweets with friends or family.

Must-try: Mille-feuille and Parisian hot chocolate.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger

Known for its artisan baking, this café specializes in traditional baguettes and rustic breads. It’s a great place for bread lovers.

Must-try: Baguette sandwich with French cheese.

9. Maison du Croissant

As the name suggests, this bakery is all about croissants and French pastries. It is a go-to place for a sweet breakfast or evening snack.

Must-try: Pistachio croissant.

10. Bon Appétit Restaurant

Bon Appétit offers a wide variety of French and European dishes. From soups and salads to gourmet desserts, it’s a great choice for families.

Must-try: Ratatouille with French bread.

Final Thoughts

Makkah offers more than just traditional food—it also welcomes flavors from around the world. Whether you’re looking for a light croissant with coffee or a fine dining French meal, these restaurants bring the taste of France to Makkah.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Makkah

1. Are there authentic French restaurants in Makkah?

Yes, Makkah has several restaurants and cafés that serve authentic French dishes, including croissants, baguettes, pastries, and fine dining meals.

2. Can I find halal French food in Makkah?

Absolutely. All French restaurants in Makkah serve halal-certified food, making them suitable for all visitors.

3. What is the best French restaurant near Masjid al-Haram?

Paul Bakery & Restaurant and Le Croissant Café are among the most popular choices near Masjid al-Haram.

4. Do French restaurants in Makkah offer vegetarian options?

Yes, many French restaurants in Makkah include vegetarian-friendly dishes such as salads, vegetable quiche, and cheese platters.
