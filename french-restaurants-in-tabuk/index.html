10 Top Best French Restaurants in Tabuk

Updated September 3, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Tabuk, located in the northwest of Saudi Arabia, is known for its mountains, history, and proximity to stunning destinations like NEOM and the Red Sea coast. With tourism and development growing in the city, international restaurants have become popular. Among them, French cuisine stands out, offering residents and travelers a mix of elegance, comfort, and authentic flavors.

Whether you’re craving buttery croissants, freshly baked baguettes, or a gourmet French dinner, Tabuk has excellent options. Here are the 10 best French restaurants and cafés in Tabuk you should try.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Tabuk

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant Tabuk

Paul is an international French bakery brand that brings authentic Parisian flavors to Tabuk. It’s perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a light dinner.

Why people love it: Elegant Parisian vibe, fresh bread and pastries, and wide menu choices.
Must-try dishes: Croissants, French onion soup, and chocolate éclairs.

2. Brioche Dorée Tabuk

This French café chain is loved worldwide for its fresh pastries and light meals. In Tabuk, it has become a favorite spot for people who want a quick French snack or coffee.

Why people love it: Casual dining, great coffee, and authentic French bakery items.
Must-try dishes: Almond croissant, chicken baguette, and cappuccino.

3. Le Croissant Café

Le Croissant Café is popular in Tabuk for its wide range of freshly baked croissants—both sweet and savory. It’s an ideal spot for breakfast or an evening snack.

Why people love it: Affordable prices, variety of croissant flavors, and family-friendly atmosphere.
Must-try dishes: Cheese croissant, pistachio croissant, and latte.

4. La Madeleine Café

La Madeleine offers French country-style food with a cozy and homely atmosphere. It’s perfect for families looking for hearty meals.

Why people love it: Comfort food, rustic décor, and freshly made dishes.
Must-try dishes: Roast chicken, ratatouille, and crème brÃ»lée.

5. Café de Paris Tabuk

Café de Paris is a stylish French café that’s great for relaxing evenings with desserts and coffee. It’s a favorite among locals for small gatherings.

Why people love it: Chic design, quality desserts, and friendly service.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, hot chocolate, and mousse au chocolat.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant Tabuk

Bon Appétit combines French and European dishes, making it a versatile choice for groups and families.

Why people love it: Wide menu variety, comfortable environment, and authentic flavors.
Must-try dishes: Ratatouille, grilled salmon with lemon butter, and crème caramel.

7. Maison du Croissant

This bakery specializes in croissants and French-style pastries. It’s an excellent stop for breakfast or a light snack.

Why people love it: Fresh daily pastries, simple yet delicious menu, and calm seating area.
Must-try dishes: Pistachio croissant, chocolate croissant, and espresso.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger Tabuk

An artisan-style bakery that serves freshly baked breads, quiches, and sandwiches. It’s especially popular for takeaways.

Why people love it: Artisan bread, authentic French recipes, and healthy options.
Must-try dishes: Baguette, quiche Lorraine, and sourdough bread.

9. Relais Parisien Tabuk

Relais Parisien is a fine dining restaurant where guests can enjoy gourmet French dishes in an upscale atmosphere.

Why people love it: Luxury dining experience, authentic gourmet meals, and professional service.
Must-try dishes: Steak au poivre, chicken with béarnaise sauce, and crème brÃ»lée.

10. Madeleine Boutique Café

This boutique café is known for its elegant desserts, cakes, and high-quality coffee. It’s perfect for birthdays, gatherings, or evening treats.

Why people love it: Beautiful dessert presentation, stylish environment, and premium taste.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

Final Thoughts

Tabuk’s growing international food culture has made French cuisine a popular choice for both locals and visitors. From fine dining restaurants to casual cafés, these 10 French restaurants in Tabuk offer a wide range of experiences. Whether you want to grab a fresh croissant in the morning or enjoy a luxurious French dinner, Tabuk has something to offer.

FAQs – French Restaurants in Tabuk

1. Are French restaurants in Tabuk halal?

Yes, all French restaurants in Tabuk serve halal-certified meals.

2. Which French café in Tabuk is best for breakfast?

Paul Bakery and Brioche Dorée are among the best choices for a French-style breakfast.

3. Do French restaurants in Tabuk offer vegetarian options?

Yes, many include vegetarian dishes such as salads, quiches, and vegetable platters.
