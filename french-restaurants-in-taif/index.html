10 Top Best French Restaurants in Taif

Updated September 3, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Taif, the city of roses in Saudi Arabia, is famous for its cool weather, stunning mountains, and beautiful gardens. Beyond its natural beauty, Taif also offers a growing culinary scene with international flavors. Among them, French cuisine has become a favorite, thanks to its mix of elegance, taste, and artistry. From cozy cafés serving croissants to fine dining restaurants offering gourmet French dishes, Taif has plenty of options for food lovers.

In this guide, we highlight the 10 best French restaurants in Taif where you can enjoy authentic French flavors.

Top Best French Restaurants in Taif

1. Paul

Paul is one of the most popular French bakery cafés in Saudi Arabia. In Taif, it offers a delightful range of croissants, artisan bread, pastries, and light meals. Their quiches and soups are also highly recommended.

2. Brioche Dorée

Brioche Dorée brings Parisian vibes to Taif with freshly baked French bread, sandwiches, and breakfast options. It’s perfect for a light brunch or coffee break.

3. La Fontaine

Known for its elegant setting, La Fontaine serves a blend of French and European cuisines. Their steak dishes, seafood entrées, and signature desserts make it a great choice for fine dining.

4. The French Corner

The French Corner is a favorite spot for authentic French meals in Taif. With options like onion soup, pasta, and a variety of pastries, it offers both casual and semi-formal dining.

5. Café Madeleine

Café Madeleine specializes in French patisserie, offering éclairs, tarts, and macarons alongside hot drinks. It’s an excellent place for an afternoon sweet treat.

6. Le Délice

Le Délice combines French café culture with modern flavors. From crepes and croissants to salads and coffee, it provides a cozy environment for casual meals.

7. La Cuisine Française

La Cuisine Française offers traditional French main courses like ratatouille, roasted chicken, and fine pasta dishes. It’s an excellent choice for those who love hearty French meals.

8. Café de Paris

Inspired by Parisian cafés, Café de Paris serves a mix of French pastries, sandwiches, and specialty coffee. Their mille-feuille and croissants are highly rated by customers.

Final Words

French restaurants in Taif bring a mix of casual cafés, patisseries, and elegant fine dining options. Whether you’re craving a buttery croissant with coffee or a full French dinner, these 10 best French restaurants in Taif promise an unforgettable experience.
