Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Yanbu

Updated September 3, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Yanbu, a coastal city on the Red Sea, is known for its industrial significance and growing tourism sector. Beyond its beaches and port, Yanbu also has a diverse food culture. Among its international offerings, French cuisine stands out for those who love croissants, pastries, and elegant European dining. Whether you want a quick breakfast, a coffee with a pastry, or a refined French dinner, Yanbu has several options to explore.

Here are the 10 top French restaurants and cafés in Yanbu that locals and visitors enjoy.

Top 10 Best French Restaurants in Yanbu

1. Paul Bakery & Restaurant Yanbu

Paul is one of the most recognized French bakery brands worldwide, and in Yanbu, it’s a go-to spot for authentic Parisian flavors. It offers freshly baked breads, pastries, and classic French dishes.

Why people love it: Elegant Parisian vibe, freshly baked goods, and wide menu variety.
Must-try dishes: Butter croissant, French onion soup, and éclair au chocolat.

2. Brioche Dorée Yanbu

Brioche Dorée is a French café chain loved for its simple, authentic flavors. It’s perfect for breakfast, light meals, or afternoon coffee breaks.

Why people love it: Cozy café atmosphere, fresh pastries, and quick service.
Must-try dishes: Chicken baguette, almond croissant, and cappuccino.

3. La Madeleine Café

La Madeleine brings French country-style cooking to Yanbu. With homely dishes and a comfortable atmosphere, it’s a favorite for families.

Why people love it: Comfort food, hearty portions, and warm ambiance.
Must-try dishes: Roast chicken with herbs, ratatouille, and crème brÃ»lée.

4. Le Croissant Café

This café is known for its freshly baked croissants with both savory and sweet fillings. It’s ideal for breakfast or a light evening meal.

Why people love it: Wide range of croissant flavors, family-friendly vibe, and affordable prices.
Must-try dishes: Cheese croissant, pistachio croissant, and latte.

5. Café de Paris Yanbu

Café de Paris is one of the most stylish French cafés in Yanbu, offering a relaxed environment with desserts and coffee. It’s a favorite spot for meetups.

Why people love it: Chic Parisian design, high-quality desserts, and welcoming service.
Must-try dishes: Mille-feuille, hot chocolate, and chocolate mousse.

6. Bon Appétit Restaurant

Bon Appétit offers a mix of French and European dishes, making it a versatile choice for families and groups.

Why people love it: Large menu, authentic flavors, and relaxed setting.
Must-try dishes: Beef bourguignon, grilled salmon with butter sauce, and crème caramel.

7. Maison du Croissant

Maison du Croissant is a boutique café specializing in croissants and pastries. It’s perfect for light snacks or sweet treats.

Why people love it: Freshly baked pastries, cozy environment, and excellent coffee.
Must-try dishes: Chocolate croissant, pistachio croissant, and espresso.

8. L’Artisan Boulanger Yanbu

This artisan-style bakery focuses on breads, quiches, and light French meals. It’s popular for takeaway and casual dining.

Why people love it: Freshly baked bread, authentic French recipes, and healthy options.
Must-try dishes: French baguette, quiche Lorraine, and sourdough bread.

9. Relais Parisien Yanbu

An upscale option in Yanbu, Relais Parisien offers fine dining French cuisine. It’s perfect for business dinners and special occasions.

Why people love it: Elegant dining setup, gourmet French dishes, and professional service.
Must-try dishes: Steak au poivre, duck confit, and crème brÃ»lée.

10. Madeleine Boutique Café

Madeleine Boutique Café is popular for its high-end desserts and French-style cakes. It’s an excellent choice for celebrating birthdays or enjoying a relaxed evening.

Why people love it: Beautiful presentation, refined ambiance, and delicious sweets.
Must-try dishes: Macarons, strawberry tart, and tiramisu.

Final Thoughts

Yanbu may be best known for its industries and seaport, but its dining scene has also embraced international flavors. These 10 French restaurants in Yanbu offer something for everyone—from casual cafés with croissants to elegant fine dining spots. Whether you’re a local resident or visiting Yanbu, you can enjoy a little taste of Paris right here in Saudi Arabia.
