Top 10 Hotels to Stay in Abha 2025 Updated

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Abha, the capital of the Asir Province in Saudi Arabia, is known for its cool climate, green mountains, and scenic landscapes. Nestled in the Sarawat Mountains, the city has become a popular destination for tourists seeking nature, culture, and relaxation.

Choosing the right hotel in Abha can elevate your travel experience, whether you’re visiting for leisure, a family trip, or a romantic getaway. From luxury resorts to cozy boutique stays, Abha offers accommodation for every preference.

Here’s a guide to the top 10 hotels to stay in Abha in 2025, featuring the best amenities, locations, and experiences.

Top Hotels In Abha

1. Abha Palace Hotel

Abha Palace Hotel is one of the most luxurious hotels in the city, offering spacious rooms, modern amenities, and breathtaking mountain views.

Highlights: Panoramic terrace, spa, fine dining restaurants
Ideal For: Luxury travelers, couples, and honeymooners

2. Raffles Abha Hotel

Raffles Abha combines elegance with comfort. Guests enjoy modern interiors, high-end services, and convenient access to Abha’s main attractions.

Highlights: Rooftop restaurant, spa services, conference halls
Ideal For: Business travelers and luxury seekers

3. Green Palace Hotel

Green Palace Hotel provides a blend of comfort and affordability. Its central location allows guests to explore local markets, parks, and tourist spots with ease.

Highlights: Swimming pool, fitness center, family-friendly rooms
Ideal For: Families and short-stay tourists

4. Al Sawah Hotel

Al Sawah Hotel is a popular choice for mid-range travelers seeking convenience and quality. The hotel offers cozy rooms, friendly service, and easy access to scenic areas around Abha.

Highlights: Free Wi-Fi, breakfast included, spacious rooms
Ideal For: Couples and solo travelers

5. Abha Palace Resort

This resort provides a luxury experience surrounded by the city’s natural beauty. Guests can enjoy mountain views, fine dining, and wellness facilities.

Highlights: Spa, outdoor pool, fine dining
Ideal For: Leisure travelers and families

6. Habala Hotel

Habala Hotel offers modern amenities in a comfortable setting. Its location makes it easy to access Abha’s main attractions, including Al-Soudah Park and Asir National Park.

Highlights: Rooftop terrace, fitness center, conference rooms
Ideal For: Business and leisure travelers

7. InterContinental Abha

InterContinental Abha delivers world-class hospitality with elegant interiors and premium services. Guests enjoy spacious rooms, international dining, and high-end amenities.

Highlights: Spa, fine dining, conference facilities
Ideal For: Luxury and business travelers

8. Abha Rose Hotel

Abha Rose Hotel is a boutique option offering cozy rooms, friendly staff, and convenient access to tourist spots. Perfect for travelers seeking comfort without breaking the bank.

Highlights: Free breakfast, comfortable rooms, central location
Ideal For: Budget-conscious travelers and solo travelers

9. Shada Hotel

Shada Hotel provides a mix of comfort and convenience. Its strategic location allows guests to explore Abha’s cultural landmarks and shopping areas easily.

Highlights: Modern rooms, on-site restaurant, Wi-Fi access
Ideal For: Families and short-term stays

10. Al Fayroz Hotel

Al Fayroz Hotel is known for its warm hospitality and comfortable rooms. It’s a suitable option for both leisure travelers and business visitors.

Highlights: Breakfast included, parking facilities, central location
Ideal For: Couples, families, and business travelers

FAQ About Hotels in Abha

Which is the best luxury hotel in Abha?

Abha Palace Hotel and Raffles Abha Hotel are the top luxury options, offering premium amenities and scenic mountain views.

Are there family-friendly hotels in Abha?

Yes, Green Palace Hotel, Abha Palace Resort, and Shada Hotel offer family-friendly rooms and amenities.

Which hotels are best for business travelers?

Raffles Abha Hotel, Habala Hotel, and InterContinental Abha provide conference facilities, business centers, and convenient locations.

Are there budget-friendly hotels in Abha?

Yes, Abha Rose Hotel, Al Sawah Hotel, and Shada Hotel offer comfort at affordable rates.

Do these hotels offer mountain views?

Many hotels, including Abha Palace Hotel and Abha Palace Resort, provide stunning views of the surrounding Sarawat Mountains.

Final Words

Abha offers a wide range of hotels for every type of traveler. From luxurious resorts like Abha Palace Hotel to boutique options like Abha Rose Hotel, you can enjoy breathtaking views, comfortable stays, and excellent service. Whether you’re visiting for leisure, business, or family trips, these top 10 hotels in Abha 2025 will ensure a memorable and comfortable experience.
