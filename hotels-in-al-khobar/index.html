Top 10 Best Hotels to Stay in Al Khobar for Every Budget (2025)

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Khobar, a vibrant city in Saudi Arabia's Eastern Province, offers a diverse range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs.

Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or a family vacation, choosing the right hotel can enhance your experience.

In 2025, Al Khobar boasts an array of options, from luxurious beachfront resorts to budget-friendly city hotels. Here's a curated list of the top 10 hotels in Al Khobar, catering to various budgets and preferences.

Best Hotels to Stay in Al Khobar

Luxury Hotels (5-Star) In AL Khobar

1. Grand Hyatt Al Khobar Hotel and Residences

Located in the heart of Al Khobar, the Grand Hyatt offers elegance and comfort with spacious rooms, a full-service spa, and panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf. It's ideal for both business and leisure travelers seeking top-tier amenities.

2. Kempinski Al Othman Hotel

Situated along the scenic coastline, Kempinski Al Othman combines traditional Arabian hospitality with modern luxury. Guests can enjoy gourmet dining, a luxurious spa, and well-appointed rooms with sea views.

3. Sofitel Al Khobar The Corniche

Offering French elegance with Arabian touches, Sofitel is known for its refined interiors, exceptional service, and stunning views of the Corniche. It's perfect for travelers seeking a blend of luxury and cultural charm.

4. Hilton Al Khobar King Fahd Causeway

This hotel provides a blend of comfort and luxury, featuring spacious rooms, a variety of dining options, and easy access to the King Fahd Causeway connecting to Bahrain. It's suitable for both business and leisure stays.

Mid-Range Hotels (4-Star)

5. voco Al Khobar by IHG

voco Al Khobar offers a contemporary stay with modern amenities, including a spa, fitness center, and multiple dining options. Its central location makes it convenient for exploring the city's attractions.

6. InterContinental Al Khobar

Known for its luxurious accommodations and prime location, InterContinental Al Khobar offers spacious rooms, a variety of dining options, and excellent service, making it a favorite among travelers.

7. Movenpick Hotel Al Khobar

Movenpick combines Swiss hospitality with Arabian warmth, offering stylish rooms, a rooftop pool, and several dining venues. It's ideal for those seeking comfort and convenience at a reasonable price.

Budget-Friendly Hotels (3-Star)

8. Holiday Inn Al Khobar - Corniche

Located near the Corniche, Holiday Inn offers comfortable rooms, an outdoor pool, and easy access to shopping and dining options. It's a great choice for travelers looking for value without compromising on quality.

9. Crowne Plaza Al Khobar

Situated in the city center, Crowne Plaza provides modern accommodations, a variety of dining options, and excellent business facilities, making it suitable for both business and leisure travelers.

10. Staybridge Suites Al Khobar

Ideal for extended stays, Staybridge Suites offers spacious suites with kitchenettes, complimentary breakfast, and a welcoming atmosphere. It's perfect for families or travelers planning a longer visit.

FAQ – Hotels in Al Khobar


Which hotel in Al Khobar offers the best luxury experience?

The Grand Hyatt Al Khobar Hotel and Residences is renowned for its luxurious accommodations and top-tier amenities, making it a top choice for luxury travelers.

Are there family-friendly hotels in Al Khobar?

Yes, Staybridge Suites Al Khobar offers spacious suites with kitchenettes and complimentary breakfast, making it ideal for families

Which hotels are best for business travelers?

InterContinental Al Khobar and Crowne Plaza Al Khobar provide excellent business facilities, including meeting rooms and business centers.

Final Words

Al Khobar's diverse hotel offerings ensure that every traveler can find accommodation that fits their budget and preferences. From luxurious beachfront resorts to comfortable mid-range and budget-friendly options, the city caters to all. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, these top 10 hotels in Al Khobar in 2025 promise a memorable stay.
