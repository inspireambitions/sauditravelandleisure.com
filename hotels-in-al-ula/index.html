Top 10 Stunning Hotels to Stay in Al Ula for Unique Experience

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Ula, a historic city in northwestern Saudi Arabia, is renowned for its dramatic rock formations, ancient heritage sites, and the UNESCO World Heritage site of Madain Saleh. Visiting this desert gem offers a chance to explore archaeological wonders and breathtaking landscapes. Choosing the right hotel is essential to enjoy a memorable stay.

This guide highlights the top 10 hotels in Al Ula, offering a mix of luxury, comfort, and unique experiences for every type of traveler in 2025.

Best Hotels in Al Ula

1. Shaden Resort

Shaden Resort combines luxury with nature. Nestled amidst the desert landscapes, it offers private villas, an infinity pool, and panoramic views of Al Ula’s mountains. Perfect for travelers seeking tranquility and elegance.

Highlights: Desert views, private villas, infinity pool
Ideal For: Couples and luxury travelers

2. Banyan Tree AlUla

Banyan Tree AlUla is a world-class desert resort offering modern amenities and eco-friendly architecture. Guests can enjoy spa treatments, outdoor pools, and exceptional dining experiences in a unique desert setting.

Highlights: Spa, outdoor pools, eco-friendly design
Ideal For: Luxury and wellness travelers

3. Ashar Resort

Ashar Resort features spacious desert lodges surrounded by stunning landscapes. The resort emphasizes comfort and adventure, offering guided tours, desert experiences, and stargazing opportunities.

Highlights: Desert lodges, guided tours, stargazing
Ideal For: Adventure and nature enthusiasts

4. Habitas Al Ula

Habitas offers a sustainable and immersive experience in Al Ula. Its tents and eco-lodges provide a blend of modern comfort and desert authenticity. Guests can enjoy cultural experiences, yoga, and nature excursions.

Highlights: Eco-lodges, cultural experiences, nature activities
Ideal For: Eco-conscious and experiential travelers

5. Sahary Al Ula

Sahary Al Ula is a boutique hotel known for personalized service and unique desert experiences. Guests can enjoy luxurious accommodations, local-inspired decor, and exclusive desert adventures.

Highlights: Boutique luxury, desert excursions, local decor
Ideal For: Couples and small groups

6. Al Ula Arac Resort

Al Ula Arac Resort offers comfortable rooms and villas with stunning views of the surrounding canyons. The resort is perfect for travelers seeking a relaxing base while exploring nearby heritage sites.

Highlights: Scenic views, comfortable accommodations, tour services
Ideal For: Sightseeing and leisure travelers

7. Intercontinental Al Ula

Intercontinental provides modern comfort and luxury within easy reach of Al Ula’s attractions. Guests enjoy spacious rooms, pools, and fine dining, ideal for those who prefer a conventional luxury hotel experience.

Highlights: Pool, modern amenities, fine dining
Ideal For: Luxury and business travelers

8. Desert Rose Camp

Desert Rose Camp offers authentic desert camping with modern facilities. Guests can sleep under the stars in well-equipped tents, experience traditional cuisine, and enjoy desert activities.

Highlights: Desert camping, stargazing, cultural cuisine
Ideal For: Adventure and cultural experience seekers

9. Al Ula Resort & Villas

This resort combines private villas with resort amenities, providing a comfortable and luxurious stay. Guests can enjoy swimming pools, fine dining, and guided tours to Al Ula’s historical sites.

Highlights: Private villas, pools, guided tours
Ideal For: Families and luxury travelers

10. Sharaan Resort

Sharaan Resort is known for its exclusive location and high-end services. Nestled near natural rock formations, it offers luxury lodges, wellness programs, and curated desert excursions.

Highlights: Exclusive location, wellness programs, desert excursions
Ideal For: Luxury and wellness travelers

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which hotel in Al Ula is best for luxury travelers?

Banyan Tree AlUla and Shaden Resort offer high-end amenities, private villas, and stunning desert views.

Are there eco-friendly hotels in Al Ula?

Yes, Habitas Al Ula focuses on sustainability while providing modern comforts and unique desert experiences.

Which hotels offer desert adventure experiences?

Ashar Resort, Desert Rose Camp, and Sahary Al Ula provide desert tours, stargazing, and cultural activities.

Are there family-friendly resorts in Al Ula?

Yes, Al Ula Resort & Villas and Sharaan Resort offer villas, pools, and activities suitable for families.

Which hotel provides authentic desert camping experience?

Desert Rose Camp offers well-equipped tents under the stars with desert excursions and traditional cuisine.

Final Words

Al Ula is a destination like no other, combining history, culture, and stunning desert landscapes. The city offers a range of accommodations from luxurious resorts like Banyan Tree AlUla and Shaden Resort to authentic desert experiences like Desert Rose Camp. Choosing the right hotel ensures a unique and memorable stay while exploring this fascinating part of Saudi Arabia.
