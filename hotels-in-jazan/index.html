Top 10 Must-Stay Hotels in Jazan for a Perfect Stay

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jazan, a scenic city in southwestern Saudi Arabia, is known for its beautiful coastline, lush islands, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors come here for business, leisure, or to explore natural attractions like Farasan Islands and the Red Sea beaches. Choosing the right hotel can make your trip more comfortable and memorable.

From luxurious resorts to budget-friendly accommodations, Jazan offers a variety of options. This guide highlights the top 10 hotels in Jazan for 2025, covering comfort, convenience, and excellent service.

Best Hotels In Jazan

1. Grand Millennium Gizan

Grand Millennium is a 5-star hotel offering luxurious rooms with modern interiors. Guests can enjoy an outdoor pool, fitness center, spa, and several dining options. Its central location provides easy access to the city’s attractions and business areas.

Highlights: Outdoor pool, spa, fine dining, central location
Ideal For: Luxury travelers and business guests

2. Novotel Jazan

Novotel Jazan is a modern hotel located near popular attractions, including Happy Times Theme Park. Facilities include an outdoor pool, fitness center, and restaurant. Guests appreciate its comfort, convenience, and excellent service.

Highlights: Family-friendly, outdoor pool, fitness center
Ideal For: Families and mid-range travelers

3. Radisson Blu Resort, Jizan

This 4-star resort provides relaxing accommodations with sea and city views. Amenities include an outdoor pool, spa, and international cuisine. Radisson Blu is perfect for both business and leisure travelers seeking a serene environment.

Highlights: Spa, outdoor pool, international dining
Ideal For: Luxury and leisure travelers

4. Mirada Gold Jizan – Chalets & Holiday Homes

Mirada Gold offers private chalets with pools, ideal for families or groups. Guests enjoy the comfort of home-like accommodations combined with hotel-grade services. It’s perfect for those who want privacy and flexibility during their stay.

Highlights: Private chalets, pools, family-friendly
Ideal For: Families and group travelers

5. Courtyard by Marriott Jazan

Courtyard by Marriott is a contemporary hotel featuring spacious rooms, a restaurant, and a fitness center. Its convenient location makes it suitable for business travelers and tourists exploring Jazan.

Highlights: Modern rooms, restaurant, fitness center
Ideal For: Business travelers and short-term visitors

6. Residence Inn by Marriott Jazan

This hotel caters to long-term travelers with suites that include kitchenettes. Guests can enjoy extended stays with comfort and convenience, while still having access to hotel facilities like pools and gyms.

Highlights: Suites with kitchenettes, pool, extended stay amenities
Ideal For: Long-term and business travelers

7. Holiday Jazan Hotel

Holiday Jazan Hotel is a budget-friendly option offering comfortable rooms with essential amenities. Its central location allows easy access to shopping areas and city attractions.

Highlights: Affordable, central location, clean rooms
Ideal For: Budget travelers

8. Al Rayah Hotel

Al Rayah Hotel provides modern accommodations close to Jazan Airport and city attractions. Guests appreciate its cleanliness, friendly service, and convenience for business and short stays.

Highlights: Proximity to airport, clean rooms, friendly staff
Ideal For: Business and short-term visitors

9. Park Jazan Hotel

Park Jazan Hotel is a mid-range hotel offering cozy rooms and basic amenities. Its central location makes it suitable for tourists and travelers who want a comfortable stay without spending too much.

Highlights: Comfortable rooms, central location, affordable
Ideal For: Mid-range and budget travelers

10. Taj Nakhba Hotel

Taj Nakhba is a 3-star hotel known for its welcoming staff and well-maintained rooms. It’s a practical option for visitors seeking comfort at a reasonable price.

Highlights: Friendly staff, clean rooms, budget-friendly
Ideal For: Budget travelers

FAQ About Hotels In Jazan

Q1: Which hotel in Jazan is best for luxury travelers?

Grand Millennium Gizan and Radisson Blu Resort are ideal for luxury stays with top-notch amenities and service.

Q2: Are there family-friendly hotels in Jazan?

Yes, Novotel Jazan and Mirada Gold Jizan – Chalets & Holiday Homes offer family-oriented facilities and comfortable rooms.

Q3: Which hotels are suitable for business travelers?

Courtyard by Marriott Jazan, Residence Inn by Marriott Jazan, and Al Rayah Hotel provide business amenities and convenient locations.

Q4: Are there budget-friendly hotels in Jazan?

Yes, Holiday Jazan Hotel, Park Jazan Hotel, and Taj Nakhba Hotel offer affordable stays with essential comfort.

Q5: Do any hotels in Jazan have pools or spa facilities?

Yes, Grand Millennium Gizan, Radisson Blu Resort, and Novotel Jazan feature swimming pools, fitness centers, and spa facilities.

Final Words

Jazan offers a variety of hotels to suit every type of traveler. From luxurious stays at Grand Millennium Gizan to budget options like Holiday Jazan Hotel, visitors can choose accommodations based on comfort, location, and budget. Selecting the right hotel ensures a smooth, enjoyable stay while exploring Jazan’s natural beauty, cultural sites, and vibrant city life.
