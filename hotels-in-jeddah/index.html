Top 10 Must-Stay Hotels in Jeddah for an Unforgettable Visit

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jeddah, the gateway to the Red Sea, is one of Saudi Arabia’s most vibrant cities. Known for its beautiful corniche, historic districts, and luxury shopping, it also offers world-class accommodation. Whether you’re visiting for business, leisure, or a mix of both, choosing the right hotel can make your stay truly unforgettable.

From five-star luxury resorts to stylish boutique hotels, Jeddah has something for every traveler. In this guide, we’ll explore the top 10 must-stay hotels in Jeddah, highlighting their features, amenities, and what makes each one unique.

Top 10 Hotels in Jeddah

1. The Ritz-Carlton Jeddah

One of the most prestigious hotels in the city, The Ritz-Carlton Jeddah combines luxury with Arabian hospitality. It features spacious rooms, world-class dining options, and stunning views of the Red Sea.

Highlights: Infinity pools, spa services, private beach access, exquisite restaurants
Ideal For: Luxury travelers and honeymooners

2. Park Hyatt Jeddah – Marina, Club and Spa

Park Hyatt offers a serene retreat in the heart of Jeddah. With elegant rooms and modern amenities, it’s perfect for business and leisure travelers alike.

Highlights: Marina views, full-service spa, fine dining restaurants
Ideal For: Couples and business travelers

3. InterContinental Jeddah

Located close to the city’s business hubs, InterContinental Jeddah is ideal for those seeking comfort and convenience. The hotel boasts luxurious rooms and international dining options.

Highlights: Rooftop pool, fitness center, conference facilities
Ideal For: Business travelers

4. Movenpick Hotel City Star Jeddah

Movenpick Hotel City Star offers stylish accommodation with modern amenities. Its central location makes it easy to explore Jeddah’s cultural and commercial attractions.

Highlights: Rooftop pool, international cuisine, central location
Ideal For: Families and short-term visitors

5. Jeddah Hilton Hotel

The Jeddah Hilton Hotel is a symbol of elegance and comfort. With panoramic views of the city, it provides a perfect blend of relaxation and sophistication.

Highlights: Outdoor pool, spa, multiple dining options
Ideal For: Luxury and leisure travelers

6. Radisson Blu Hotel, Jeddah

Radisson Blu is a modern hotel offering convenience and comfort. Its contemporary design and friendly service make it a popular choice among visitors.

Highlights: Rooftop restaurant, gym, business center
Ideal For: Business travelers and couples

7. Crowne Plaza Jeddah

Crowne Plaza is perfect for guests seeking a balance of luxury and affordability. It offers comfortable rooms and easy access to shopping and business districts.

Highlights: Conference facilities, restaurants, fitness center
Ideal For: Business travelers

8. Park Inn by Radisson Jeddah

This mid-range hotel provides a cozy stay with modern amenities. Its location is ideal for exploring both shopping areas and tourist attractions.

Highlights: Family-friendly rooms, rooftop pool, casual dining
Ideal For: Families and solo travelers

9. Wyndham Garden Jeddah

Wyndham Garden offers stylish rooms with modern facilities. The hotel focuses on comfort and convenience for both business and leisure travelers.

Highlights: Swimming pool, fitness center, meeting rooms
Ideal For: Business travelers and tourists

10. Assila, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Jeddah

Assila offers a unique blend of luxury and local charm. Guests enjoy exquisite interiors, high-end amenities, and proximity to Jeddah’s top attractions.

Highlights: Rooftop terrace, fine dining, personalized services
Ideal For: Luxury and boutique hotel lovers

FAQ About Hotels in Jeddah


Which hotel in Jeddah is best for luxury stays?

The Ritz-Carlton Jeddah and Assila Luxury Collection Hotel are top choices for luxury travelers seeking premium amenities and services.

Are there family-friendly hotels in Jeddah?

Yes, hotels like Movenpick Hotel City Star, Park Inn by Radisson, and Radisson Blu are family-friendly with pools and spacious rooms.

Which hotels are best for business travelers?

InterContinental Jeddah, Radisson Blu, Crowne Plaza, and Wyndham Garden offer conference facilities, business centers, and convenient locations.

Do these hotels have sea views?

The Ritz-Carlton Jeddah and Park Hyatt offer stunning Red Sea views from select rooms and suites.

Are there budget-friendly options among these hotels?

Yes, mid-range hotels like Park Inn by Radisson and Wyndham Garden provide comfort and modern amenities at more affordable prices.

Final Words

Jeddah offers a wide range of accommodation to suit every traveler’s needs. From ultra-luxury resorts like The Ritz-Carlton to boutique hotels like Assila, each hotel provides a unique experience. Whether you’re visiting for business, leisure, or a special occasion, these top 10 must-stay hotels in Jeddah will make your visit memorable and comfortable.
