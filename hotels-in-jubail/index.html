Top 10 Best Hotels to Stay in Jubail for Comfort and Convenience

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jubail, a major industrial city in Saudi Arabia’s Eastern Province, is known for its modern infrastructure, business hubs, and seaside attractions. Whether visiting for work or leisure, choosing the right hotel can enhance your stay. The city offers a range of accommodation, from luxury resorts to budget-friendly hotels, ensuring comfort and convenience for every traveler.

Here’s a guide to the top 10 best hotels to stay in Jubail, perfect for business travelers, families, and tourists alike.

Best Hotels In Jubail

1. Mövenpick Hotel Jubail

Mövenpick Hotel offers modern luxury with a focus on comfort and service. Guests can enjoy spacious rooms, multiple dining options, and a rooftop pool with city views. It’s perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

Highlights: Rooftop pool, fitness center, fine dining
Ideal For: Business travelers and families

2. Novotel Jubail

Novotel Jubail is known for its contemporary design and convenient location. The hotel features spacious rooms, conference facilities, and leisure amenities, making it ideal for business trips and short stays.

Highlights: Conference rooms, fitness center, restaurants
Ideal For: Business travelers

3. Coral Jubail Hotel

Coral Jubail offers a blend of comfort and affordability. Its central location allows easy access to shopping centers and industrial areas, making it a convenient option for travelers.

Highlights: Free Wi-Fi, restaurant, business center
Ideal For: Mid-range travelers

4. Radisson Blu Hotel Jubail

Radisson Blu combines modern elegance with excellent service. The hotel has stylish rooms, a rooftop terrace, and multiple dining options. Its facilities cater to both business and leisure visitors.

Highlights: Rooftop terrace, meeting rooms, fitness center
Ideal For: Business and leisure travelers

5. Holiday Inn Jubail

Holiday Inn offers comfortable rooms and family-friendly amenities. It’s well-located for tourists and business travelers seeking a reliable hotel experience.

Highlights: Outdoor pool, restaurant, conference facilities
Ideal For: Families and business travelers

6. Al Bilad Hotel

Al Bilad Hotel is a budget-friendly option with clean rooms and essential amenities. It’s suitable for travelers who want a simple yet comfortable stay in Jubail.

Highlights: Free parking, Wi-Fi, central location
Ideal For: Budget-conscious travelers

7. Tulip Inn Jubail

Tulip Inn combines convenience with good service. The hotel provides comfortable rooms, meeting spaces, and easy access to the city’s main attractions.

Highlights: Restaurant, meeting rooms, fitness center
Ideal For: Business travelers and short-term visitors

8. Marina Jubail Hotel

Marina Jubail Hotel offers cozy accommodations with a focus on comfort. Its central location and modern amenities make it ideal for leisure and business travelers alike.

Highlights: On-site dining, Wi-Fi, fitness facilities
Ideal For: Mid-range travelers

9. Jubail Plaza Hotel

Jubail Plaza Hotel is a comfortable hotel offering clean rooms, friendly service, and essential amenities. Its location is convenient for both business and tourist visits.

Highlights: Free breakfast, parking, Wi-Fi
Ideal For: Families and business travelers

10. Al Yamama Hotel

Al Yamama Hotel is a simple and affordable option, perfect for short stays. Guests appreciate its friendly service and proximity to shopping and industrial areas.

Highlights: Free Wi-Fi, breakfast, central location
Ideal For: Budget-conscious travelers

FAQ – Hotels in Jubail

Q1. Which hotel is best for luxury stays in Jubail?

Mövenpick Hotel Jubail and Radisson Blu Hotel offer luxurious rooms, premium services, and excellent amenities.

Q2. Are there family-friendly hotels in Jubail?

Yes, Holiday Inn Jubail and Mövenpick Hotel are great options for families with kids and spacious rooms.

Q3. Which hotels are best for business travelers?

Novotel Jubail, Radisson Blu, and Tulip Inn offer conference facilities, business centers, and convenient locations.

Q4. Are there budget-friendly hotels in Jubail?

Al Bilad Hotel and Al Yamama Hotel are affordable options with clean rooms and essential amenities.

Q5. Do any hotels in Jubail have pools or fitness centers?

Yes, Mövenpick Hotel, Radisson Blu, and Holiday Inn offer pools and fitness facilities for guests.

Final Words

Jubail provides a wide variety of hotels for every type of traveler. Whether you prefer luxury with stunning views, mid-range comfort, or budget-friendly stays, these top 10 best hotels in Jubail ensure convenience, comfort, and a pleasant stay. From Mövenpick and Radisson Blu to simple options like Al Bilad Hotel, your visit to Jubail can be both productive and enjoyable.
