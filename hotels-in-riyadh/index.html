Top 10 Hotels to Stay in Riyadh 2025 (Updated)

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is the capital of Saudi Arabia. It is a city of business, culture, and modern attractions. Every year, millions of visitors come here for work, shopping, or tourism. Choosing the right hotel is important to enjoy your stay.

In Riyadh, you will find luxury hotels, budget stays, and family-friendly options. Many hotels are close to malls, cultural sites, and business districts. Whether you are a business traveler or a tourist, Riyadh has a hotel for you.

Here are the Top 10 Hotels to stay in Riyadh in 2025

1. Four Seasons Hotel Riyadh at Kingdom Centre

The Four Seasons Hotel Riyadh is one of the most luxurious hotels in the city. It is located inside the Kingdom Centre Tower, a famous landmark of Riyadh.

The hotel offers spacious rooms with modern design and beautiful city views. Guests can enjoy world-class dining, a spa, and a large fitness center. There are also tennis and squash courts for sports lovers.

One of the best parts is the location. You can easily access the Sky Bridge and the shopping mall inside the tower. The service is top-class, making it perfect for business leaders, families, and luxury travelers.

2. The Ritz-Carlton Riyadh

The Ritz-Carlton Riyadh is known for its royal style. The hotel looks like a palace, with grand halls, fountains, and marble interiors.

It offers over 490 rooms and suites. The rooms are large, elegant, and filled with luxury details. Guests can enjoy fine dining, a cigar lounge, and a world-class spa. The hotel also has indoor pools and fitness areas.

Many government events and international conferences are held here. This makes the hotel popular among diplomats and business leaders. If you want to experience true luxury in Riyadh, The Ritz-Carlton is the place.

3. Hyatt Regency Riyadh Olaya

The Hyatt Regency Riyadh Olaya is located in the business district of the city. It is a modern hotel perfect for business and leisure travelers.

The rooms are stylish with great views of the Riyadh skyline. The hotel offers several restaurants serving Middle Eastern and international food. There is also a spa, indoor pool, and fitness center.

Its central location makes it easy to reach shopping malls, offices, and tourist attractions. For those looking for comfort and convenience in Riyadh, Hyatt Regency is a strong choice.

4. Narcissus Hotel & Spa Riyadh

The Narcissus Hotel & Spa is a five-star luxury hotel in downtown Riyadh. Its design mixes classic elegance with modern comfort.

The hotel offers suites with lavish interiors, marble bathrooms, and high-end facilities. The spa is one of the best in the city, offering a relaxing experience after a busy day. Guests can also enjoy indoor pools, gyms, and excellent dining.

Its location in Olaya makes it close to top business areas and shopping centers. For luxury travelers who want comfort with a touch of glamour, Narcissus Hotel is perfect.

5. Fairmont Riyadh

The Fairmont Riyadh is located near the King Khalid International Airport, making it ideal for travelers. It is a new and stylish luxury hotel.

The hotel has modern rooms, business lounges, and several restaurants. Guests can relax in the spa, swim in the indoor pool, or enjoy fitness facilities.

The Fairmont is also popular for events and weddings. Its large ballrooms and meeting halls are among the best in Riyadh. For travelers who need luxury close to the airport, Fairmont Riyadh is the right choice.

6. Radisson Blu Hotel, Riyadh

The Radisson Blu Hotel is one of the most trusted names in Riyadh. It combines comfort, style, and affordability.

The rooms are modern and well-equipped. The hotel offers dining options with international cuisine, fitness centers, and a spa. Business travelers like its meeting rooms and event halls.

Its central location makes it easy to reach Riyadh’s attractions and shopping areas. Radisson Blu is a perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers looking for comfort at a good price.

7. Shaza Riyadh

The Shaza Riyadh is a unique hotel inspired by Arabian and Andalusian design. It offers a cultural and elegant stay.

The hotel’s rooms and suites are decorated with traditional touches and modern facilities. Guests can enjoy the wellness spa, pool, and gourmet restaurants.

It is located near King Abdullah Park, a popular tourist attraction. This makes it great for families and tourists who want both comfort and culture. Shaza Riyadh is different from typical hotels – it offers a true Middle Eastern experience.

8. Aloft Riyadh

The Aloft Riyadh is a trendy hotel designed for young and modern travelers. It is part of Marriott’s lifestyle brand.

The hotel has bright, stylish rooms with modern technology. It offers a pool, gym, and several dining options. The atmosphere is casual and creative, making it popular among young professionals.

Its central location makes it easy to explore the city. For those looking for a budget-friendly yet stylish stay, Aloft Riyadh is a great choice.

9. Fraser Suites Riyadh

The Fraser Suites Riyadh is perfect for long stays and families. It offers serviced apartments with hotel facilities.

Each suite has a kitchen, living area, and modern design. Guests can also enjoy the swimming pool, spa, gym, and rooftop terrace.

Located in the city center, it is close to shopping malls and business districts. For families or business travelers who want space and comfort, Fraser Suites is ideal.

10. InterContinental Riyadh

The InterContinental Riyadh is one of the oldest luxury hotels in the city. It combines modern comfort with traditional Saudi hospitality.

The hotel has large rooms, golf courses, swimming pools, and fine dining restaurants. Guests can enjoy international cuisine and local dishes.

Its location is central, making it easy to explore Riyadh’s attractions. For travelers who want a mix of history, comfort, and international service, InterContinental Riyadh is a great choice.

FAQs About Hotels in Riyadh

What is the best area to stay in Riyadh?

Olaya and King Fahd Road are the best for tourists. They are central and close to shopping malls and attractions.

Are there luxury hotels near Riyadh Airport?

Yes. The Fairmont Riyadh is near the airport and offers five-star service.

Can families find good hotels in Riyadh?

Yes. Hotels like Fraser Suites Riyadh and Shaza Riyadh are great for families.

Do Riyadh hotels allow unmarried couples?

Most international hotels require proof of marriage. Rules are becoming more flexible, but it is best to check with the hotel before booking.

What is the average price of hotels in Riyadh?

Luxury hotels cost between $200–$500 per night. Mid-range hotels are around $80–$150 per night. Budget hotels can be found for less.

Final Words

Riyadh offers hotels for every type of traveler. From luxury stays like the Four Seasons and Ritz-Carlton, to modern business hotels like Hyatt Regency, to cultural gems like Shaza Riyadh. Families can enjoy the comfort of Fraser Suites, while young travelers can stay in stylish hotels like Aloft Riyadh.

In 2025, Riyadh continues to grow as a global city. These hotels make your trip more comfortable, whether you come for business, tourism, or family travel.
