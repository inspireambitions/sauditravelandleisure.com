Top 10 Hotels to Stay in Tabuk 2025 Updated

2025-08-26

Tabuk, a vibrant city in northwestern Saudi Arabia, is a hub for business, tourism, and historical exploration. With its desert landscapes, modern infrastructure, and proximity to the Red Sea coast, Tabuk attracts travelers of all kinds. Choosing the right hotel can make your visit comfortable and memorable.

From luxury accommodations to budget-friendly options, Tabuk offers a variety of hotels. This guide highlights the top 10 hotels to stay in Tabuk in 2025, ensuring comfort, convenience, and value.

Best Hotels In Tabuk

1. Hilton Garden Inn Tabuk

Hilton Garden Inn is a top-rated 4-star hotel offering modern comforts and exceptional service. Rooms are spacious, and facilities include a fitness center, a spa, and an on-site restaurant. Its central location makes it convenient for both business and leisure travelers.

Highlights: Central location, spa, fitness center, on-site dining
Ideal For: Luxury and business travelers

2. Best Western Plus Tabuk City Center

Known for its excellent service and comfortable rooms, Best Western Plus is a popular choice for travelers seeking quality and convenience. Amenities include free Wi-Fi, breakfast, and a business center.

Highlights: Free breakfast, business facilities, central location
Ideal For: Mid-range travelers and business visitors

3. Grand Millennium Tabuk

Grand Millennium offers luxurious accommodations with modern interiors, a spa, and multiple dining options. Guests can enjoy relaxing facilities while exploring the city’s attractions.

Highlights: Spa, fine dining, elegant rooms
Ideal For: Luxury travelers and couples

4. Skyline Tabuk Hotel

Skyline Hotel features contemporary rooms, a steam room, and a 24-hour business center. Its modern design and convenient location make it ideal for business and leisure travelers alike.

Highlights: Modern amenities, business center, spa facilities
Ideal For: Business travelers and short-stay visitors

5. Holiday Inn Tabuk by IHG

Holiday Inn provides comfortable accommodations and family-friendly facilities. The hotel features an outdoor pool, fitness center, and meeting rooms, making it suitable for business or vacation stays.

Highlights: Pool, gym, meeting rooms, family-friendly
Ideal For: Families and business travelers

6. Swiss In Tabuk Hotel

Swiss In combines comfort and style with well-furnished rooms and friendly service. Guests enjoy easy access to local shops, restaurants, and attractions.

Highlights: Stylish rooms, central location, excellent service
Ideal For: Mid-range travelers

7. Five Seasons Hotel & Spa

A boutique hotel that emphasizes personalized service and relaxation. Guests can enjoy spa treatments, a peaceful environment, and easy access to the city’s business districts.

Highlights: Spa, boutique experience, personalized service
Ideal For: Couples and business travelers

8. Larichy Hotel

Larichy Hotel is a budget-friendly option with clean rooms and basic amenities. It is perfect for short stays and travelers who want comfort without spending too much.

Highlights: Affordable rates, clean rooms, central location
Ideal For: Budget-conscious travelers

9. Mena Hotel Tabuk

Mena Hotel offers comfortable rooms and essential amenities. Its convenient location makes it easy to explore the city or attend business meetings.

Highlights: Comfortable rooms, central location, practical amenities
Ideal For: Business travelers and short-term visitors

10. Ewaa Express Hotel – Tabuk

Ewaa Express is a simple and practical hotel for travelers looking for basic accommodation. Guests appreciate its convenient location and friendly service.

Highlights: Affordable, central, basic amenities
Ideal For: Budget travelers and short stays

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which hotel in Tabuk is best for luxury stays?

Hilton Garden Inn Tabuk and Grand Millennium Tabuk are top choices for luxury travelers, offering modern amenities, spa services, and fine dining.

Are there family-friendly hotels in Tabuk?

Yes, Holiday Inn Tabuk and Hilton Garden Inn provide family-friendly facilities and comfortable accommodations for all ages.

Which hotels are ideal for business travelers?

Skyline Tabuk Hotel, Holiday Inn Tabuk, and Hilton Garden Inn offer business centers, meeting rooms, and convenient locations.

Are there budget-friendly hotels in Tabuk?

Yes, Larichy Hotel and Ewaa Express Hotel offer affordable stays without compromising basic comfort.

Do Tabuk hotels provide fitness or spa facilities?

Yes, Hilton Garden Inn, Grand Millennium, and Five Seasons Hotel & Spa offer gyms, pools, and spa services.

Final Words

Tabuk provides a variety of hotels suitable for every traveler, from luxury seekers to budget-conscious visitors. Options like Hilton Garden Inn Tabuk and Grand Millennium offer premium comfort, while Larichy Hotel and Ewaa Express are ideal for travelers on a budget. Selecting the right hotel ensures a comfortable and enjoyable stay while exploring Tabuk’s attractions, business hubs, and beautiful landscapes.
