Top 10 Amazing Hotels to Stay in Taif for a Memorable Trip

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Taif, often called the “City of Roses,” is a beautiful mountain city in Saudi Arabia known for its cool climate, rose gardens, and historical charm.

Nestled in the Sarawat Mountains, it’s a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking relaxation, nature, and cultural experiences.

To make your stay unforgettable, choosing the right accommodation is essential. In this guide, we bring you the Top 10 Amazing Hotels to Stay in Taif, offering everything from luxury experiences to budget-friendly comfort.

Best Amazing Hotels In Taif

1. InterContinental Taif

If luxury is what you seek, InterContinental Taif is among the best options. Located near the Shafa Mountains, the hotel offers elegant rooms, modern amenities, and breathtaking views. Guests can enjoy fine dining, a fitness center, and top-notch hospitality, making it perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

2. Awaliv International Hotel

This high-rise hotel is known for its stylish interiors and city views. Awaliv International features spacious rooms, an indoor pool, spa services, and multiple dining options. Its central location makes it convenient for exploring Taif’s main attractions.

3. Le Méridien Al Hada

Situated in the Al Hada area, this luxury hotel offers panoramic mountain views. Le Méridien Al Hada is famous for its beautiful outdoor pool, lush gardens, and relaxing environment. It’s a great choice for families or couples looking for a peaceful getaway.

4. Iridium Hotel Taif

A modern gem in Taif, Iridium Hotel is loved for its contemporary design and high-end amenities. Guests can enjoy stylish rooms, an on-site restaurant, spa, and excellent service. It’s ideal for travelers who prefer a modern yet comfortable stay.

5. Boudl Taif

Boudl is a well-known Saudi hotel chain, and its Taif branch is popular with families. The hotel offers apartment-style accommodation with kitchen facilities, making it great for longer stays. It’s budget-friendly yet provides good service and comfort.

6. Ramada by Wyndham Al Hada

Overlooking the Al Hada mountains, this hotel offers a mix of comfort and scenic views. Ramada features cozy rooms, restaurants, and recreational activities. Its location makes it a favorite for visitors who want to enjoy Taif’s cooler climate.

7. OYO 176 Hotel Safari Al Hada

For travelers on a budget, OYO 176 Hotel Safari Al Hada offers affordable accommodation without compromising on basic comfort. The hotel is simple, clean, and conveniently located for quick access to tourist attractions.

8. Platinum Al Biea

This hotel is known for its elegant interiors and relaxing atmosphere. Platinum Al Biea offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and an in-house restaurant. It’s a mid-range option that balances price and quality.

9. Al Muhaidb Resort Al Hada

Perfect for families and groups, this resort offers villa-style accommodation. With private spaces, kitchens, and recreational facilities, Al Muhaidb Resort gives guests a homely yet luxurious experience.

10. Rohara Hotel Apartments

A great option for extended stays, Rohara Hotel Apartments provide well-furnished apartments with kitchens and living areas. It’s perfect for families or travelers looking for more independence during their stay in Taif.

FAQ About Hotels In Taif

Q1. What is the best area to stay in Taif?

The Al Hada area is popular for its cool climate and scenic mountain views, while the city center is best for easy access to attractions.

Q2. Are there budget-friendly hotels in Taif?

Yes, options like OYO 176 Hotel Safari Al Hada and Boudl Taif provide affordable yet comfortable stays.

Q3. Which hotels in Taif are good for families?

Hotels like Boudl Taif and Al Muhaidb Resort Al Hada are great for families, as they offer apartment-style accommodations.

Q4. Do Taif hotels offer mountain views?

Yes, several hotels such as Le Méridien Al Hada and Ramada by Wyndham Al Hada provide stunning mountain views.

Q5. What is the best luxury hotel in Taif?

InterContinental Taif and Iridium Hotel are among the top luxury options with excellent services and amenities.

Final Words

Taif is a city full of charm, history, and natural beauty. Whether you’re visiting to explore the rose gardens, enjoy the cool mountain air, or discover its cultural heritage, choosing the right accommodation will make your journey memorable. From luxury hotels like InterContinental Taif to budget-friendly stays like OYO 176 Hotel Safari Al Hada, this list has something for every type of traveler.

Plan your next trip to Taif and enjoy a stay that matches your style and budget.
