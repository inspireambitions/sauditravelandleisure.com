Top 10 Hotels to Stay in Yanbu 2025 for Comfort and Convenience

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Yanbu, a picturesque coastal city in Saudi Arabia’s Al Madinah Province, is known for its beautiful Red Sea coastline, industrial hubs, and modern infrastructure. Whether you’re visiting for business, leisure, or a family vacation, selecting the right hotel is key to a smooth and enjoyable stay.

Best Hotels in Yanbu

1. Kempinski Hotel & Resort Sariya Yanbu

Kempinski Sariya is a world-class beachfront resort offering an unparalleled luxury experience. Guests can relax on the private beach, unwind in the full-service spa, and enjoy multiple dining options with stunning Red Sea views. Perfect for honeymooners or travelers looking for a lavish stay.

Highlights: Private beach, spa, fine dining, Red Sea views
Ideal For: Luxury travelers and couples

2. Platinum Hotel Yanbu

Located in the heart of Yanbu, Platinum Hotel provides modern, stylish accommodations. Guests enjoy spacious rooms, an outdoor pool, and a fully equipped fitness center. Its central location makes exploring the city’s attractions simple and convenient.

Highlights: Outdoor pool, gym, central location
Ideal For: Mid-range travelers

3. Radisson Blu Hotel Yanbu

Radisson Blu offers contemporary rooms with panoramic sea views. The hotel features a rooftop terrace, fitness facilities, and multiple restaurants, making it ideal for business travelers and vacationers alike.

Highlights: Rooftop terrace, fitness center, sea-view rooms
Ideal For: Business and leisure travelers

4. YM Resort

YM Resort is perfect for families seeking comfort and entertainment. Featuring villa-style accommodations with private pools, the resort offers recreational activities for all ages, ensuring a memorable stay for both adults and children.

Highlights: Private pools, family-friendly activities, beach access
Ideal For: Families and group travelers

5. Holiday Inn Yanbu

Holiday Inn Yanbu blends comfort with convenience. With modern rooms, a swimming pool, and fitness facilities, it’s suitable for both short stays and longer visits. Its friendly staff ensures a welcoming experience for all guests.

Highlights: Pool, gym, family-friendly, business amenities
Ideal For: Families and business travelers

6. Canary Beach Hotel

Canary Beach Hotel provides cozy accommodations with easy access to Yanbu Lakes and the coastline. Its private beach area is perfect for relaxation, while the rooms offer comfort at an affordable rate.

Highlights: Beach access, comfortable rooms, central location
Ideal For: Leisure travelers and couples

7. Radwa Ramada Resort

Radwa Ramada offers a balance between relaxation and convenience. Guests can enjoy comfortable rooms, a pool, and recreational activities, all within walking distance of the city’s main attractions.

Highlights: Pool, recreational activities, central location
Ideal For: Families and couples

8. Al Yamama Al Mumayaz Hotel Apartments

Perfect for long-term stays, Al Yamama Al Mumayaz provides fully furnished apartments with kitchens. The hotel offers a homely atmosphere while maintaining modern amenities, ideal for travelers needing independence during their stay.

Highlights: Kitchen facilities, spacious apartments, central location
Ideal For: Extended stays and families

9. Ibis Yanbu

Ibis Yanbu is an excellent budget-friendly option. It offers clean, modern rooms with essential amenities. Its convenient location makes it easy to explore the city without breaking the bank.

Highlights: Affordable, modern rooms, convenient location
Ideal For: Budget-conscious travelers

10. Al Higgi Hotel

Al Higgi Hotel is a simple, comfortable hotel located near the city center. Guests can enjoy essential amenities and easy access to shopping, dining, and business areas, making it a practical choice for short stays.

Highlights: Central location, essential amenities, friendly staff
Ideal For: Budget and short-term visitors

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Which hotel in Yanbu is best for luxury travelers?

Kempinski Hotel & Resort Sariya Yanbu provides unmatched luxury with private beaches, spa facilities, and fine dining.

Are there family-friendly hotels in Yanbu?

Yes, YM Resort and Holiday Inn Yanbu are ideal for families with children-friendly activities and spacious accommodations.

Which hotels are suitable for business travelers?

Radisson Blu Yanbu and Holiday Inn Yanbu provide business centers, meeting rooms, and convenient locations for corporate stays.

Are there budget-friendly hotels in Yanbu?

Ibis Yanbu and Al Higgi Hotel offer comfortable stays at an affordable price without compromising on quality.

Which hotels in Yanbu offer beach access?

Kempinski Hotel & Resort Sariya Yanbu, Radisson Blu Yanbu, and Canary Beach Hotel provide private beach access for guests.

Final Thoughts

Yanbu offers a wide variety of hotels suitable for every type of traveler. From luxurious beachfront resorts like Kempinski Hotel & Resort Sariya Yanbu to affordable options like Ibis Yanbu, every visitor can find accommodations that fit their needs. By selecting the right hotel, you can enjoy the city’s stunning coastline, modern amenities, and welcoming atmosphere, making your trip truly memorable.
