Driving Realities and Road Rules

Driving in Qatar is straightforward: the roads are modern and signage is bilingual (Arabic and English). International driving permits are accepted, and rental cars are plentiful. Fuel is affordable relative to global standards. However, be mindful of local driving habits: keep to speed limits, be wary of sudden lane changes in urban areas, and remember that desert driving away from marked roads requires a vehicle equipped for sand and ideally a driver with local experience.

Given the countryГўВҖВҷs size, domestic flights are unnecessary for most itineraries.
