Will my Saudi driving license be accepted in Kuwait?

GCC driving licenses are usually accepted across GCC countries. If you hold a non-GCC license, carry an International Driving Permit or an Arabic translation as required. Verify with the Kuwaiti traffic authorities or your embassy before departure.

Are there petrol stations and rest stops along the way?

Yesâmajor highways have regular petrol stations, convenience stores, and rest areas. Fuel is readily available; however, fill up before you enter long remote stretches.

What are the common reasons for border delays?
