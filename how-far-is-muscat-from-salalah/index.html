FAQ

How long does it take to drive non-stop from Muscat to Salalah?

Is it safe to drive from Muscat to Salalah at night?

Night driving on long stretches is discouraged because of unlit roads, possible animal crossings, and reduced support services. Plan to drive during daylight and schedule overnight stops if needed.

What is the best time of year to visit Salalah?

If you want the unique green season, visit during Khareef (June芒聙聯September). For drier, quieter travel with fewer visitors, choose the cooler months outside Khareef.

Choose to fly if time is limited and you prefer predictability. Choose to drive if you prioritize scenic variety, flexible stops, and a deeper sense of place. Consider flying one way and driving the other to combine benefits.
