Safety, Permits, and Cultural Etiquette

General safety advice

Saudi highways are well-maintained, but long-distance driving requires planning. Keep emergency numbers handy, inform someone of your intended route, and maintain good hydration and rest. Avoid spontaneous detours into unmarked desert tracks without a capable 4x4 and reliable communications.

Cultural etiquette to observe

Respect for local customs is essential. Dress modestly, be courteous in public spaces, and seek permission when photographing people, particularly in rural or private contexts. Public behavior that is respectful of local norms will yield positive interactions and smoother travel.

Permits and restricted areas

Some regions in the Kingdom require permits for visitation or restricted access for security reasons. Confirm access to archaeological or military-proximate areas before you arrive. If you are planning to travel toward border regions, official clearances may be required.
