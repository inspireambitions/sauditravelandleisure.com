Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long should I stay in Dubai for my first visit?
A: For a first-time visit, 5â7 full days gives a comfortable balance: one or two days for main city highlights (Downtown, Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall), a day for a desert safari, a day for the Marina/Jumeirah beach district, and an extra day for shopping or a day trip to Abu Dhabi.

For lower overall trip costs with tolerable weather, consider late spring or the shoulder weeks outside major holidays and events. For the best combination of comfort and deals, avoid major international events and local holidays.
