Top 14 Best Indian Restaurants in Abha (2025 Updated)

Updated August 28, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Abha, the capital of the Asir region in Saudi Arabia, is not only famous for its cool weather, green mountains, and cultural charm but also for its diverse food scene.

For Indian food lovers, Abha offers plenty of restaurants serving everything from rich Mughlai curries to spicy biryanis and comforting vegetarian dishes.

Whether you’re a tourist or a resident, here are the 14 best Indian restaurants in Abha—complete with pros, cons, pricing, and must-try dishes.

How to Choose the Best Indian Restaurant in Abha

Authenticity & Taste

Look for spots specializing in regional Indian food—North Indian tandoori dishes or South Indian dosas.

Location & Accessibility

Abha has restaurants near key areas like Al Muftaha Village, Abha Dam, and popular hotels—choose based on convenience.

Price Range & Value

Most Indian restaurants in Abha range between SAR 15–40 per person.

Ambience & Dining Style

Families may prefer full-service restaurants, while students and workers often go for budget eateries.

Variety & Dietary Needs

If you’re vegetarian or need halal-certified dishes, most Indian restaurants in Abha cater to you.

Top 14 Best Indian Restaurants in Abha

1. Zaika Indian Restaurant Abha

Quick Answer: A popular fine-dining Indian restaurant serving Mughlai gravies and grilled kebabs.

  • Pros: Elegant ambience, family-friendly, wide menu
  • Cons: Slightly more expensive than casual places
  • Price Range: SAR 30–45
  • Best For: Family dinners and celebrations
  • Signature Dish: Mutton Rogan Josh

2. Swaad Restaurant Abha

Quick Answer: A favorite among locals for homestyle Indian curries and thalis.

  • Pros: Comfort food, affordable pricing
  • Cons: Small dining space
  • Price Range: SAR 15–25
  • Best For: Everyday meals, comfort food cravings
  • Signature Dish: Rajma Chawal

3. Curry House Abha

Quick Answer: Known for rich curries and vegetarian options, this is a top pick for Indian expats.

  • Pros: Great vegetarian menu, attentive staff
  • Cons: Not ideal for large groups
  • Price Range: SAR 20–30
  • Best For: Vegetarians and small families
  • Signature Dish: Paneer Butter Masala

4. Hyderabad Biryani House

Quick Answer: If you love biryani, this is the go-to restaurant in Abha.

  • Pros: Authentic Hyderabadi biryani, budget-friendly
  • Cons: Menu focused mainly on biryani
  • Price Range: SAR 15–25
  • Best For: Quick biryani cravings
  • Signature Dish: Chicken Dum Biryani

5. Indian Palace Abha

Quick Answer: A premium spot offering North and South Indian cuisine with an elegant setting.

  • Pros: Beautiful interiors, diverse menu
  • Cons: Higher price point
  • Price Range: SAR 35–50
  • Best For: Special occasions and fine dining
  • Signature Dish: Royal Vegetarian Thali

6. Spice Hub Abha

Quick Answer: Best known for South Indian delicacies like dosa, idli, and sambar.

  • Pros: Authentic South Indian taste, budget-friendly
  • Cons: Small seating area
  • Price Range: SAR 15–25
  • Best For: Vegetarian meals and light snacks
  • Signature Dish: Masala Dosa

7. Mughal Darbar Abha

Quick Answer: A royal Mughlai restaurant serving rich gravies, kebabs, and festive platters.

  • Pros: Authentic Mughlai cuisine, large portions
  • Cons: Rich food may feel heavy for some
  • Price Range: SAR 30–40
  • Best For: Group dinners and festive occasions
  • Signature Dish: Chicken Korma

8. Tandoori Nights Abha

Quick Answer: Perfect for grilled tandoori dishes and spicy curries.

  • Pros: Excellent kebabs, fresh naan
  • Cons: Service can be slow during peak hours
  • Price Range: SAR 20–35
  • Best For: Evening meals with friends
  • Signature Dish: Tandoori Chicken

9. Royal Tandoor Abha

Quick Answer: Blends traditional tandoor cooking with modern flavors.

  • Pros: Fresh bread, flavorful kebabs
  • Cons: Crowded during weekends
  • Price Range: SAR 25–35
  • Best For: Family dining
  • Signature Dish: Seekh Kebab

10. Indian Zaika Express Abha

Quick Answer: A budget-friendly eatery for everyday Indian food.

  • Pros: Affordable, quick service
  • Cons: Limited seating
  • Price Range: SAR 10–20
  • Best For: Solo diners and students
  • Signature Dish: Dal Tadka with Roti

11. Punjab Darbar Abha

Quick Answer: Famous for Punjabi curries and lassi at affordable rates.

  • Pros: Flavorful Punjabi dishes, affordable
  • Cons: Simple interiors
  • Price Range: SAR 15–25
  • Best For: Quick family dinners
  • Signature Dish: Butter Chicken

12. Taste of India Abha

Quick Answer: Offers a mix of North and South Indian cuisines with homestyle flavors.

  • Pros: Balanced menu, friendly staff
  • Cons: Can be crowded at night
  • Price Range: SAR 20–30
  • Best For: Families and mixed groups
  • Signature Dish: Chicken Curry with Naan

13. Deccan Spice Abha

Quick Answer: Known for spicy Andhra and Hyderabadi recipes.

  • Pros: Spicy, authentic taste, good portions
  • Cons: Too spicy for mild eaters
  • Price Range: SAR 20–30
  • Best For: Spice lovers
  • Signature Dish: Andhra Chicken Curry

14. Al Hind Restaurant

Quick Answer: A casual spot serving affordable Indian curries and biryanis.

  • Pros: Pocket-friendly, homely meals
  • Cons: Limited variety compared to fine-dining restaurants
  • Price Range: SAR 12–20
  • Best For: Budget travelers and quick meals
  • Signature Dish: Chicken Curry

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Indian restaurant in Abha is best for biryani?

Hyderabad Biryani House is the top choice for authentic Hyderabadi dum biryani at budget prices.

Where can I find South Indian food in Abha?

Spice Hub offers dosas, idlis, and sambar that taste close to authentic South Indian cooking.

What is the average cost of Indian food in Abha?

Casual restaurants cost SAR 15–25 per person, while fine dining ranges from SAR 30–50.

Which restaurant is best for vegetarian food in Abha?

Curry House and Indian Palace are the best for vegetarian meals and thalis.

Final Words

Abha is not only a scenic hill city but also a place where you can enjoy authentic Indian food. From budget eateries like Swaad and Zaika Express to fine-dining spots like Indian Palace and Zaika Restaurant, there’s something for everyone. Whether you’re a spice lover or a vegetarian, these top 14 Indian restaurants in Abha are sure to satisfy your cravings.
