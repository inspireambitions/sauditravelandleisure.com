Top 14 Best Indian Restaurants in Dammam (2025 Updated)

2025-08-28

Dammam, a vibrant port city in Saudi Arabia’s Eastern Province, boasts an exciting and rich Indian food scene—from homestyle curries to flavorful South Indian specialties and biryanis.

Whether you’re a food-loving local or a visitor craving authentic flavors, here are the 14 best Indian restaurants in Dammam, complete with pros, cons, price ranges, and standout dishes.

How to Choose the Right Indian Restaurant in Dammam

  • Cuisine Authenticity – Look for restaurants offering distinct regional dishes like Kerala meals or Tamil specialties.
  • Location & Convenience – Many are centrally located near key areas like Al Hamra and Al Souq.
  • Budget Fit – Ranges vary from affordable casual dining to mid-range family-friendly spots.
  • Ambience & Seating – Some offer private family sections; others are cozy and casual.
  • Menu Variety – Preference for vegetarian selections or spicy fare? Choose accordingly.

Top 14 Indian Restaurants in Dammam

1. Green Plate Indian Family Restaurant

Quick Answer: A beloved family spot noted for its Kerala-style meals, delivering authentic South Indian flavors with homely vibes.

  • Pros: Authentic taste, family-friendly, high ratings (~4.5)
  • Cons: Occasionally busy at peak hours
  • Price Range: Moderate
  • Best For: Families, South Indian cuisine lovers

2. Anjappar Indian Restaurant

Quick Answer: Trusted for flavorful Tamilian dishes like dosa and Chettinad curries—clean ambiance and attentive staff.

  • Pros: Exceptional South Indian authenticity, clean setting
  • Cons: Menu focused on South Indian only
  • Price Range: Moderate
  • Best For: Tamilian cuisine enthusiasts

3. Shezan Restaurant

Quick Answer: A well-established Indian restaurant known for quality food and traditional dishes.

  • Pros: Trusted brand, consistent quality
  • Cons: Slightly pricier than alternatives
  • Price Range: Mid to high
  • Best For: Fine casual meals, large menus

4. Taj Zadi (Tajzadi)

Quick Answer: Antique-themed restaurant offering spicy curries, biryani, and a diverse menu—family-friendly ambiance.

  • Pros: Rich menu, warm décor, affordable
  • Cons: Decor style may not suit all tastes
  • Price Range: Moderate
  • Best For: Families seeking variety

5. Horizon Restaurant

Quick Answer: Quiet, clean, and delicious—great for those who prefer calm dining and tasty Indian drinks.

  • Pros: Clean, friendly, good beverages, reasonable price
  • Cons: Less decorative setting
  • Price Range: Budget to moderate
  • Best For: Quiet dining, casual meals

6. Masala House Restaurant

Quick Answer: Modern Indian/Pakistani eatery, ideal for families—known for flavorful biryanis, chaat, and catering services.

7. Royal Malabar Restaurant

Quick Answer: Kerala-style restaurant praised for flavorful South and North Indian dishes like appam, prawn, and mutton specialties.

  • Pros: Authentic Kerala menu, family-friendly, fresh ingredients
  • Cons: Decor is functional
  • Price Range: Moderate
  • Best For: Authentic Kerala cuisine

8. Holidays Restaurant (Holiday(s) Restaurant)

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Indian restaurant in Dammam serves the most authentic Kerala dishes?

Try Green Plate Indian Family Restaurant or Royal Malabar—both receive praise for their genuine Kerala specialties like appam and prawn curry.

Best for South Indian cuisine?

Anjappar is widely praised for its Chettinad curries, dosa, and authentic South Indian ambiance.

Which spot offers great value with family-friendly portions?

Indian Tanoor Restaurant stands out for its large servings, clean setting, and helpful staff.

Any recommendation for mixed-cuisine or mall proximity?

Oceana and Copper Chandni offer easy access and diverse menus, perfect for groups or after-shopping meals.
