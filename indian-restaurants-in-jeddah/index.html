Top 14 Best Indian Restaurants in Jeddah (2025 Updated)

Updated August 27, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

If you’re craving the rich flavors of India while in Saudi Arabia, Jeddah is the perfect city to explore.

From traditional North Indian curries to South Indian dosas and fusion dishes, the city offers a wide variety of Indian dining experiences.

Whether you want fine dining, budget-friendly meals, or late-night snacks, these top 14 Indian restaurants in Jeddah will satisfy every food lover.

This updated 2025 guide highlights the best Indian restaurants in Jeddah with insights on prices, must-try dishes, and what each spot is best known for.

Top 14 Best Indian Restaurants in Jeddah

1. Copper Chandni

  • Pros: Elegant ambiance, wide variety of authentic North Indian dishes.
  • Cons: Slightly pricey during weekends.
  • Price Range: SAR 90–150 per person.
  • Best For: Family dinners and special occasions.

2. Zaika Indian Restaurant

  • Pros: Famous for biryani and kebabs, great service.
  • Cons: Small seating area gets crowded.
  • Price Range: SAR 70–120 per person.
  • Best For: Biryani lovers and casual dining.

3. Bharat Restaurant

  • Pros: Authentic taste, vegetarian and vegan options available.
  • Cons: Parking can be difficult.
  • Price Range: SAR 60–100 per person.
  • Best For: Vegetarian Indian food in Jeddah.

4. Saffron Lounge

  • Pros: Premium dining, stylish interiors, flavorful curries.
  • Cons: Higher on the price scale.
  • Price Range: SAR 120–200 per person.
  • Best For: Luxury Indian fine dining.

5. Maharaja by Vineet

  • Pros: Michelin-star chef, modern Indian cuisine, excellent service.
  • Cons: Expensive compared to others.
  • Price Range: SAR 180–250 per person.
  • Best For: Special occasions and gourmet Indian food.

6. Delhi Darbar

  • Pros: Affordable, homely taste, generous portions.
  • Cons: Basic interiors.
  • Price Range: SAR 40–80 per person.
  • Best For: Budget-friendly authentic Indian food.

7. Taste of India

  • Pros: Diverse menu with both South and North Indian dishes.
  • Cons: Service can be slow at peak hours.
  • Price Range: SAR 70–110 per person.
  • Best For: Families and mixed groups with varied preferences.

8. Hyderabad House

  • Pros: Famous Hyderabadi biryani, authentic spices.
  • Cons: Not much variety beyond biryani.
  • Price Range: SAR 50–90 per person.
  • Best For: Authentic Hyderabadi biryani fans.

9. Rangoli Restaurant

  • Pros: Stylish décor, wide vegetarian selection.
  • Cons: A little on the expensive side for portions.
  • Price Range: SAR 90–140 per person.
  • Best For: Couples and vegetarian Indian food.

10. Anjar Indian Restaurant

  • Pros: Great seafood curries, family-friendly.
  • Cons: Menu limited for vegetarians.
  • Price Range: SAR 80–120 per person.
  • Best For: Seafood lovers.

11. Ashoka Restaurant

  • Pros: Classic Indian recipes, casual dining atmosphere.
  • Cons: Interiors could use an update.
  • Price Range: SAR 50–100 per person.
  • Best For: Everyday Indian meals.

12. Spice Village

  • Pros: Bold flavors, good mix of Mughlai and Punjabi dishes.
  • Cons: Service can be inconsistent.
  • Price Range: SAR 80–130 per person.
  • Best For: Meat lovers and group dining.

13. Curry Tree

  • Pros: South Indian specialties like dosa and idli, quick service.
  • Cons: Limited seating.
  • Price Range: SAR 40–70 per person.
  • Best For: Quick vegetarian meals and South Indian dishes.

14. Biryani House Jeddah

  • Pros: Affordable, authentic biryanis, casual setup.
  • Cons: Crowded during weekends.
  • Price Range: SAR 30–60 per person.
  • Best For: Affordable and quick Indian biryani.

Final Words

Indian food in Jeddah is a true cultural experience, offering everything from luxury fine dining to budget-friendly family restaurants. Whether you’re searching for authentic biryani, vegetarian curries, or modern fusion flavors, these top Indian restaurants in Jeddah will surely impress you. If you’re visiting in 2025, make sure to add these dining spots to your foodie bucket list.

FAQs about Indian Restaurants in Jeddah

Q1. What is the most famous Indian restaurant in Jeddah?

Maharaja by Vineet and Copper Chandni are among the most famous for fine dining, while Zaika and Hyderabad House are popular for biryani.

Q2. Are there vegetarian Indian restaurants in Jeddah?

Yes, Bharat Restaurant, Curry Tree, and Rangoli are great for vegetarian options.

Q3. How much does Indian food cost in Jeddah?

On average, Indian meals cost between SAR 40–150 per person, depending on whether you choose casual dining or fine dining.

Q4. Which restaurant serves the best biryani in Jeddah?

Hyderabad House and Biryani House Jeddah are top choices for authentic biryani.

Q5. Do these restaurants offer delivery?

Yes, most of them are available on delivery apps like HungerStation, Jahez, and Mrsool.
