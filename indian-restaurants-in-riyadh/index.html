14 Top Best Indian Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is known for its diverse food scene. Indian cuisine is very popular here. From traditional dishes to modern fusion, the city has many options.

Whether you crave spicy curries, biryani, or tandoori dishes, Riyadh has something for every Indian food lover. Many restaurants offer authentic flavors prepared by skilled chefs.

14 Top Indian Restaurants in Riyadh You Must Try

1. Zafran Indian Bistro

Zafran is famous for its flavorful curries, biryanis, and tandoori dishes. The restaurant offers a modern dining experience with traditional Indian tastes. It is suitable for families and business dinners. The staff is friendly, and the menu has options for vegetarians and non-vegetarians.

2. Copper Chandni

Copper Chandni serves authentic Indian cuisine in an elegant setting. Its specialties include butter chicken, paneer tikka, and lamb rogan josh. The restaurant’s décor mixes modern and traditional Indian elements. It is a great place for celebrations and family gatherings.

3. Saffron Lounge

Saffron Lounge offers a premium Indian dining experience. Guests enjoy a mix of North and South Indian dishes. Signature dishes include chicken biryani, lamb curry, and naan bread. The restaurant is perfect for romantic dinners or business lunches.

4. Bombay Chowpatty

Bombay Chowpatty brings street food flavors to Riyadh. Its menu includes chaats, pav bhaji, and pani puri. The casual setting makes it perfect for friends and quick meals. It is one of the most popular places for authentic Indian street food.

5. Indya by Vineet

Indya by Vineet is a high-end Indian restaurant. It offers gourmet dishes with creative presentation. The chef focuses on modern Indian flavors with international influences. The restaurant is ideal for food enthusiasts seeking a luxurious dining experience.

After a luxurious meal at Indya by Vineet, you can also try some of the best Italian restaurants in Riyadh for another culinary experience

6. Curry House

Curry House is known for its rich curries, kebabs, and vegetarian options. The restaurant has a cozy and casual environment. Many locals and tourists visit for its authentic taste and reasonable prices.

7. Masala Art

Masala Art offers traditional Indian meals with fresh ingredients. Guests enjoy dishes like chicken tikka masala, paneer butter masala, and lamb biryani. The restaurant is family-friendly and also offers takeaway options.

8. Raju Omlet

Raju Omlet is a casual Indian café known for its breakfast and snacks. The menu includes omelets, parathas, and Indian-style sandwiches. It is a popular spot for quick meals and casual dining.

9. Bikanervala

Bikanervala is famous for its sweets, snacks, and North Indian cuisine. It serves chaat, samosas, and traditional Indian desserts. Families and tourists love its casual atmosphere and authentic flavors.

10. Indian Kitchen

Indian Kitchen offers home-style Indian cooking. Guests enjoy biryanis, curries, and fresh breads. The restaurant is simple yet delivers a rich and authentic taste. It is suitable for families and casual dinners.

11. Khyber

Khyber is a premium Indian restaurant in Riyadh. It specializes in North Indian and Mughlai dishes. Signature items include lamb korma, tandoori chicken, and naan. The elegant décor makes it perfect for formal dining.

12. Curry Leaf

Curry Leaf focuses on South Indian cuisine. It offers dosa, idli, sambar, and coconut chutney. The restaurant provides a cozy and traditional South Indian dining experience. Vegetarians often prefer this place.

13. Tandoori Nights

Tandoori Nights is known for its clay oven-cooked dishes. Guests enjoy kebabs, tandoori chicken, and naan bread. The restaurant is casual and great for family outings or group dinners.

14. Biryani House

Biryani House specializes in Indian rice dishes. Its biryanis are flavorful, spicy, and authentic. It also offers curries and vegetarian meals. The casual setting makes it ideal for quick and satisfying meals.

FAQs About Indian Restaurants in Riyadh

Are Indian restaurants in Riyadh expensive?

Prices vary. Luxury restaurants like Indya by Vineet are higher, while casual places like Biryani House are affordable.

Do these restaurants serve vegetarian food?

Yes. Most Indian restaurants in Riyadh offer a variety of vegetarian dishes.

Can tourists eat Indian food in Riyadh safely?

Yes. Indian restaurants follow high hygiene standards and are safe for visitors.

Do they offer delivery or takeaway?

Many restaurants, including Masala Art and Bikanervala, offer delivery and takeaway options.

Are there fine dining Indian options in Riyadh?

Yes. Indya by Vineet, Khyber, and Saffron Lounge offer premium Indian fine dining experiences.

Final Words

Riyadh is a great city for Indian food lovers. From fine dining at Indya by Vineet to casual street-style dishes at Bombay Chowpatty, there is something for everyone. Families, friends, and tourists can enjoy authentic flavors prepared by skilled chefs.

These 14 Indian restaurants in Riyadh are perfect for anyone craving the taste of India. Try a few and enjoy an unforgettable culinary experience.
