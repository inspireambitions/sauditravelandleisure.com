Transportation: Getting Around Safely

Airport arrival and first steps

Dubai International Airport is modern, well-signed, and staffed with English-speaking personnel. Once through immigration, choose official transport options. Real-World Decision Trees: What To Do If Things Go Wrong

If you feel followed while walking

Move to a populated shop or a staffed area immediately. Call your hotel or local emergency services. Share your ride-hailing trip with a contact and request that the driver waits until you safely enter your hotel or a well-lit public place.

If you are harassed in a taxi

Ask the driver to stop in a public area and get out. Take a photo of the license plate and vehicle, note the time, and report to the ride-hailing company and local police. If you feel physically threatened, call emergency services immediately.

If you need medical assistance

Go to the nearest 24-hour clinic or hospital. If you cannot move, call ambulance services. Use your travel insurance providerâs emergency number for guidance, and keep a digital copy of your insurance details accessible.

If you are accused of a legal infraction

Remain calm and request consular assistance. Contact your embassy or consulate, and seek legal representation through trusted channels. Avoid public statements or social media posts that could complicate legal processes.
