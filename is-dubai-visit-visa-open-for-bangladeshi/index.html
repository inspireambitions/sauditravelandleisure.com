Why the Pause Happened and What Changed

Key reasons authorities tightened processing

Immigration measures tightened for a handful of operational reasons that travel planners and applicants must acknowledge:

  • Move toward skilled recruitment: The UAE continues to prioritize skilled professionals and compliant recruitment channels.

How to Strengthen Your Application: Practical Tactics

Immigration examines funds and their source. Provide clear bank statements with consistent balances, salary slips, and employer letters. Large, sudden deposits without explanation invite queries.
