FAQ

Q: Should I cancel a trip to Dubai if there are regional tensions?
A: Not necessarily. Unless your government issues a travel ban, weigh the severity of advisories, the flexibility of your bookings, and your personal risk tolerance. If disruptions would cause major hardship, consider postponing; otherwise travel with contingency plans and flexible insurance.

Q: What are the most important documents to carry and how should I store them?
A: Carry your passport, visa, travel insurance policy, emergency contact list, and copies of prescriptions. Store encrypted digital copies and a physical photocopy separate from the originals.

Q: Will airlines refund flights canceled for security reasons?
A: Many major carriers provide rebooking or refunds when flights are canceled for airspace or security reasons. Keep all communications and receipts, and confirm airline policies at booking.

Q: What should I do if I feel unsafe during my stay?
A: Move to a secure, populated area or your hotel, contact local emergency services and your embassy only if necessary, and notify your insurer if you require medical or evacuation assistance. Hotel staff can often provide immediate, practical support.
