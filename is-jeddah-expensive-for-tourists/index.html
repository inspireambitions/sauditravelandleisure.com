Where People Overpay (And How To Avoid It)

Some common cost traps for visitors:

  • Buying items in tourist-heavy malls instead of local markets.

Avoiding these pitfalls is chiefly a matter of planning and willingness to swap one kind of convenience for another. If your priority is comfort and efficiency, build those costs in deliberately.
