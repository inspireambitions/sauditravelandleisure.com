In Riyadh, tourists can access various emergency resources, including the police by dialing 999, medical assistance through 997, and fire services at 998. Many hotels also provide assistance and information on local emergency protocols to ensure visitors stay safe during their travels.

When using taxis and ride-sharing apps in Riyadh, it's generally safe. Opt for reputable services like Uber or Careem, which ensure driver verification. Always verify the vehicle details before entering and share your trip details with someone for added security.
