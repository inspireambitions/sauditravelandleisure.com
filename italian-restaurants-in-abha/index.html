12 Top Best Italian Restaurants in Abha

Updated August 30, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Looking for authentic Italian food in Abha? ð
This city is known for its cool weather, mountain views, and now — a growing love for Italian cuisine.
From wood-fired pizzas to creamy pastas, Abha offers plenty of Italian dining experiences.
We’ve rounded up the 12 best Italian restaurants in Abha where you can enjoy delicious meals, cozy ambiance, and good value.

Why is Italian food popular in Abha?

Italian food is loved in Abha because of its fresh flavors, family-friendly meals, and comfort dishes.
Pizzas, pastas, and desserts like tiramisu fit perfectly with the cool climate of the city.

Best Italian Restaurants in Abha

1. La Cucina Abha – Best for Authentic Flavors

La Cucina is a go-to spot for real Italian vibes.
They serve handmade pasta, wood-fired pizza, and fresh salads.

  • Pros: Authentic recipes, cozy seating.
  • Cons: Can get crowded during weekends.
  • Price Range: SAR 80–150 per person.
  • Best For: Couples and small families.

2. Spazio Italiano – Best for Pizza Lovers

This place is known for its thin-crust pizzas and wide range of toppings.
The menu also features seafood pasta and risottos.

  • Pros: Great pizza, good service.
  • Cons: Limited vegetarian options.
  • Price Range: SAR 70–120 per person.
  • Best For: Pizza nights with friends.

3. Bella Roma – Best Family-Friendly Italian

Bella Roma combines casual dining with tasty Italian meals.
They have generous portions and a child-friendly menu.

  • Pros: Big portions, kids’ meals available.
  • Cons: Simple décor.
  • Price Range: SAR 60–110 per person.
  • Best For: Families with kids.

4. Al Forno Abha – Best for Wood-Fired Pizza

Al Forno stands out with its open kitchen and pizza oven.
The smell of freshly baked dough makes the experience special.

  • Pros: Freshly baked pizzas, quick service.
  • Cons: Small seating area.
  • Price Range: SAR 65–120 per person.
  • Best For: Quick dinners.

5. Trattoria Toscana – Best for Romantic Dining

This restaurant brings a fine-dining Italian experience.
Candlelight dinners, creamy risottos, and Italian wines set the mood.

  • Pros: Elegant atmosphere, quality food.
  • Cons: Higher prices.
  • Price Range: SAR 120–200 per person.
  • Best For: Romantic nights.

6. Pasta Fresca – Best for Fresh Pasta

If you love pasta, this is the place.
They make pasta daily with fresh sauces like Alfredo and Arrabiata.

  • Pros: Fresh pasta, good value.
  • Cons: Limited seating.
  • Price Range: SAR 55–100 per person.
  • Best For: Pasta enthusiasts.

7. Ristorante Milano – Best for Variety

Milano offers a large Italian menu.
From pizzas to seafood and desserts, there’s something for everyone.

  • Pros: Wide menu, decent prices.
  • Cons: Service can be slow.
  • Price Range: SAR 70–130 per person.
  • Best For: Groups and families.

8. Pizzeria Napoli – Best for Takeaway

Napoli specializes in quick pizzas and pasta for takeout.
Perfect for those who want Italian food at home.

  • Pros: Fast service, affordable.
  • Cons: Limited dine-in space.
  • Price Range: SAR 50–90 per person.
  • Best For: Quick bites and takeout.

9. Villa Italia – Best for Ambiance

This restaurant has a warm, stylish interior.
Their lasagna and tiramisu are top picks.

  • Pros: Great ambiance, delicious desserts.
  • Cons: Parking can be difficult.
  • Price Range: SAR 90–160 per person.
  • Best For: Special occasions.

10. Mama Mia Abha – Best for Budget-Friendly Italian

A casual spot with affordable pasta and pizza options.
Great choice for students and families on a budget.

  • Pros: Low prices, good taste.
  • Cons: Limited fine-dining dishes.
  • Price Range: SAR 40–80 per person.
  • Best For: Budget-friendly meals.

11. Piccolo Ristorante – Best Hidden Gem

Piccolo is small but known for fresh flavors.
Their pesto pasta and desserts are worth trying.

  • Pros: Fresh food, quiet place.
  • Cons: Limited tables.
  • Price Range: SAR 60–100 per person.
  • Best For: Solo diners or couples.

12. Sapori d’Italia – Best Overall Italian Experience

This restaurant offers a complete Italian dining journey.
From appetizers to desserts, everything feels authentic.

  • Pros: Full-course meals, authentic taste.
  • Cons: Can be pricey.
  • Price Range: SAR 100–180 per person.
  • Best For: Those wanting the full Italian experience.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Abha

What is the average cost of Italian food in Abha?

Most Italian restaurants in Abha cost SAR 60–150 per person, depending on the restaurant type.

Do Italian restaurants in Abha offer vegetarian options?

Yes, many serve vegetarian pizzas, salads, and pastas, though options vary.

Which is the best Italian restaurant for families in Abha?

Bella Roma and Milano are best for families with kids.

Which Italian restaurant in Abha is best for couples?

Trattoria Toscana offers the most romantic Italian dining experience.

Final Words

Abha has a growing love for Italian cuisine.
Whether you want budget-friendly pasta, romantic dinners, or wood-fired pizza, this list covers it all.
If you’re visiting Abha, don’t miss these 12 best Italian restaurants for an unforgettable dining experience.
