Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Al Khobar

Updated September 1, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Khobar is one of Saudi Arabia’s most vibrant cities, and it’s also a foodie’s paradise. If you love authentic Italian cuisine—fresh pasta, wood-fired pizza, creamy risotto—this city won’t disappoint. Whether you’re dining near the Corniche, visiting Al Rashid Mall, or exploring cozy local neighborhoods, you’ll find plenty of Italian restaurants serving both casual and fine dining experiences.

Here’s a list of the 12 best Italian restaurants in Al Khobar where you can enjoy a taste of Italy.

Top Italian Restaurants in Al Khobar [[2025 Updated]

1. Piatto Restaurant (Near Al Rashid Mall)

Piatto is one of the most well-known Italian restaurants in Al Khobar. Located close to Al Rashid Mall, it’s a family-friendly spot offering authentic pasta, stone-baked pizzas, and fresh salads. The warm ambiance makes it great for gatherings. Don’t miss their tiramisu and homemade gelato, which are local favorites.

2. La Gondola (Al Khobar Corniche)

La Gondola is perfect for those seeking fine dining with a sea view. Located near the Corniche, this elegant restaurant is famous for seafood pasta, risotto, and authentic wood-fired pizza. The romantic setting makes it an ideal spot for couples. Service is excellent, ensuring a luxury dining experience.

3. Il Gusto Italiano

For a homely Italian feel, Il Gusto Italiano is the go-to. Their freshly baked bread, traditional lasagna, and creamy pasta are highly recommended. The cozy environment makes it perfect for small gatherings or family meals. Guests often highlight the friendly staff and authentic flavors passed down from Italian tradition.

4. Pizza Fusion (Prince Faisal Bin Fahd Rd)

Pizza Fusion puts a modern twist on Italian classics. Known for using organic ingredients and eco-friendly packaging, this casual spot is ideal for quick lunches or group hangouts. Their specialty pizzas, baked with fresh toppings, stand out, but they also serve healthy salads and pasta.

5. Oregano Restaurant

Oregano combines modern interiors with authentic Italian recipes. Popular dishes include Margherita pizza, creamy Alfredo pasta, and signature salads. Families love this spot for its kids’ menu and casual setting. Located near the city center, it’s perfect for weekend dining.

6. Donatello Restaurant

If you’re searching for an upscale Italian dining experience, Donatello delivers. Known for gourmet steaks, seafood pasta, and wood-fired pizza, this restaurant blends luxury with flavor. Its stylish interiors make it a preferred choice for business dinners and celebrations.

7. La Piazza (King Fahd Rd)

La Piazza brings rustic Italian vibes with its cozy setting. Their gnocchi, risotto, and handcrafted pizzas are customer favorites. The location on King Fahd Road makes it easily accessible, attracting families, friends, and couples alike.

8. Romantico Italiano

Romantico Italiano offers an intimate, romantic dining experience. Couples enjoy the freshly made pasta and wood-oven pizzas, while families appreciate the wide variety of menu options. The peaceful ambiance makes it one of the best Italian restaurants for date nights in Al Khobar.

9. Amore Italian Kitchen

Amore is known for blending traditional Italian flavors with a modern touch. Their creamy lasagna, signature pizzas, and panna cotta are must-tries. With a lively atmosphere, it’s a great choice for groups of friends looking for an energetic dining spot.

10. Oliveto Ristorante (Luxury Dining)

Oliveto is one of Al Khobar’s most luxurious Italian restaurants. Perfect for special occasions, it serves premium seafood risotto, artisan pasta, and grilled meats. With its chic design and high-end service, Oliveto is popular for business meetings and fine dining experiences.

11. Mama Rossa

Mama Rossa delivers homestyle Italian food with generous portions. Known for its baked pasta, fresh salads, and flavorful pizzas, this restaurant is budget-friendly while maintaining authentic taste. Families love the relaxed atmosphere, making it a reliable choice for casual dining.

12. Eataliano

Eataliano is a casual restaurant offering classic Italian comfort food. From stone-baked pizza to creamy carbonara, the menu covers all the favorites. With vegetarian-friendly options and affordable prices, it’s a popular spot for both locals and tourists looking for authentic Italian flavors.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Al Khobar

Q1: What is the best Italian restaurant in Al Khobar for families?

Oregano and Mama Rossa are great family-friendly choices with kids’ menus and casual settings.

Q2: Which Italian restaurant in Al Khobar is best for fine dining?

Oliveto Ristorante and La Gondola are perfect for fine dining and special occasions.

Q3: Are there affordable Italian restaurants in Al Khobar?

Yes, Eataliano and Mama Rossa are budget-friendly while still offering authentic Italian dishes.

Q4: Do Italian restaurants in Al Khobar serve vegetarian food?

Yes, most restaurants like Pizza Fusion and Eataliano have vegetarian-friendly options.

Q5: Where can I get the best pizza in Al Khobar?

Pizza Fusion, Piatto, and La Piazza are known for their authentic wood-fired pizzas.
