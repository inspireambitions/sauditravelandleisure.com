12 Top Best Italian Restaurants in Al Ula

Updated September 1, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Ula is one of Saudi Arabia’s most breathtaking destinations, famous for its ancient heritage and stunning desert landscapes. Along with cultural attractions, Al Ula also offers a growing food scene, including some fantastic Italian restaurants. Whether you are craving wood-fired pizza, creamy pasta, or authentic fine dining experiences, Al Ula has something to satisfy every Italian food lover.

In this guide, we highlight the 12 top Italian restaurants in Al Ula that combine delicious food, inviting ambiance, and excellent service.

Best Italian Restaurants in Al Ula [2025 Updated]

1. Circolo

Located in the heart of Al Ula, Circolo offers a modern take on Italian cuisine. The restaurant is known for its wood-fired pizzas, fresh pasta, and elegant dining atmosphere. Visitors also enjoy the warm hospitality, making it a favorite for both tourists and locals.

2. Saffron Italian Kitchen

Saffron Italian Kitchen blends traditional Italian flavors with a touch of Middle Eastern hospitality. Their creamy risottos, rich lasagna, and handmade pasta dishes stand out. The restaurant’s cozy setup makes it perfect for families and couples alike.

3. La Scala Trattoria

A hidden gem in Al Ula, La Scala Trattoria serves authentic Italian recipes passed down through generations. Signature dishes like spaghetti carbonara and tiramisu keep diners coming back. The rustic interior adds to its charm.

4. Villa Toscana

Villa Toscana brings the elegance of Tuscan dining to Al Ula. Known for its fine dining setup, this restaurant specializes in grilled meats, seafood pasta, and a wide selection of Italian wines. It’s an excellent choice for special occasions.

5. Al Ula Pizzeria

If you are craving classic pizza, Al Ula Pizzeria is the go-to spot. Their thin-crust pizzas are baked in a traditional oven, with toppings ranging from Margherita to seafood specials. Affordable pricing also makes it popular among groups.

6. Ristorante Roma

Ristorante Roma captures the true spirit of Rome with its authentic dishes. From fresh bruschetta to rich bolognese pasta, every plate reflects Italian tradition. The restaurant’s relaxed atmosphere makes it ideal for casual dining.

7. Mamma Mia Al Ula

As the name suggests, Mamma Mia is all about homestyle Italian cooking. Their creamy Alfredo pasta and wood-oven pizzas are highly recommended. The friendly staff and lively vibe make it a great place for families.

8. Osteria Bella Notte

Osteria Bella Notte offers a romantic Italian dining experience in Al Ula. Their seafood risotto, truffle pasta, and fine desserts like panna cotta are guest favorites. It’s a perfect choice for couples looking for an intimate dinner.

9. Pasta Fresca

For fresh, handmade pasta, Pasta Fresca stands out. Their menu changes seasonally, ensuring unique dishes prepared with the freshest ingredients. Light and healthy options are also available, making it suitable for all diets.

10. Il Forno Italiano

Known for its authentic oven-baked specialties, Il Forno Italiano combines traditional cooking methods with modern flavors. Their four-cheese pizza and mushroom risotto are a must-try. The restaurant also has a family-friendly setting.

11. Dolce Vita

Dolce Vita adds a sweet touch to Al Ula’s Italian food scene. Aside from pasta and pizza, the restaurant is also famous for its desserts like tiramisu, gelato, and cannoli. It’s the perfect spot for those with a sweet tooth.

12. Trattoria Milano

Ending the list is Trattoria Milano, where guests enjoy hearty meals inspired by Northern Italy. Popular dishes include beef lasagna, seafood linguine, and gourmet pizzas. The warm and welcoming staff enhance the overall dining experience.

Final Words

From fine dining establishments to cozy trattorias, Al Ula offers some of the best Italian restaurants in Saudi Arabia. Whether you’re a tourist exploring the city’s history or a local looking for a weekend treat, these 12 Italian spots guarantee a memorable dining experience.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Al Ula

What is the best Italian restaurant in Al Ula?

Circolo and Villa Toscana are often considered the top Italian dining spots in Al Ula due to their authentic menus and premium service.

Are there affordable Italian restaurants in Al Ula?

Yes, Al Ula Pizzeria and Pasta Fresca are great budget-friendly options offering quality Italian food at reasonable prices.

Do Italian restaurants in Al Ula serve vegetarian options?

Most Italian restaurants in Al Ula, including La Scala Trattoria and Pasta Fresca, offer vegetarian pasta and pizza choices.

Can I find fine dining Italian restaurants in Al Ula?

Absolutely. Villa Toscana and Osteria Bella Notte provide elegant fine dining experiences with premium Italian dishes.
