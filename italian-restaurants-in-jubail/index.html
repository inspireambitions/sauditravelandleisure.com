Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Jubail

2025-09-01

Jubail, known as one of Saudi Arabia’s most important industrial and port cities, is also home to a growing food scene. Among its many international cuisines, Italian food is especially popular. Whether you’re looking for a family-friendly pizzeria, a cozy café, or fine dining with authentic flavors, Jubail has plenty of options.

Here’s a guide to the 12 best Italian restaurants in Jubail where you can enjoy pizza, pasta, and more.

Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Jubail [2025 Updated]

1. Piatto Jubail (Fanateer Mall)

Located in Fanateer Mall, Piatto is a well-loved Italian chain that offers authentic pasta, wood-fired pizza, and fresh salads. The restaurant has a cozy, family-friendly setting, making it perfect after shopping or a day by the Corniche. Don’t miss their tiramisu and gelato for dessert.

2. La Gondola (Fanateer District)

La Gondola is a fine-dining Italian restaurant in Jubail’s Fanateer District. Known for its elegant ambiance and authentic menu, it’s popular for romantic dinners and special occasions. Their seafood risotto, lasagna, and wood-oven pizzas are highly recommended.

3. Pizza Fusion Jubail

Pizza Fusion offers a modern and eco-friendly twist on Italian dining. With organic ingredients and sustainable packaging, it’s a great choice for health-conscious diners. Their thin-crust pizzas, pasta, and fresh salads are popular with both locals and expats.

4. Oregano Restaurant

Oregano is a casual, family-friendly spot that combines authentic Italian recipes with a modern look. Their Alfredo pasta, Margherita pizza, and fresh soups are customer favorites. With a dedicated kids’ menu, it’s perfect for family outings.

5. Amore Italian Kitchen

Amore is known for blending traditional Italian flavors with a modern dining experience. Their lasagna, creamy pasta, and specialty pizzas are a hit among both locals and visitors. The vibrant setting makes it a great spot for group dinners.

6. Il Gusto Italiano

Il Gusto Italiano brings homemade Italian food to Jubail. Their freshly baked bread, hearty lasagna, and creamy pasta stand out for their authentic flavors. It’s a cozy spot for small gatherings and families who love traditional Italian taste.

7. Donatello Ristorante

Donatello offers an upscale Italian dining experience with gourmet pasta, grilled steaks, and seafood dishes. Its stylish interiors and refined service make it ideal for business dinners and celebrations. Their presentation and flavors give a true fine-dining experience.

8. Mama Rossa

Mama Rossa is a cozy Italian restaurant serving generous portions of pasta, pizza, and fresh salads. With a relaxed and homely vibe, it’s a favorite among families looking for affordable yet authentic Italian food in Jubail.

9. La Piazza

La Piazza delivers rustic Italian charm with dishes like gnocchi, risotto, and handcrafted pizzas. Its location makes it easily accessible, while the comfortable setting makes it ideal for casual dining with friends or family.

10. Romantico Italiano

True to its name, Romantico Italiano offers a warm and intimate dining experience. Couples love the freshly made pasta and wood-fired pizzas, while families enjoy the variety on the menu. It’s one of Jubail’s best romantic Italian restaurants.

11. Eataliano

Eataliano is a casual and budget-friendly Italian restaurant in Jubail. The menu includes carbonara, lasagna, and stone-baked pizzas, all prepared with quality ingredients. Vegetarian-friendly options make it suitable for all types of diners.

12. Pizza Inn Jubail

Pizza Inn is a popular chain in Jubail, located in several key areas of the city. It offers a wide variety of pizzas, pasta, and salads at affordable prices. Known for its quick service and family-friendly environment, it’s a reliable option for casual Italian dining.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Jubail

Q1: What is the best Italian restaurant in Jubail for families?

Piatto and Oregano are family-friendly choices with kids’ menus and casual seating.

Q2: Which Italian restaurant in Jubail is best for fine dining?

Donatello and La Gondola are top picks for luxury dining and special occasions.

Q3: Where can I find affordable Italian food in Jubail?

Eataliano and Pizza Inn are great budget-friendly options.

Q4: Do Italian restaurants in Jubail offer vegetarian meals?

Yes, restaurants like Pizza Fusion and Eataliano provide vegetarian-friendly dishes.

Q5: What are the most popular Italian dishes in Jubail?

Pizza, pasta, risotto, and tiramisu are among the most ordered dishes.

Final Words

From casual eateries in Fanateer Mall to fine dining in Fanateer District, Jubail’s Italian food scene has something for everyone. Whether you’re craving a quick pizza or a romantic pasta dinner, these 12 Italian restaurants in Jubail guarantee a flavorful experience.
