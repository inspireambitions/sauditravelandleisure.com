12 Top Best Italian Restaurants in Makkah (2025 Food Guide)

Updated August 30, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Makkah is best known for its spiritual significance, but it also offers a wide range of international cuisines.
Among them, Italian restaurants in Makkah are gaining popularity for their comforting pizzas, creamy pastas, and fine dining experiences.
Whether you’re a local, a pilgrim, or a tourist, this list of the 12 best Italian restaurants in Makkah will help you find the perfect place to enjoy authentic Italian flavors.

Where can I find the best Italian restaurants in Makkah?

1. Piatto – A Family Favorite

Piatto is a well-known Italian chain with a branch in Makkah.
It serves wood-fired pizzas, creamy pastas, and salads in a cozy, family-friendly setting.
Must try: Alfredo pasta, Margherita pizza.

  • Price: SAR 70–120
  • Best for: Families & casual dining
  • Why visit? Consistent flavors, good portions

2. Al Dente – Authentic Italian Dining

Located inside a luxury hotel, Al Dente offers authentic Italian recipes prepared by skilled chefs.
The ambiance is elegant, perfect for formal dinners.
Must try: Risotto, lasagna.

  • Price: SAR 100–180
  • Best for: Couples & business dinners
  • Why visit? Elegant fine dining with Italian classics

3. La Piazza Restaurant

La Piazza brings a modern Italian dining experience with a wide menu of pasta, pizza, and seafood.
It’s great for both groups and families.
Must try: Spaghetti carbonara, seafood pizza.

  • Price: SAR 70–110
  • Best for: Group outings
  • Why visit? Spacious seating, modern interiors

4. Pizza Inn – Casual Italian-American Touch

Pizza Inn serves Italian-style pizzas with an American twist.
It’s budget-friendly and popular among young crowds.
Must try: Pepperoni pizza, cheese bread.

  • Price: SAR 40–80
  • Best for: Students & quick meals
  • Why visit? Affordable, fast service

5. Pastaria – Pasta Specialists

As the name suggests, Pastaria is all about freshly made pasta dishes.
It offers classic and fusion recipes at reasonable prices.
Must try: Penne arrabbiata, lasagna.

  • Price: SAR 60–90
  • Best for: Pasta lovers
  • Why visit? Focused on pasta, authentic taste

6. Bella Roma – Elegant Dining Experience

Bella Roma combines Italian décor with traditional recipes.
It’s known for its romantic atmosphere and excellent service.
Must try: Ravioli, tiramisu.

  • Price: SAR 90–140
  • Best for: Couples & special occasions
  • Why visit? Stylish interiors + authentic recipes

7. Olive Garden – International Italian Chain

Olive Garden offers classic Italian-American dishes in a casual yet upscale setting.
It’s ideal for family gatherings and group dining.
Must try: Fettuccine Alfredo, breadsticks, soups.

  • Price: SAR 80–130
  • Best for: Families & large groups
  • Why visit? Internationally recognized brand

8. Toscana Restaurant

Toscana focuses on Tuscan-style Italian cuisine, emphasizing fresh ingredients and olive oil-rich dishes.
Must try: Grilled chicken with rosemary, Tuscan soup.

  • Price: SAR 90–150
  • Best for: Health-conscious diners
  • Why visit? Unique Tuscan flavors in Makkah

9. Pizza Hut (Italian-Style Range)

While Pizza Hut is a global brand, its Italian-style pizzas and pasta range are favorites in Makkah.
Must try: Creamy pasta, stuffed crust pizza.

  • Price: SAR 40–80
  • Best for: Quick meals & families
  • Why visit? Widely available, kid-friendly

10. La Cucina – Contemporary Italian Eatery

La Cucina is a chic Italian restaurant offering modern recipes with traditional taste.
It’s popular among young professionals and foodies.
Must try: Spaghetti Bolognese, bruschetta.

  • Price: SAR 70–110
  • Best for: Groups & casual dinners
  • Why visit? Trendy vibe + fresh food

11. Rossopomodoro – Neapolitan Pizza Experts

Rossopomodoro brings authentic Naples-style pizzas to Makkah.
The soft dough and rich tomato sauce make it a favorite for pizza lovers.
Must try: Pizza margherita, calzone.

  • Price: SAR 80–120
  • Best for: Pizza enthusiasts
  • Why visit? True Neapolitan pizza taste

12. Spazio – Luxury International Dining

Located in Abraj Al Bait Towers, Spazio is a fine dining restaurant with Italian specialties.
It offers an upscale setting with international and Italian favorites.
Must try: Risotto, grilled salmon, pasta primavera.

  • Price: SAR 150–250
  • Best for: Luxury experiences & tourists
  • Why visit? Exclusive fine dining + stunning views of the Holy Mosque

FAQ – People Also Ask

Which Italian restaurant in Makkah is best for families?

Piatto and Olive Garden are family-friendly with wide menus.

Where can I find the best pizza in Makkah?

Rossopomodoro offers authentic Neapolitan pizza, while Pizza Inn is best for budget-friendly pizza.

What are the most affordable Italian restaurants in Makkah?

Pizza Inn and Pizza Hut are the most budget-friendly.

Which Italian restaurant is best for fine dining in Makkah?

Spazio and Al Dente are perfect for luxury dining.

Are there vegetarian options in Italian restaurants in Makkah?

Yes, most spots like La Piazza, Bella Roma, and Piatto serve vegetarian pasta and pizzas.

Final Words

From luxury dining at Spazio to budget pizza at Pizza Inn, Makkah has Italian restaurants for every type of diner.
Whether you’re looking for authentic Neapolitan pizza, creamy pastas, or a romantic dinner, these 12 restaurants will satisfy your Italian cravings in the holy city.
