12 Top Best Italian Restaurants in Riyadh

2025-08-25

Riyadh has a growing food scene. Italian cuisine is very popular in the city. From pizza and pasta to fine dining, there are many options to try.

Visitors can enjoy authentic flavors prepared by skilled chefs. Whether you want a casual meal or a luxurious dinner, Riyadh has Italian restaurants to satisfy every taste.

Here are the 12 Top Italian restaurants in Riyadh you should try in 2025

1. Cipriani Riyadh

Cipriani is a fine Italian restaurant in Riyadh. It is famous for pasta, risotto, and veal dishes. The elegant décor and premium service make it perfect for romantic dinners or business meals. Guests love the classic tiramisu and fresh seafood options.

2. Piatto

Piatto is a family-friendly Italian restaurant. It serves pizza, pasta, and fresh salads. The portions are generous, and the prices are reasonable. It is a great option for casual dining with friends and family.

3. BiCE Restaurant

BiCE offers gourmet Italian dining in Riyadh. The menu includes seafood pasta, risotto, and steak. The modern interiors and quiet setting make it suitable for special occasions and business dinners.

4. Roberto’s

Roberto’s is known for Italian fine dining with a contemporary touch. Guests enjoy pizzas, pasta, and international Italian fusion dishes. The restaurant is perfect for upscale dinners or celebrations.

5. Spazio Italian

Spazio Italian combines traditional Italian flavors with modern presentation. Guests can enjoy authentic pasta, seafood, and signature desserts. The stylish atmosphere makes it ideal for romantic dinners.

6. La Cucina

La Cucina is a cozy Italian restaurant popular for its pasta, pizza, and fresh ingredients. The casual setting makes it great for families and friends. The service is friendly, and the menu has vegetarian options.

7. Il Posto

Il Posto serves classic Italian dishes with a modern twist. Guests love the wood-fired pizzas, creamy risottos, and fresh pasta. The restaurant offers a comfortable and stylish dining environment.

8. Piacere Italian Restaurant

Piacere is a well-known Italian restaurant in Riyadh. It specializes in pizza, pasta, and Italian desserts. The décor is elegant, making it suitable for both casual and formal meals.

9. Rossini

Rossini offers premium Italian dining with Mediterranean influences. Guests enjoy seafood pasta, risotto, and fine wines. The restaurant is known for its elegant service and sophisticated atmosphere.

10. Tutti Matti

Tutti Matti is a casual Italian restaurant famous for its pizza and pasta. The flavors are authentic, and the prices are reasonable. It is perfect for a quick Italian meal or casual dining with family.

11. Casa Mia

Casa Mia is a cozy Italian eatery in Riyadh. It offers fresh pasta, pizza, and delicious Italian desserts. The relaxed atmosphere makes it perfect for casual dining or weekend outings.

12. Vivaldi

Vivaldi is a premium Italian restaurant known for its authentic recipes and fine ingredients. Guests enjoy risotto, seafood pasta, and gourmet Italian dishes. The luxurious interiors make it ideal for celebrations or romantic dinners.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Riyadh

Are Italian restaurants in Riyadh expensive?

Prices vary. Fine dining options like Cipriani are expensive, while casual restaurants like Piatto are affordable.

Do these restaurants serve vegetarian food?

Yes. Most Italian restaurants offer vegetarian pizzas, pasta, and salads.

Can tourists eat safely in Italian restaurants?

Yes. Hygiene standards in Riyadh restaurants are high, making them safe for tourists.

Do these restaurants offer delivery or takeaway?

Many restaurants, including Piatto and Casa Mia, offer delivery and takeaway services.

Are there family-friendly Italian restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes. Piatto, Tutti Matti, and La Cucina are popular choices for families.

Final Words

Riyadh offers a variety of Italian dining experiences. From fine dining at Cipriani and BiCE to casual meals at Piatto and Tutti Matti, there is something for everyone. Families, friends, and tourists can enjoy authentic Italian flavors prepared by skilled chefs.

These 12 Italian restaurants in Riyadh provide unforgettable dining experiences in 2025. Don’t miss trying a few during your visit to the city.
