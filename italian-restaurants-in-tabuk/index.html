Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Tabuk

Updated September 1, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Tabuk, a historic city in northwest Saudi Arabia, is not only known for its cultural heritage and natural beauty but also for its growing food scene. Italian cuisine, in particular, has gained popularity among both locals and visitors. From wood-fired pizzas to creamy pasta dishes and fine dining experiences, Tabuk has plenty of options to satisfy your Italian food cravings.

In this guide, we’ve rounded up the 12 best Italian restaurants in Tabuk that combine authentic flavors, inviting ambiance, and top-notch service.

Best Italian Restaurants in Tabuk [2025 updated]

1. Piatto

Piatto is one of the most well-known Italian restaurant chains in Saudi Arabia, and its Tabuk branch is a favorite. With signature dishes like Alfredo pasta, wood-oven pizzas, and fresh salads, Piatto offers a modern yet authentic Italian dining experience.

2. La Piazza

For a mix of traditional recipes and contemporary presentations, La Piazza stands out. Their risottos, pasta primavera, and signature tiramisu are highly recommended. The elegant interiors make it ideal for family dinners and gatherings.

3. Pizza Napoli

If you’re craving thin-crust pizzas straight from a wood oven, Pizza Napoli is the place to go. Their Margherita and seafood pizzas are particularly popular. The casual setting makes it perfect for quick bites.

4. Bella Italia

Bella Italia specializes in fresh pasta and authentic sauces. Their creamy carbonara and lasagna are customer favorites. With warm hospitality and a cozy vibe, it feels like a little slice of Italy in Tabuk.

5. Trattoria Tabuk

Known for its rustic Italian dishes, Trattoria Tabuk offers hearty meals such as spaghetti bolognese, mushroom risotto, and oven-baked pizza. It’s a great choice for both couples and families.

6. Donatello’s

Donatello’s combines Italian flavors with a modern touch. Their menu includes a variety of pasta, gourmet pizzas, and desserts like panna cotta. The restaurant also has a stylish interior for a comfortable dining experience.

7. Tavola

Tavola is loved for its wide selection of pasta dishes made with fresh ingredients. Their seafood linguine and truffle pasta are standout items. It’s an excellent spot for fine dining in Tabuk.

8. Pizza Roma

Pizza Roma serves authentic Roman-style pizzas along with antipasti and salads. Their simple yet flavorful recipes have made them a local favorite for quick and tasty Italian meals.

9. Il Gusto Italiano

Il Gusto Italiano brings classic Italian flavors with a touch of luxury. Their steak with herb butter, alongside classic pasta dishes, makes it one of Tabuk’s more upscale Italian restaurants.

10. Casa Di Pasta

For handmade pasta and rich sauces, Casa Di Pasta is a must-visit. Their ravioli and fettuccine Alfredo are highly recommended. The restaurant’s family-friendly environment makes it popular among locals.

11. Dolce Vita

Dolce Vita is best known for its Italian desserts and pastries, though their savory menu also includes delicious pizzas and pasta. It’s the perfect place for a sweet ending to your Italian meal.

12. Osteria Tabuk

Osteria Tabuk offers an intimate Italian dining experience. Their truffle risotto, seafood pasta, and classic tiramisu create a fine balance of flavors. It’s an ideal spot for date nights or special occasions.

Final Words

From budget-friendly trattorias to high-end fine dining restaurants, Tabuk has a wide range of Italian eateries that cater to every taste. Whether you’re in the mood for quick pizza or a full gourmet dinner, these 12 top Italian restaurants in Tabuk guarantee a memorable dining experience.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Tabuk

What is the most popular Italian restaurant in Tabuk?

Piatto and La Piazza are among the most popular Italian dining spots in Tabuk for their authentic flavors and modern ambiance.

Are there affordable Italian restaurants in Tabuk?

Yes, Pizza Napoli and Pizza Roma are excellent budget-friendly options offering delicious pizzas at reasonable prices.

Do Italian restaurants in Tabuk serve vegetarian options?

Absolutely. Most restaurants, including Casa Di Pasta and Bella Italia, offer vegetarian pasta and pizza selections.

Can I find fine dining Italian restaurants in Tabuk?

Yes. Tavola, Il Gusto Italiano, and Osteria Tabuk provide elegant fine dining experiences with authentic Italian dishes.
