Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Taif

Updated September 1, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Taif, known as the "City of Roses," is famous for its cool climate, stunning mountains, and traditional Saudi charm. But beyond its natural beauty, Taif also offers a growing international food scene. If you’re craving Italian food—whether it’s wood-fired pizza, creamy pasta, or fine dining with a view—this city won’t disappoint.

Here’s a guide to the 12 best Italian restaurants in Taif for locals and visitors alike.

Best Italian Restaurants in Taif [2025 Updated]

1. Piatto Taif (Taif Heart Mall)

Piatto is a well-loved Italian chain that has made its mark in Taif. Located inside Taif Heart Mall, it’s perfect for families and friends after a day of shopping. Their wood-fired pizzas, creamy Alfredo pasta, and fresh salads are top picks. The friendly staff and cozy setting make it a go-to for casual dining.

2. Pizza Inn (Shahar Street)

Pizza Inn is popular among locals for its wide range of pizzas and pasta dishes. Located on Shahar Street, it offers both dine-in and takeaway options. The menu includes classic Margherita, meat lovers, and cheesy pasta dishes. It’s budget-friendly and family-oriented, making it a convenient stop for quick meals.

3. La Piazza

La Piazza delivers a cozy, rustic Italian vibe. Known for its handcrafted pizzas and creamy risotto, it’s a great spot for a relaxed evening with friends. The restaurant also offers outdoor seating, perfect for Taif’s cool evenings. Their tiramisu is one of the most recommended desserts.

4. Amore Italian Kitchen

Amore Italian Kitchen in Taif blends authentic recipes with a modern touch. Diners love their signature lasagna, wood-fired pizzas, and panna cotta. The lively atmosphere makes it ideal for group dinners or family outings. Its central location adds to its popularity.

5. Oregano Restaurant

Oregano is a family-friendly Italian restaurant serving classic favorites like creamy Alfredo pasta, Margherita pizza, and Caesar salad. With a kids’ menu and casual seating, it’s perfect for weekend family gatherings. The relaxed atmosphere keeps visitors coming back.

6. La Gondola (Luxury Dining)

For those seeking a fine dining experience, La Gondola is a top choice. Known for its elegant interiors and authentic Italian flavors, this restaurant serves gourmet pasta, seafood risotto, and wood-fired pizza. It’s one of the most romantic spots in Taif, great for date nights or celebrations.

7. Donatello Ristorante

Donatello offers a refined Italian dining experience with a focus on premium ingredients. Their menu includes steaks, artisan pasta, and specialty pizzas. The chic interior design makes it suitable for business dinners and formal gatherings.

8. Mama Rossa

Mama Rossa is a cozy Italian eatery in Taif that focuses on homestyle cooking. Their baked pasta, cheesy pizza, and fresh salads are customer favorites. The restaurant is known for its generous portions at affordable prices, making it a reliable family choice.

9. Romantico Italiano

Romantico Italiano offers a romantic setting with soft lighting and a traditional Italian menu. Couples love the freshly made pasta and oven-baked pizzas. Families also enjoy the wide variety of choices, making it versatile for different occasions.

10. Pizza Fusion

Pizza Fusion stands out for its healthy and organic approach to Italian food. Using eco-friendly packaging and fresh toppings, they specialize in flavorful, thin-crust pizzas. Their pasta and salads are also popular for health-conscious diners.

11. Eataliano

Eataliano is a casual, budget-friendly Italian spot in Taif. With a diverse menu featuring carbonara, lasagna, and stone-baked pizza, it’s a hit among both locals and tourists. Vegetarian-friendly dishes make it inclusive for all preferences.

12. Il Gusto Italiano

Il Gusto Italiano brings homestyle Italian flavors to Taif with its freshly baked bread, traditional lasagna, and hearty pasta dishes. The warm and welcoming atmosphere makes it ideal for families and small groups. Their desserts, especially tiramisu, are highly recommended.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Taif

Q1: What is the best Italian restaurant in Taif for families?

Mama Rossa and Oregano are popular for families thanks to their casual setting and kids’ menus.

Q2: Which Italian restaurant in Taif is best for fine dining?

La Gondola and Donatello Ristorante are excellent choices for upscale dining.

Q3: Where can I find affordable Italian food in Taif?

Eataliano and Pizza Inn offer tasty Italian meals at budget-friendly prices.

Q4: Do Italian restaurants in Taif serve vegetarian food?

Yes, most restaurants including Pizza Fusion and Eataliano have vegetarian options.

Q5: What are the most popular Italian dishes in Taif restaurants?

Pizza, creamy pasta, risotto, and tiramisu are among the top favorites.

Final Words

Whether you’re exploring Shahar Street, shopping at Taif Heart Mall, or enjoying the mountain views, the city offers plenty of Italian dining options. From fine dining at La Gondola to casual family meals at Mama Rossa, these 12 Italian restaurants in Taif guarantee a delicious experience.
