Top 12 Best Italian Restaurants in Yanbu

Updated September 1, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Yanbu, located on the Red Sea coast, is known for its beautiful beaches, industrial port, and relaxed lifestyle. But it’s also a city where food lovers can explore a wide variety of cuisines—including authentic Italian. From fine dining by the sea to family-friendly pizzerias, Yanbu offers plenty of choices for pasta, pizza, and more.

Here’s a guide to the 12 best Italian restaurants in Yanbu you should try.

Best Italian Restaurants in Yanbu [2025 Updated]

1. Piatto Yanbu (Dana Mall)

Piatto is a well-known Italian chain with a branch in Yanbu’s Dana Mall. Popular for its authentic wood-fired pizzas, creamy pastas, and fresh salads, it’s perfect for families and friends after shopping. Their tiramisu and gelato are customer favorites.

2. La Gondola (Seafront Area)

La Gondola is one of Yanbu’s top fine-dining Italian restaurants. Located near the seafront, it offers a romantic ambiance with authentic dishes like seafood risotto, lasagna, and stone-baked pizza. Ideal for couples or special occasions.

3. Pizza Inn Yanbu

Pizza Inn is a popular chain in Yanbu, offering a wide selection of pizzas, pasta, and salads. Known for quick service and family-friendly dining, it’s a great spot for casual meals at affordable prices.

4. Oregano Restaurant

Oregano is a cozy, family-friendly restaurant serving Italian classics. Their Alfredo pasta, Margherita pizza, and Caesar salad are among the top picks. A dedicated kids’ menu makes it a go-to choice for family outings.

5. Amore Italian Kitchen

Amore brings a modern touch to traditional Italian dining. Known for its flavorful lasagna, fresh pasta, and specialty pizzas, it’s a vibrant spot for group dinners. The lively atmosphere makes it especially popular among young diners.

6. Donatello Ristorante

For fine dining in Yanbu, Donatello is a top pick. With elegant interiors and a premium menu featuring steaks, seafood pasta, and gourmet pizzas, it’s a favorite for business dinners and luxury dining.

7. Il Gusto Italiano

Il Gusto Italiano is perfect for those who want homestyle Italian flavors. Their freshly baked bread, lasagna, and hearty pasta dishes are comforting and authentic. It’s a cozy choice for family dinners and small gatherings.

8. Mama Rossa

Mama Rossa is a casual Italian eatery known for its large portions and homely vibe. Their baked pasta, cheesy pizzas, and fresh salads are affordable yet authentic, making it a family favorite in Yanbu.

9. La Piazza

La Piazza offers a rustic Italian feel with its menu of gnocchi, risotto, and handcrafted pizzas. It’s a great choice for casual dinners with friends, and the comfortable seating makes it ideal for longer meals.

10. Romantico Italiano

Romantico Italiano is a quiet and intimate restaurant, making it one of Yanbu’s best romantic spots. Couples love the wood-oven pizzas and creamy pasta dishes. Families also appreciate the variety of options on the menu.

11. Pizza Fusion Yanbu

Pizza Fusion is popular among health-conscious diners thanks to its organic ingredients and eco-friendly approach. Their thin-crust pizzas, fresh salads, and light pastas are perfect for a balanced yet delicious meal.

12. Eataliano

Eataliano is a casual Italian restaurant offering a wide range of pizzas, pastas, and desserts. With affordable prices and vegetarian-friendly options, it’s a hit with both locals and tourists exploring Yanbu.

Final Words

From fine dining on the seafront at La Gondola to casual family meals at Mama Rossa, Yanbu offers Italian restaurants to suit every mood and budget. Whether you’re craving a quick pizza or a romantic pasta dinner, these 12 best Italian restaurants in Yanbu will satisfy your taste buds.

FAQs About Italian Restaurants in Yanbu

Q1: What is the best Italian restaurant in Yanbu for families?

Piatto and Oregano are popular family-friendly choices with kids’ menus and casual settings.

Q2: Which Italian restaurant in Yanbu is best for fine dining?

Donatello and La Gondola are excellent for luxury dining and romantic evenings.

Q3: Where can I find affordable Italian food in Yanbu?

Mama Rossa and Pizza Inn are budget-friendly while still serving authentic Italian meals.

Q4: Do Italian restaurants in Yanbu offer vegetarian options?

Yes, Pizza Fusion and Eataliano both provide vegetarian-friendly pizzas, salads, and pasta dishes.
