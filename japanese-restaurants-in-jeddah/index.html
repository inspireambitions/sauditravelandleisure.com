10 Best Japanese Restaurants in Jeddah You Must Try

Updated September 13, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jeddah is one of Saudi Arabia’s most vibrant cities, known for its Red Sea coastline, cultural diversity, and booming food scene. Among its many international cuisines, Japanese food has taken center stage with sushi bars, ramen houses, and elegant fine-dining spots opening across the city.

If you’re a fan of sushi rolls, sashimi, or authentic Japanese noodles, this guide will take you through the 10 best Japanese restaurants in Jeddah you should definitely try.

1. Nozomi Jeddah

An upscale Japanese fine-dining spot offering premium sushi, wagyu beef, and contemporary Japanese dishes. Perfect for those seeking a luxury dining experience.

2. Benihana

World-famous for teppanyaki shows, Benihana combines entertainment with great food. Their sushi rolls and hibachi dishes make it a hit for families and groups.

3. Sakura – Crown Plaza Hotel

A calm, authentic Japanese restaurant located in Crown Plaza Hotel. Known for its sashimi platters and ramen, it’s ideal for business lunches and romantic evenings.

4. Yokari

Yokari focuses on fine Japanese dining with a modern touch. Their omakase menu and premium sushi cuts make it a must for true Japanese food lovers.

5. Tokyo Restaurant

One of the oldest and most trusted Japanese restaurants in Jeddah. Their traditional menu features miso soup, tempura, and sushi that keep customers coming back.

6. Gold Sushi Club

Trendy, stylish, and innovative — Gold Sushi Club is popular for creative sushi platters, desserts, and a lively atmosphere.

7. Sushi Yoshi

A more budget-friendly spot offering a wide variety of sushi rolls, ramen, and bento boxes. Perfect for casual meals and students.

8. Kuuru Jeddah

Kuuru is modern and artistic, serving Japanese fusion dishes with high-end presentation. A great place for foodies and fine-dining fans.

9. Toki Restaurant

Located at Leylaty, Toki combines Japanese flavors with Asian fusion. Known for its elegant décor and upscale vibe, it’s a romantic hotspot.

10. Shogun Lounge

Shogun offers a relaxed dining experience with a variety of sushi rolls, ramen bowls, and casual bites. It’s perfect for everyday Japanese cravings.

Final Words

Jeddah’s Japanese dining scene is thriving, offering everything from budget-friendly sushi at Sushi Yoshi to luxury dining experiences at Nozomi and Toki. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual night out, or just craving sushi, these 10 best Japanese restaurants in Jeddah will satisfy your appetite.
