10 Best Japanese Restaurants in Madinah You Must Try

Updated September 13, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Madinah, one of the most sacred cities in the world, is not only a spiritual destination but also a city that embraces global cuisines. In recent years, Japanese food has become increasingly popular here, with residents and visitors looking for sushi, ramen, tempura, and other authentic dishes. Whether you want a fine-dining experience or a quick sushi roll, Madinah has options that cater to every taste and budget.

Here’s a guide to the 10 best Japanese restaurants in Madinah that are worth visiting.

1. Sakura – Pullman Zamzam Hotel

Located inside Pullman Zamzam Hotel, Sakura is one of the most authentic Japanese restaurants in Madinah. With sushi platters, sashimi, and hot miso soups, it’s a favorite among travelers and locals alike.

2. Tokyo Restaurant

A well-known Japanese dining spot offering miso soup, tempura, and classic sushi rolls. A family-friendly environment that keeps guests coming back.

3. Gold Sushi Club

Trendy and modern, Gold Sushi Club serves colorful sushi rolls, creative Japanese platters, and desserts — making it a hit among young diners and groups.

4. Sushi Yoshi

Affordable and casual, Sushi Yoshi specializes in sushi rolls, ramen, and bento boxes. Perfect for students, families, and everyday dining.

5. P.F. Chang’s – Madinah

A global favorite, P.F. Chang’s blends Japanese-inspired dishes with Asian flavors. From dynamite shrimp to Mongolian beef, it’s great for celebrations.

6. Toki

Known for its elegant interior and fine-dining experience, Toki brings Japanese fusion dishes with bold flavors and premium presentation.

7. Shogun Lounge

A casual Japanese eatery offering ramen bowls, sushi rolls, and rice platters. Great for those craving quick and tasty meals.

8. Mandarin Asian Restaurant

Mandarin is an Asian fusion restaurant that features Japanese dishes like teriyaki chicken, sushi, and noodle bowls on its diverse menu.

9. Red Lotus

Red Lotus serves dumplings, wonton soups, and fresh sushi platters. A cozy dining option for smaller gatherings.

10. Noodle House

Specializing in noodle-based dishes, this spot offers ramen and stir-fried noodle plates with Japanese flavors, perfect for casual, budget-friendly dining.

Final Words

Madinah may be best known for its sacred heritage, but its food scene is growing rapidly. From fine-dining experiences at Sakura and Toki to budget-friendly sushi at Sushi Yoshi and Noodle House, the city has something for every Japanese food lover. If you’re craving sushi, ramen, or tempura, these 10 best Japanese restaurants in Madinah should be at the top of your list.
