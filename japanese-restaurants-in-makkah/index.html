10 Best Japanese Restaurants in Makkah You Should Try

Updated September 13, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Makkah, the holy city that attracts millions of visitors every year, is not only rich in spirituality and tradition but also in its diverse food culture. While Middle Eastern cuisine dominates the local dining scene, Japanese restaurants in Makkah are gaining popularity among residents and travelers looking for sushi, sashimi, ramen, and other authentic flavors from Japan.

If you’re craving fresh sushi rolls or a cozy Japanese dining experience, here’s a list of the 10 best Japanese restaurants in Makkah to explore.

1. Sakura – Crowne Plaza Hotel

A sophisticated Japanese dining spot offering sushi, sashimi, and miso soup in a serene setting. Perfect for couples and business travelers.

2. Tokyo Restaurant

One of the oldest Japanese restaurants in Saudi Arabia, serving miso soup, tempura, and classic sushi rolls. A family-friendly choice.

3. Gold Sushi Club

Trendy and modern, Gold Sushi Club offers colorful sushi platters, maki rolls, and Japanese desserts. A favorite for social gatherings.

4. Sushi Yoshi

Affordable and casual, Sushi Yoshi is loved for its sushi rolls, ramen bowls, and bento boxes. Great for students and families.

5. P.F. Chang’s – Makkah

A global brand serving Asian-Japanese fusion dishes like dynamite shrimp, Mongolian beef, and sushi rolls. Best for celebrations.

6. Kuuru

Kuuru is all about artful Japanese fusion dining. Known for premium sushi, wagyu beef, and modern plating. A luxury dining spot.

7. Shogun Lounge

A casual Japanese lounge with sushi rolls, soups, and noodles. Affordable and suitable for everyday cravings.

8. Mandarin Asian Restaurant

Though focused on Asian fusion, Mandarin offers authentic Japanese items like sushi and teriyaki dishes.

9. Red Lotus

A cozy restaurant blending Japanese and Asian flavors. Their sushi platters and dumplings are customer favorites.

10. Noodle House

Specializing in noodles and soups with a Japanese twist, Noodle House is perfect for fast, affordable meals.

Final Words

Makkah is not only a city of spirituality but also a hub for global cuisines. From affordable sushi at Sushi Yoshi to luxury fine dining at Kuuru, the city offers something for every foodie. If you’re visiting or living in Makkah and craving Japanese flavors, these 10 best Japanese restaurants are sure to satisfy your appetite.
