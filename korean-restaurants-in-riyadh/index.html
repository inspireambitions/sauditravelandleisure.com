Top 8 Best Korean Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is a city full of diverse dining experiences. Beyond traditional Saudi food, the city offers flavors from around the world. Korean cuisine has gained huge popularity here. From barbecue and kimchi to bibimbap and spicy stews, Korean food is now a must-try in Riyadh.

In this guide, we explore the 8 best Korean restaurants in Riyadh. Whether you want casual dining, group meals, or premium experiences, these restaurants bring authentic Korean flavors to the capital.

Along with Korean options, you can also explore the best Japanese, Indian, Italian, French, and Chinese restaurants in Riyadh for a full culinary journey.

Best Korean Restaurants in Riyadh

1. Cho Dang Gol

Cho Dang Gol is one of Riyadh’s most loved Korean barbecue spots. Guests can grill their own meat at the table, including beef, pork, and chicken.

The restaurant has a cozy and modern interior. Its menu also includes bibimbap, kimchi, and savory stews. Perfect for families and friends, Cho Dang Gol combines authentic flavors with an interactive dining experience.

2. Han Kook Kwan

Han Kook Kwan offers traditional Korean dishes in a serene setting. Popular items include bulgogi, japchae, and seafood pancakes.

The restaurant focuses on authenticity. Ingredients are fresh, and flavors are true to Korean cuisine. It is ideal for casual meals or quiet dinners with family.

3. Koryo Korean Restaurant

Koryo is a premium Korean dining spot in Riyadh. It serves high-quality barbecue, sashimi-style dishes, and hearty Korean soups.

The interior is elegant and modern, making it perfect for business lunches or special occasions. Guests often praise the attentive service and flavor of every dish

4. Mapo Galbi

Mapo Galbi is known for its spicy Korean barbecue. The restaurant also offers hotpots, kimchi fried rice, and traditional side dishes.

It’s popular with groups and families who enjoy a lively dining atmosphere. The bold flavors and interactive grills make every visit fun and memorable.

5. Bulgogi House

Bulgogi House specializes in marinated grilled beef and pork. Its menu includes traditional Korean classics like bibimbap and tteokbokki.

The casual atmosphere makes it suitable for everyday dining. Affordable prices combined with authentic flavors make it a local favorite.

6. Seorae Korean BBQ

Seorae brings an upscale Korean barbecue experience to Riyadh. Guests can enjoy premium cuts of meat and an array of side dishes.

The restaurant has a contemporary ambiance and is perfect for celebrations or group dinners. Its attention to detail ensures a true Korean dining experience.

7. Arirang Korean Restaurant

Arirang focuses on traditional Korean flavors. Signature dishes include bulgogi, kimchi stew, and seafood pancakes.

The cozy environment and authentic menu attract both locals and expatriates. Arirang is ideal for casual dining with friends or family.

8. Kimchi & Co.

Kimchi & Co. offers a mix of traditional and modern Korean dishes. Popular items include spicy chicken, bibimbap, and an assortment of Korean street food snacks.

The vibrant interior and friendly service make it a great choice for casual meals and quick bites. It’s a popular spot among young crowds in Riyadh.

FAQ About Korean Restaurants in Riyadh

What is the best Korean restaurant in Riyadh for barbecue?

Cho Dang Gol and Seorae Korean BBQ are top choices for authentic Korean barbecue, with interactive grills and premium cuts of meat.

Which Korean restaurants in Riyadh are family-friendly?

Mapo Galbi, Bulgogi House, and Arirang Korean Restaurant are ideal for families. They offer casual dining, group meals, and flavorful side dishes.

Are there affordable Korean restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes. Bulgogi House, Kimchi & Co., and Mapo Galbi provide authentic Korean dishes at reasonable prices, perfect for casual dining.

Where can I try authentic Korean soups and stews?

Han Kook Kwan, Koryo, and Arirang serve traditional Korean soups, stews, and hotpots, giving you the true taste of Korea.

Do Korean restaurants in Riyadh serve more than barbecue?

Absolutely. Most restaurants also offer bibimbap, kimchi, tteokbokki, seafood pancakes, and other Korean specialties.

Which Korean restaurant is best for groups or celebrations?

Seorae Korean BBQ and Mapo Galbi are great for groups. Their lively environment and interactive dining experiences make gatherings fun.

Final Words

Riyadh has become a hub for international cuisines, and Korean food is no exception. From sizzling barbecue to spicy stews and bibimbap, these 8 best Korean restaurants in Riyadh offer authentic flavors for all tastes.
