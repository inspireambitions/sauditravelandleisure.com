7 Top Best Mexican Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh’s dining scene is a melting pot of global cuisines, and Mexican food has carved a special place for itself among food lovers in the city. Known for its bold spices, vibrant colors, and hearty meals, Mexican cuisine brings a festive dining experience to the Saudi capital. From sizzling fajitas to cheesy quesadillas and refreshing guacamole, Mexican restaurants in Riyadh offer authentic flavors that transport you straight to Mexico.

In this guide, we’ll explore the 7 best Mexican restaurants in Riyadh, perfect for taco Tuesdays, family dinners, or a night out with friends.

Best Mexican Restaurants in Riyadh

1. Fuego Restaurant

Fuego is a must-visit for anyone craving authentic Mexican flavors in Riyadh. With its rustic décor and lively atmosphere, it offers a wide range of tacos, burritos, and enchiladas that keep customers coming back. Their guacamole, prepared fresh at the table, is a crowd favorite.

What to Try: Beef tacos, chicken fajitas, churros
Location: King Fahd Road

2. El Chico

One of the most popular Mexican chains in Riyadh, El Chico delivers a perfect balance of authentic Mexican dishes with a family-friendly environment. The restaurant is spacious, making it ideal for gatherings and celebrations.

What to Try: Nachos supreme, chimichangas, margarita mocktails
Location: Granada Mall

3. Cantina Laredo

Cantina Laredo is known for giving traditional Mexican recipes a modern twist. The sleek interiors and vibrant food presentations make it a favorite among young diners.

What to Try: Queso fundido, shrimp tacos, tres leches cake
Location: Tahlia Street

4. Fajita House

As the name suggests, Fajita House specializes in sizzling fajitas served with fresh tortillas, sauces, and dips. The menu also includes a variety of burritos, quesadillas, and Mexican rice dishes.

What to Try: Chicken fajitas, beef burritos, Mexican rice
Location: Al Olaya District

5. On the Border

This American-Mexican chain has gained a solid reputation in Riyadh for its authentic taste and generous portions. The casual dining setup makes it a great spot for families and groups of friends.

What to Try: Loaded nachos, fish tacos, sizzling fajita platter
Location: Panorama Mall

6. El Rancho

El Rancho blends a rustic Mexican feel with deliciously authentic flavors. From tacos to tamales, the restaurant offers a wide variety of traditional Mexican dishes.

What to Try: Tamales, steak tacos, Mexican flan
Location: King Abdullah Road

7. Chili’s (Mexican Menu)

While Chili’s is known as an American casual dining chain, its Mexican-inspired menu items have earned it a loyal fan base in Riyadh. The Tex-Mex influence makes it perfect for those who love bold, cheesy flavors.

What to Try: Chicken quesadillas, fajita trio, molten lava cake
Location: Multiple branches across Riyadh

FAQ – Mexican Restaurants in Riyadh

Are there vegetarian options in Mexican restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes, most Mexican restaurants in Riyadh offer vegetarian dishes like veggie tacos, cheese enchiladas, and vegetarian burritos.

Do Mexican restaurants in Riyadh serve halal food?

Yes, the majority of Mexican restaurants in Riyadh prepare their dishes with halal ingredients to cater to local dietary preferences.

Which Mexican restaurant in Riyadh is best for families?

El Chico and On the Border are great family-friendly choices with spacious seating and kids’ menus.

What is the average cost of dining at a Mexican restaurant in Riyadh?

Expect to spend around SAR 100–200 per person, depending on the restaurant and your menu choices.

Can I find authentic Mexican desserts in Riyadh?

Yes, popular desserts like churros, tres leches cake, and flan are commonly served at Mexican restaurants in the city.

Final Words

Mexican cuisine in Riyadh has grown in popularity, thanks to its rich flavors and lively dining experiences. Whether you’re a fan of sizzling fajitas, cheesy nachos, or spicy tacos, these 7 top Mexican restaurants in Riyadh will not disappoint. So next time you’re craving a fiesta of flavors, head to one of these spots for a memorable meal.
