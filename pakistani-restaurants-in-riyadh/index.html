8 Best Pakistani Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is a melting pot of cultures, and its food scene reflects that diversity. For those who love bold flavors, aromatic spices, and hearty meals, Pakistani cuisine in Riyadh offers an authentic experience. From sizzling kebabs and flavorful biryanis to creamy curries and freshly baked naan, Pakistani restaurants in Riyadh bring the taste of Lahore and Karachi right to the heart of Saudi Arabia.

Whether you are a tourist, an expat, or a local food lover, here are the 8 best Pakistani restaurants in Riyadh you must try.

Best Pakistani Restaurants in Riyadh

1. Bar.B.Q Tonight

Bar.B.Q Tonight is one of the most popular Pakistani restaurants in Riyadh, known for its variety of grilled dishes, tandoori items, and flavorful curries. The restaurant offers a buffet and Ã  la carte menu, making it ideal for family gatherings and group dinners. Their chicken tikka, seekh kebabs, and haleem are highly recommended. The atmosphere is lively, with a touch of traditional Pakistani culture.

2. Student Biryani

If you are a biryani lover, Student Biryani should be your first stop. Originally from Karachi, this iconic chain has made its mark in Riyadh with its authentic Pakistani biryani recipes. Their chicken, beef, and mutton biryanis are packed with flavor and spices. The casual dining setting makes it perfect for quick meals without compromising on taste.

3. Lal Qila Restaurant

Lal Qila is designed to reflect the grandeur of Mughal architecture while serving rich Pakistani and Mughlai dishes. The restaurant offers both buffet and Ã  la carte options, featuring curries, kebabs, and traditional desserts. Families especially enjoy the spacious dining area and the cultural ambiance, making it a top choice for gatherings.

4. Salt n Pepper Restaurant

Salt n Pepper is a fine-dining Pakistani restaurant in Riyadh that combines elegance with authentic flavors. From chicken karahi to mutton handi and barbecue platters, every dish is cooked with rich spices. The restaurant is also known for its family-friendly atmosphere and excellent customer service.

5. Bundu Khan Restaurant

Bundu Khan brings the flavors of Lahore to Riyadh with its signature barbecue and curry dishes. Their mutton chops, chicken malai boti, and parathas are customer favorites. The restaurant has a warm ambiance, making it an excellent place for family meals and group dining.

6. Lasani Restaurant

Lasani is a well-known casual Pakistani restaurant offering affordable yet delicious meals. Their chicken karahi, nihari, and paya are very popular among Pakistani food lovers. The restaurant provides both dine-in and takeaway services, making it a convenient option for quick and authentic meals.

7. Red Onion Restaurant

Red Onion offers a mix of Pakistani, Indian, and Middle Eastern dishes, but its Pakistani menu is the real star. From biryani and karahi to barbecue platters, the flavors are rich and satisfying. The casual setting and reasonable pricing make it a go-to spot for students and families.

8. Shahjahan Restaurant

Shahjahan is another hidden gem in Riyadh that focuses on traditional Pakistani food. Their curries, kababs, and fresh rotis are cooked with authentic spices and flavors. The simple ambiance is balanced by the quality of the food, making it a favorite for those who want a homely Pakistani dining experience.

Biryani and chicken karahi are among the most popular Pakistani dishes in Riyadh.

Final Words

Pakistani cuisine in Riyadh offers a variety of options for every taste and budget. From fine dining restaurants like Lal Qila and Salt n Pepper to casual eateries like Student Biryani and Lasani, the city has something for everyone. If you’re craving rich curries, smoky kebabs, or classic biryani, these 8 best Pakistani restaurants in Riyadh will not disappoint.
