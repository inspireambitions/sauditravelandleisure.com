Top 15 Places to Visit in Al Khobar [2025 Update]

2025-09-21

Al Khobar is one of the most vibrant cities in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. Known for its stunning coastline, luxury malls, lively streets, and cultural attractions, Al Khobar is a favorite destination for both locals and tourists.

Whether you love shopping, dining, beaches, or family activities, this city has something for everyone. In this guide, we’ll explore the top 15 places to visit in Al Khobar.
