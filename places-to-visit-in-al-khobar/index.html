Top 15 Places to Visit in Al Khobar [2025 Update]

Updated September 21, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Al Khobar is one of the most vibrant cities in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. Known for its stunning coastline, luxury malls, lively streets, and cultural attractions, Al Khobar is a favorite destination for both locals and tourists.

Whether you love shopping, dining, beaches, or family activities, this city has something for everyone. In this guide, we’ll explore the top 15 places to visit in Al Khobar.

What is Al Khobar famous for?

Al Khobar is known for its corniche, luxury shopping malls, Half Moon Bay, and King Fahd Causeway.

2. Is Al Khobar family-friendly?

Yes, the city has many parks, beaches, and malls suitable for families.

3. Can I cross to Bahrain from Al Khobar?

Yes, via the King Fahd Causeway, which connects Al Khobar to Bahrain.

4. What is the best time to visit Al Khobar?

The best time is October to March when the weather is pleasant.

5. Are beaches in Al Khobar free?

Yes, most public beaches are free, while private resorts may charge entry fees.
