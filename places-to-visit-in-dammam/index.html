Explore 15 Top Attractions in Dammam, Saudi Arabia

Updated September 21, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Dammam, the capital of Saudi Arabia’s Eastern Province, is a lively coastal city on the Arabian Gulf. It is known for its beautiful corniche, cultural landmarks, modern shopping centers, and family-friendly attractions. Dammam is also well-connected to Al Khobar and Dhahran, making it a perfect hub for exploring the Eastern Region.

If you’re planning a trip, here are the top 15 places to visit in Dammam that you shouldn’t miss.

Final Words

Dammam is more than just a modern port city — it’s a mix of beaches, history, parks, shopping, and family fun. Whether you’re traveling with kids, friends, or on a solo trip, these top 15 places in Dammam will give you an unforgettable experience.

â FAQ about Places to Visit in Dammam

1. What is Dammam best known for?

Dammam is famous for its Corniche, Marjan Island, and shopping malls, along with being the hub of the Eastern Province.

2. Is Dammam family-friendly?

Yes, with plenty of parks, beaches, malls, and a zoo, it’s perfect for families.

3. What is the best time to visit Dammam?

The best time is between October and March when the weather is cooler.

4. Can I do water sports in Dammam?

Yes, places like Half Moon Bay and Coral Island are great for swimming, boating, and fishing.

5. How far is Dammam from Al Khobar?

It’s about 25 km (20–25 minutes by car), making it easy to visit both cities in one trip.
