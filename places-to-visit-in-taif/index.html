Top 15 Places to Visit in Taif City

2025-09-21

Taif, known as the “City of Roses”, is one of Saudi Arabia’s most charming destinations. Located in the mountains of the Hejaz region, Taif is famous for its cool climate, fragrant rose gardens, historical sites, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Whether you love history, shopping, or outdoor adventures, Taif has something for everyone. In this guide, let’s explore the top 15 places to visit in Taif that you shouldn’t miss.

1. What is Taif famous for?

Taif is famous for its cool weather, rose gardens, and mountain views.
