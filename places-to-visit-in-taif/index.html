Top 15 Places to Visit in Taif City

Updated September 21, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Taif, known as the “City of Roses”, is one of Saudi Arabia’s most charming destinations. Located in the mountains of the Hejaz region, Taif is famous for its cool climate, fragrant rose gardens, historical sites, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Whether you love history, shopping, or outdoor adventures, Taif has something for everyone. In this guide, let’s explore the top 15 places to visit in Taif that you shouldn’t miss.

Final Words

Taif is a hidden gem of Saudi Arabia. From breathtaking mountains and rose farms to historical palaces and modern parks, the city offers a perfect blend of tradition and adventure. Whether you’re planning a short trip or a week-long stay, these top 15 places to visit in Taif will make your journey unforgettable.

â FAQ about Places to Visit in Taif

1. What is Taif famous for?

Taif is famous for its cool weather, rose gardens, and mountain views.

2. When is the best time to visit Taif?

The best time is March to May and September to November for pleasant weather.

3. Is Taif good for families?

Yes, Taif has many family-friendly attractions like Al Rudaf Park, King Fahd Park, and Taif Zoo.

4. How far is Taif from Makkah?

It’s about 70–80 km (1.5 to 2 hours by car).

5. Can I buy rose products in Taif?

Yes! Taif is the best place to buy rose perfumes, oils, and rose water directly from local factories.
