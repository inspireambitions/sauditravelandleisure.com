Top 20 Best Restaurants to Try in Abha for Dining Experience

2025-08-26

Abha, the charming city in the Asir region of Saudi Arabia, is not only famous for its cool climate, green mountains, and cultural heritage but also for its vibrant food scene. Whether you’re a fan of authentic Saudi dishes, international flavors, or modern dining experiences, Abha has plenty of options to satisfy every taste. From local eateries serving traditional Arabian food to fine dining spots with global cuisines, the city offers an amazing variety for food lovers.

In this updated 2025 guide, we’ve rounded up the Top 20 Restaurants in Abha that you must try. Each restaurant brings a unique experience, making your trip to Abha truly unforgettable.

Best Restaurants in Abha

1. Al Sinara Restaurant

Al Sinara is a well-known seafood restaurant in Abha. Guests can enjoy a wide variety of freshly prepared fish and shrimp dishes in a family-friendly atmosphere. The cozy interiors and traditional Arabian-style seating make it a great place for gatherings.

2. Fire Grill

Fire Grill is a popular choice for those who love Tex-Mex cuisine. With tacos, burritos, and sizzling fajitas on the menu, this spot adds an international touch to Abha’s food scene. Perfect for casual dining.

3. Green Mountain Restaurant

Located near the famous Green Mountain, this restaurant offers breathtaking views along with a delicious menu. Known for its traditional Saudi dishes and grilled specialties, it’s a must-visit for tourists.

4. Al Romansiah

Al Romansiah is one of the most loved Saudi chains serving authentic Kabsa, Mandi, and other Arabian rice dishes. It’s a great option for families looking to experience local flavors in generous portions.

5. The View Restaurant

As the name suggests, The View Restaurant offers stunning panoramic sights of Abha’s mountains while serving a mix of Arabian and international cuisine. A romantic spot for couples.

6. Tandoori House

If you love South Asian food, Tandoori House is the perfect choice. Famous for its flavorful curries, biryanis, and tandoori kebabs, it’s a favorite among Indian and Pakistani food lovers.

7. N Lounge

N Lounge offers a modern dining experience with international dishes, mocktails, and a vibrant atmosphere. With stylish interiors, it is perfect for young crowds and social gatherings.

8. Al Romansiah Express

For quick bites of authentic Saudi food, Al Romansiah Express is a more casual version of the chain. It’s great for those who want fast service without compromising on taste.

9. Palm Court Restaurant

Palm Court offers a mix of Middle Eastern and international dishes. Known for its elegant ambiance and fine service, it’s ideal for business lunches or family dinners.

10. Abha Palace Hotel Restaurant

Located inside Abha Palace Hotel, this restaurant provides a luxury dining experience. With a wide buffet selection and Ã  la carte options, it caters to both local and international tastes.

11. Al Romansiah Kabsa Spot

Specializing in traditional Saudi rice dishes, this branch of Al Romansiah focuses on Kabsa and Mandi served in a homely setting. Perfect for travelers looking for a taste of authenticity.

12. Al Romansiah Grill House

This variation of the Al Romansiah chain is known for its grilled meats and BBQ platters. A popular place for gatherings, it offers large portions and great value.

13. Starbucks Abha

For those who love coffee culture, Starbucks in Abha provides a cozy corner to relax with global coffee blends, desserts, and light meals. A popular hangout spot.

14. Abu Zaid Restaurant

Abu Zaid is well known for its affordable yet delicious Saudi and Middle Eastern dishes. A family-friendly restaurant offering a quick service experience.

15. Pizza Hut Abha

Pizza Hut remains a go-to spot for pizza lovers. From classic cheese to loaded toppings, it’s a great option for families with kids.

16. Shawaya House

Shawaya House is one of the best places in Abha for grilled chicken, traditional stews, and rice dishes. It combines fast service with homely food flavors.

17. Herfy Abha

Herfy is a local Saudi fast-food chain offering burgers, fried chicken, and sandwiches. It’s a budget-friendly spot perfect for quick meals.

18. Burger Fuel

For gourmet burger fans, Burger Fuel is the right place. Offering large burgers with premium ingredients, it’s a modern spot with a vibrant vibe.

19. Kudu

Kudu is a popular fast-food chain in Saudi Arabia known for breakfast sandwiches, burgers, and wraps. A convenient choice for travelers in a hurry.

20. Broccoli Pizza & Pasta

This restaurant offers Italian-inspired fast food like pizzas, pastas, and salads. Known for its quick service and fresh ingredients, it’s a great choice for casual dining.

Final Words

Abha’s food scene reflects the diversity of its culture and people. Whether you’re craving traditional Saudi dishes, Indian curries, Mexican tacos, or gourmet burgers, the city has something for everyone. The Top 20 Restaurants in Abha listed above guarantee a memorable dining experience for tourists and locals alike.

So, on your next trip to Abha, make sure you try at least a few from this list—you won’t be disappointed!

FAQs about Restaurants in Abha


Q1: What food is Abha famous for?

Abha is known for traditional Saudi dishes like Kabsa, Mandi, and grilled meats, as well as a variety of international cuisines.

Q2: Are there vegetarian options in Abha restaurants?

Yes, many restaurants in Abha offer vegetarian and vegan dishes, especially international and Indian restaurants.

Q3: What is the average cost of dining in Abha?

Casual dining can cost around 30–50 SAR per person, while fine dining restaurants may cost 100–200 SAR per person.

Q4: Are there family-friendly restaurants in Abha?

Yes, most restaurants in Abha are family-friendly and provide private seating areas.

Q5: Can I find international cuisines in Abha?

Absolutely. Abha offers Indian, Pakistani, Italian, Mexican, Turkish, and Asian restaurants along with local Saudi options.
