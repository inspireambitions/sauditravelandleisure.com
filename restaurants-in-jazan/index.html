Top 20 Restaurants in Jazan 2025 Updated

2025-08-27

Jazan, located in the southwest of Saudi Arabia, is famous for its coastal beauty, fresh seafood, and unique local flavors.

As the city grows, so does its dining scene. Whether you’re craving traditional Saudi dishes, fresh fish by the Red Sea, or international flavors, the restaurants in Jazan offer a mix of affordable local gems and premium dining spots.

In this 2025 updated guide, we highlight the Top 20 restaurants in Jazan, with details on pros, cons, price ranges, and what each is best known for.

FAQs About Restaurants in Jazan


Q1: What are the best seafood restaurants in Jazan?

Al Marsah and Fish Market are the most popular for fresh seafood.

Q2: Are there affordable restaurants in Jazan?

Yes, Al Khayal, Shawarma House, and Al Manar are budget-friendly.

Q3: Which restaurants in Jazan are good for families?

Al Nakheel, Jazan Palace, and Al Qasr Al Malaki are family-friendly.

Q4: Are there vegetarian-friendly restaurants in Jazan?

Yes, Bamboo Asian and La Venezia offer vegetarian dishes.

Q5: What fine dining restaurants are in Jazan?

Steak House and L’Azurde International Restaurant are top fine dining picks.
