Top 20 Restaurants in Jeddah 2025 Updated

2025-08-26

Jeddah, the gateway to the holy city of Mecca, is not only a cultural and historical hub but also a culinary paradise. The city is known for its diverse food scene, from traditional Saudi cuisine to international flavors. Whether you’re a food lover, a tourist, or a local resident, exploring Jeddah’s restaurants is a must.

In this guide, we present the top 20 restaurants in Jeddah for 2025, covering various cuisines, ambiances, and budgets. Each restaurant offers a unique dining experience, making your culinary journey unforgettable.

Best Restaurants in Jeddah

1. Al-Najdiyah Village

Famous for authentic Saudi cuisine, Al-Najdiyah Village offers a traditional dining experience. The interior is designed like an old Saudi village, providing a cultural ambiance.

Highlights: Authentic Saudi dishes, traditional ambiance
Ideal For: Tourists seeking local flavors

2. Shababik Restaurant

Shababik offers Middle Eastern cuisine with a modern twist. Known for its grilled meats and seafood, it combines tradition with contemporary dining.

Highlights: Middle Eastern cuisine, family-friendly
Ideal For: Families and groups

3. Toki Restaurant

Toki specializes in Asian cuisine, particularly Chinese and Japanese dishes. It’s popular for sushi, dim sum, and flavorful stir-fries.

Highlights: Sushi, Asian fusion, elegant interiors
Ideal For: Couples and food enthusiasts

4. Sushi Yoshi

A high-end sushi restaurant, Sushi Yoshi is known for fresh seafood and authentic Japanese preparation. The minimalist design enhances the dining experience.

Highlights: Sushi, Japanese cuisine, premium ingredients
Ideal For: Japanese cuisine lovers

5. Nozomi

Nozomi is an upscale Japanese restaurant offering sushi, sashimi, and other Asian delicacies. It’s perfect for a fine dining experience in Jeddah.

Highlights: Sushi, Japanese fine dining, modern ambiance
Ideal For: Couples and business dinners

6. Lusin

Lusin offers Armenian and Mediterranean cuisine with a luxurious touch. The restaurant is famous for its mezze, grilled meats, and desserts.

Highlights: Armenian cuisine, Mediterranean dishes, elegant interiors
Ideal For: Romantic dinners and family gatherings

7. The Globe

Located atop the iconic Jeddah Tower, The Globe provides panoramic city views and international cuisine. It’s ideal for special occasions and breathtaking experiences.

Highlights: Panoramic views, international cuisine, fine dining
Ideal For: Special occasions and romantic dinners

8. Myazu

Myazu offers Japanese and Asian fusion in a stylish environment. Guests enjoy sushi, noodles, and creative dishes with premium ingredients.

Highlights: Japanese fusion, sushi, modern ambiance
Ideal For: Asian cuisine enthusiasts

9. Byblos

Byblos specializes in Lebanese cuisine, serving traditional mezze, grills, and fresh seafood. The restaurant’s elegant décor enhances the dining experience.

Highlights: Lebanese cuisine, mezze, seafood
Ideal For: Families and groups

10. Piatto

Piatto is an Italian restaurant offering pasta, pizza, and gourmet desserts. It’s perfect for casual dining with friends or family.

Highlights: Italian cuisine, pizza, pasta
Ideal For: Italian food lovers

11. Il Gusto

Il Gusto serves authentic Italian dishes in a cozy yet sophisticated environment. Guests rave about the pasta, risotto, and fresh ingredients.

Highlights: Italian cuisine, cozy ambiance, fine dining
Ideal For: Italian cuisine enthusiasts

12. Al Khodariyah Restaurant

Specializing in traditional Saudi seafood, Al Khodariyah is known for its freshly caught fish and coastal dishes.

Highlights: Fresh seafood, Saudi coastal dishes
Ideal For: Seafood lovers

13. Kabsa House

Kabsa House is famous for traditional Saudi rice dishes like kabsa and mandi. Its family-friendly atmosphere makes it a popular choice among locals.

Highlights: Saudi kabsa, family-friendly, traditional flavors
Ideal For: Local cuisine enthusiasts

14. Nozomi Garden

A branch of Nozomi, this restaurant combines Japanese cuisine with a beautiful garden setting, offering a serene dining experience.

Highlights: Sushi, Japanese cuisine, garden ambiance
Ideal For: Couples and peaceful dining

15. Al Baik

Al Baik is a Saudi fast-food icon, famous for its fried chicken and signature sauces. It’s a must-try for both locals and tourists.

Highlights: Fried chicken, fast food, iconic brand
Ideal For: Quick meals and casual dining

16. Paul Bakery & Restaurant

Paul offers French bakery delights, pastries, and continental dishes. It’s a perfect spot for breakfast, brunch, or coffee.

Highlights: French pastries, continental dishes, cozy environment
Ideal For: Breakfast and casual dining

17. Reem Al Bawadi

Reem Al Bawadi serves authentic Middle Eastern cuisine with a rich and cultural atmosphere. Its mezzes, grills, and traditional desserts are highly praised.

Highlights: Middle Eastern cuisine, cultural ambiance
Ideal For: Family gatherings and tourists
