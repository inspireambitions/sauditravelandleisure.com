Top 20 Restaurants to Try in Jubail for an Amazing Culinary Experience

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Jubail, located in Saudi Arabia's Eastern Province, boasts a vibrant culinary scene that caters to diverse palates. From traditional Saudi dishes to international cuisines, the city offers a plethora of dining options. Here's a curated list of the top 20 restaurants in Jubail for 2025

Best Restaurants in Jubail

1. Brasa De Brazil

Cuisine: Brazilian Steakhouse
Location: Fanateer, Jubail
Highlights: Authentic Brazilian barbecue, extensive salad bar, and a variety of grilled meats served tableside.
Price Range: SAR 150–250 per person
Pros: High-quality meats, vibrant atmosphere, and excellent service.
Cons: Can be pricey for some diners

2. Steak House

Cuisine: American Steakhouse
Location: Various locations in Jubail
Highlights: Juicy steaks, burgers, and a selection of appetizers.
Price Range: SAR 100–200 per person
Pros: Consistent quality, family-friendly environment, and prompt service.
Cons: Limited vegetarian options.

3. Al Diwan Restaurant

Cuisine: Lebanese
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Traditional Lebanese mezze, grilled meats, and fresh bread.
Price Range: SAR 80–180 per person
Pros: Authentic flavors, cozy ambiance, and attentive staff.
Cons: Occasional wait times during peak hours.

4. Piatto Italian Restaurant

Cuisine: Italian
Location: Fanateer, Jubail
Highlights: Wood-fired pizzas, creamy pastas, and delectable desserts.
Price Range: SAR 90–200 per person
Pros: Authentic Italian dishes, elegant decor, and family-friendly.
Cons: Can be crowded during weekends

5. Puli Contemporary Multi Cuisine

Cuisine: Indian, Chinese, Asian Fusion
Location: Prince Metib St, Jubail
Highlights: Vibrant fusion of Indian, Chinese, and Asian cuisines.
Price Range: SAR 70–160 per person
Pros: Diverse menu, flavorful dishes, and modern ambiance.
Cons: Spicy dishes may not suit all palates

6. Outback Steakhouse

Cuisine: Australian-American
Location: Jubail Mall
Highlights: Signature steaks, bloomin' onion, and hearty sides.
Price Range: SAR 100–220 per person
Pros: Consistent quality, casual dining, and generous portions.
Cons: Can be noisy during peak hours.

7. Red Ginger Restaurant

Cuisine: Asian Fusion
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Exquisite Asian flavors with a modern twist.
Price Range: SAR 80–180 per person
Pros: Innovative dishes, stylish decor, and attentive service.
Cons: Limited dessert options

8. Royal Malabar Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Authentic South Indian cuisine, including biryanis and seafood.
Price Range: SAR 60–150 per person
Pros: Flavorful dishes, friendly staff, and cozy ambiance.
Cons: Spicy for some diners.

9. Taya Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian, Middle Eastern Fusion
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Fusion of South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisines with vegetarian and vegan options.
Price Range: SAR 70–160 per person
Pros: Innovative dishes, high-quality ingredients, and excellent hospitality.
Cons: Some dishes may be spicy for certain palates.

10. Rice & Spice Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Rich flavors of Indian cuisine, including biryanis and curries.
Price Range: SAR 60–140 per person
Pros: Authentic flavors, generous portions, and affordable prices.
Cons: Limited seating during peak hours.

11. Cookxon Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Traditional Indian dishes with a modern presentation.
Price Range: SAR 70–160 per person
Pros: Consistent quality, flavorful dishes, and attentive service.
Cons: Limited dessert options.

12. Applebee’s

Cuisine: American
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Classic American dishes, including burgers, ribs, and salads.
Price Range: SAR 80–180 per person
Pros: Family-friendly, casual atmosphere, and diverse menu.
Cons: Can be crowded during weekends.

13. Great Wall Chinese & Seafood Restaurant

Cuisine: Chinese, Seafood
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Vibrant tastes of China and fresh seafood.
Price Range: SAR 70–150 per person
Pros: Fresh ingredients, flavorful dishes, and attentive service.
Cons: Limited vegetarian options.

14. Al Tuwayah Buffet Restaurant

Cuisine: Middle Eastern, Barbecue
Location: Al Huwaylat, Jubail
Highlights: Open buffet with a variety of Middle Eastern dishes.
Price Range: SAR 50–120 per person
Pros: Wide variety, affordable prices, and family-friendly.
Cons: Can be crowded during peak hours.

15. The Ranch Restaurant

Cuisine: International, Mexican
Location: Al Sinaiyah, Jubail
Highlights: Delicious Mexican cuisines served with a touch of classic Latin American flavors.
Price Range: SAR 70–160 per person
Pros: Diverse menu, flavorful dishes, and cozy ambiance.
Cons: Limited dessert options.

16. Regal Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Authentic Indian cuisine with a variety of dishes.
Price Range: SAR 60–140 per person
Pros: Flavorful dishes, friendly staff, and affordable prices.
Cons: Limited seating during peak hours.

17. The Grill Hut

Cuisine: Barbecue
Location: Jubail
Highlights: Best barbecue delights with unique presentation.
Price Range: SAR 80–180 per person
Pros: Generous portions, flavorful dishes, and casual atmosphere.
Cons: Can be noisy during peak hours.

18. Rummaan Hyderabadi Restaurant

Cuisine: Indian
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Authentic Hyderabadi cuisine with traditional recipes.
Price Range: SAR 70–160 per person
Pros: Flavorful dishes, friendly staff, and cozy ambiance.
Cons: Spicy for some diners.

19. OK BAB Korean Restaurant

Cuisine: Korean
Location: Jubail City Center
Highlights: Traditional Korean dishes including bibimbap, Korean BBQ, and kimchi.

Price Range: SAR 80–180 per person
Pros:

  • Authentic Korean flavors
  • Attentive staff
  • Cozy ambiance

Cons:

  • Can be spicy for some
  • Limited seating

20. Café Liwan Jubail

Cuisine: Café / Light Meals
Location: Al Fanateer, Jubail
Highlights: Specialty coffee, desserts, sandwiches, and salads in a modern setting.

Price Range: SAR 50–120 per person
Pros:

  • Instagram-worthy interiors
  • Great coffee and desserts
  • Relaxed atmosphere

Cons:

  • Small main courses
  • Crowded in evenings

Final Words

Jubail has a diverse and vibrant food scene, ranging from traditional Saudi cuisine to international delights. Whether you want authentic Indian curries, Korean BBQ, fresh seafood, or Italian pizzas, Jubail has something for everyone. These Top 20 Restaurants in Jubail 2025 are perfect for families, couples, friends, and solo travelers looking for a memorable dining experience.

FAQ – Restaurants in Jubail

Q1: What are the best fine dining restaurants in Jubail?

Brasa de Brazil, Lusin, and Taya offer elegant ambiance and premium menus.

Q2: Are there affordable restaurants in Jubail for families?

Al Romansiah, Al Tuwayah Buffet, and Regal Restaurant offer tasty meals at reasonable prices.

Q3: Which restaurants serve international cuisine in Jubail?

Piatto (Italian), Sushi Counter (Japanese), and OK BAB (Korean) are popular international options.

Q4: Are there vegetarian options in Jubail restaurants?

Yes, Puli Contemporary Multi Cuisine, Taya, and Café Liwan provide vegetarian-friendly dishes.
