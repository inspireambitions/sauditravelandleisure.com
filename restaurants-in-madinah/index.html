Top 20 Restaurants to Visit in Madinah for Incredible Journey

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Madinah, the second holiest city in Islam, is not only known for its spiritual significance but also for its rich and diverse food culture. From traditional Saudi flavors to international cuisines, the city offers an incredible dining experience for locals, pilgrims, and tourists alike. Whether you are looking for fine dining, casual family-friendly spots, or quick bites, Madinah has something for everyone.

In this guide, we’ve updated the Top 20 Restaurants in Madinah for 2025, covering a wide range of cuisines and ambiances to help you find the perfect place to enjoy your meal.

Best Restaurants In Madinah

1. Al Baik

One of Saudi Arabia’s most famous fast-food chains, Al Baik is a must-visit in Madinah. Known for its crispy fried chicken, garlic sauce, and affordable meals, it’s always packed with hungry customers.

2. Hardee’s

For lovers of burgers and American fast food, Hardee’s is a great choice in Madinah. With fresh ingredients and juicy burgers, it offers a quick and satisfying dining experience.

3. Swiss House Restaurant

A family-friendly spot, Swiss House offers steaks, pasta, seafood, and desserts. The cozy interior and extensive menu make it one of the most popular international dining options.

4. Abu Tarboosh

Craving Lebanese cuisine? Abu Tarboosh is a top choice. From shawarma and mixed grills to hummus and tabbouleh, the restaurant serves authentic Middle Eastern flavors.

5. Al Fateh Restaurant

This restaurant is known for its traditional Pakistani and Indian food. From biryani to kebabs, it attracts visitors who love spicy and flavorful dishes.

6. Steaky Restaurant

As the name suggests, Steaky specializes in premium steaks and international main courses. The restaurant is elegant and perfect for a slightly upscale dinner in Madinah.

7. Arabesque Restaurant – Shaza Al Madinah Hotel

Located inside the luxurious Shaza Al Madinah Hotel, Arabesque offers fine dining with Arabic, Asian, and Mediterranean flavors. The ambiance and buffet spread make it an excellent choice.

8. At-tabaq Restaurant

A local favorite, At-tabaq is known for serving Saudi dishes and Arabian rice platters like Kabsa and Mandi. It’s budget-friendly and great for families.

9. Shahd Al Madinah

If you’re looking for traditional Saudi food, Shahd Al Madinah serves delicious lamb dishes, grilled chicken, and authentic rice platters.

10. Al Rawdha Restaurant

This is a perfect option for casual family dining, offering both Middle Eastern and international dishes. Their grilled specialties and mixed platters are highly recommended.

11. Istanbul Restaurant

Istanbul Restaurant brings authentic Turkish cuisine to Madinah. From kebabs and pide to baklava, it’s a favorite for those who love Turkish flavors.

12. House of Donuts

For a sweet treat, House of Donuts is an iconic stop. Their donuts, pastries, and coffee are perfect for breakfast or an evening snack.

13. Palm Restaurant

This family-oriented restaurant serves a wide mix of Arabian and continental dishes. Their friendly service and generous portions make it stand out.

14. Fish Market Restaurant

Seafood lovers will enjoy Fish Market Restaurant, where fresh fish and prawns are cooked to order. A great option for a different dining experience.

15. Al Modeef Restaurant

A popular spot for both locals and tourists, Al Modeef specializes in traditional and modern Arabian dishes, blending flavor with affordability.

16. Zaika Indian Restaurant

For authentic Indian food, Zaika is a go-to spot. Their curries, naan, and tandoori dishes are flavorful and loved by many visitors.

17. Fettuccine Restaurant

If you’re craving Italian cuisine, Fettuccine Restaurant offers pasta, pizza, and desserts in a cozy setting. A good choice for families and couples.

18. Domino’s Pizza

A global favorite, Domino’s Pizza offers quick and easy meals with its wide range of pizzas, pasta, and sides. Perfect for a casual bite.

19. Zoosh Restaurant

This modern eatery offers a mix of local and international food. Their creative menu and stylish interiors make it popular among young diners.

20. Starbucks

For coffee lovers, Starbucks in Madinah is always a reliable choice. From coffee and frappuccinos to quick bites, it’s perfect for a light snack.

Final Words

Madinah’s food culture reflects its diversity, history, and hospitality. From world-famous chains like Al Baik and Domino’s to fine-dining spots like Arabesque and family favorites like Abu Tarboosh, there’s something for everyone in the city. Whether you’re here as a pilgrim, tourist, or resident, these Top 20 Restaurants in Madinah (2025 Updated) will ensure you have a memorable dining experience.

What food is Madinah most famous for?

Madinah is famous for traditional Saudi dishes like Kabsa, Mandi, and grilled lamb.

Are there vegetarian options in Madinah restaurants?

Yes, many restaurants, including international ones, serve vegetarian dishes.

What is the most popular fast-food restaurant in Madinah?

Al Baik is the most popular fast-food restaurant in Madinah, known for its fried chicken.

Are there international cuisines available in Madinah?

Yes, you can find Indian, Turkish, Italian, American, and Chinese restaurants in Madinah.
