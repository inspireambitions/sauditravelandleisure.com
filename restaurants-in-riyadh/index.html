Top 20 Restaurants to Try in Riyadh for an Unforgettable Dining Experience

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is not only the capital of Saudi Arabia but also a growing food destination. The city offers a mix of traditional Saudi cuisine, Middle Eastern flavors, and international fine dining. From luxury restaurants to casual street food, Riyadh has it all.

In 2025, the dining scene continues to expand with new spots opening every month. Whether you want authentic Arabic dishes, premium steakhouses, or global cuisines, Riyadh will surprise you.

Here are the Top 20 Restaurants in Riyadh

1. Nozomi Riyadh

Nozomi is one of the best Japanese restaurants in Riyadh. It is famous for its sushi, robata grills, and fine dining atmosphere. The modern décor with dim lighting makes it perfect for dinner with friends or business partners. The menu mixes traditional Japanese flavors with a modern twist. Their wagyu beef, black cod, and sushi rolls are highly recommended.

2. Spazio 77

Located on the 77th floor of Kingdom Centre, Spazio offers luxury dining with a view. It serves Italian and international cuisine. Guests love the pasta, seafood, and desserts. The restaurant is ideal for special occasions or romantic dinners. The view from the top is breathtaking, especially at night.

3. Al Orjouan – Ritz Carlton

Al Orjouan is known for its grand international buffet. It offers Middle Eastern dishes, seafood nights, and a wide selection of desserts. Located inside The Ritz-Carlton Riyadh, it is popular for family brunches and celebrations. The elegant atmosphere and wide variety make it a must-try.

4. Hualan

Hualan offers modern Chinese dining in Riyadh. It is famous for its Peking duck, dim sum, and creative Asian dishes. The design of the restaurant is stylish with Chinese art and modern interiors. Perfect for those who enjoy high-end Asian cuisine.

5. Myazu

Myazu is a Japanese fine dining restaurant that has gained popularity in recent years. It offers sushi, teppanyaki, and modern Japanese fusion dishes. The presentation is artistic, and the flavors are rich. Many call it one of the best dining experiences in Riyadh.

6. Assaraya Turkish Restaurant

For Turkish food lovers, Assaraya is a local favorite. It offers kebabs, grilled meats, and traditional Turkish bread. The prices are affordable, and the portions are big. Families love this restaurant for its authentic flavors and friendly service.

7. Lusin

Lusin is a well-known Armenian restaurant. It offers a unique dining experience with dishes like kebabs, manti dumplings, and tabbouleh. The interiors are elegant with Armenian cultural touches. Lusin is a top choice for those looking for something different from regular Middle Eastern cuisine.

8. Amo Hamza Seafood

Amo Hamza is one of the best seafood restaurants in Riyadh. It is known for grilled fish, shrimp platters, and seafood soups. The restaurant has multiple branches, making it easy to find. Families often choose it for casual seafood dining.

9. Elements – Four Seasons Hotel

Elements is a fine dining restaurant inside the Four Seasons Riyadh. It offers international cuisine with live cooking stations. Guests can enjoy Asian, Arabic, and Western dishes. The brunch here is very popular. With a luxurious setting, it is perfect for families and business travelers.

10. Al Nafoura Lebanese Restaurant

Al Nafoura offers traditional Lebanese food with classic flavors. Shawarma, grilled meats, and mezze are the highlights. It is casual and family-friendly. The atmosphere is simple but welcoming, making it a go-to for Lebanese cuisine lovers.

11. Tokyo Restaurant

Tokyo Restaurant is one of the first Japanese restaurants in Riyadh. It is loved for its sushi, ramen, and bento boxes. The setting is casual but stylish. Many locals and tourists choose it for affordable yet authentic Japanese food.

12. Najd Village

Najd Village gives you the true taste of Saudi cuisine. The restaurant is designed like a traditional Saudi village with carpets, cushions, and cultural décor. The food includes kabsa, jareesh, and lamb dishes. Eating here feels like traveling back in time to old Riyadh.

13. Mirage Chinese Restaurant

Mirage is another great Chinese restaurant in Riyadh. It is known for hot pots, dim sum, and seafood dishes. The restaurant has a family-friendly environment and traditional Chinese design. It is perfect for group dinners.

14. The Globe – Al Faisaliah Tower

The Globe is one of Riyadh’s most unique restaurants. It is located inside a golden sphere at the top of Al Faisaliah Tower. The view is spectacular, and the menu offers international gourmet dishes. Dining here is a luxury experience worth trying.

15. La Brasserie – Al Faisaliah Hotel

La Brasserie is famous for its breakfast and international buffet. It offers live stations, fresh juices, and a wide variety of dishes. Families and business travelers often enjoy its elegant atmosphere and delicious food.

16. Zafran Indian Bistro

Zafran is a popular Indian restaurant in Riyadh. It offers biryanis, curries, and tandoori grills. The flavors are authentic, and the service is excellent. It is a good choice for those who love spicy and aromatic Indian food.

17. Al Romansiah

Al Romansiah is one of the most popular Saudi food chains. It is known for kabsa, mandi, and large family platters. Prices are affordable, and the portions are generous. Locals often recommend it for authentic Saudi dining.

18. Cipriani Riyadh

Cipriani brings Italian fine dining to Riyadh. It is famous for pasta, veal dishes, and its classic tiramisu. The interiors are elegant, inspired by Italian style. It is perfect for business dinners or romantic meals.

19. Piatto

Piatto is a family-friendly Italian chain in Riyadh. It is known for pasta, pizza, and fresh salads. The portions are big, and prices are reasonable. Families love it for casual Italian dining.

20. BiCE Restaurant

BiCE is another fine Italian restaurant in Riyadh. It offers gourmet dishes like risotto, seafood pasta, and steak. The modern interiors and quiet setting make it a good spot for special occasions.

FAQs About Restaurants in Riyadh

What is the most famous restaurant in Riyadh?

Restaurants like Nozomi, Spazio, and Najd Village are among the most famous.

Can tourists find international food in Riyadh?

Yes. Riyadh has Japanese, Italian, Chinese, Indian, and global restaurants.

Are restaurants in Riyadh expensive?

Prices vary. Luxury dining can cost $100+ per person, while casual restaurants are much cheaper.

Do restaurants in Riyadh serve alcohol?

No. Alcohol is banned in Saudi Arabia. Restaurants serve mocktails, fresh juices, and non-alcoholic drinks.

What time do restaurants in Riyadh open?

Most restaurants open by noon and close late at night. Some fine dining restaurants open only in the evening.

Final Words

Riyadh’s dining scene is diverse and exciting. From traditional Saudi restaurants like Najd Village to luxury dining at The Globe and Spazio 77, there is something for everyone. Whether you want Arabic, Asian, Italian, or international cuisine, Riyadh has it all.

These top 20 restaurants in Riyadh will give you unforgettable food experiences. Try a few during your trip and explore the flavors of the capital city.
