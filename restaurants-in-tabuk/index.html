Top 20 Restaurants in Tabuk 2025 Updated

2025-08-27

Tabuk, located in northwest Saudi Arabia, is a growing city that blends tradition with modern living.

Whether you’re a local resident, business traveler, or tourist exploring the historical landmarks, the restaurants in Tabuk have something for everyone.

From authentic Saudi dishes and Middle Eastern grills to international fine dining, Tabuk’s food scene has grown rapidly in 2025.

Here’s our updated list of the Top 20 restaurants in Tabuk, with insights on price range, best experiences, and what makes each spot worth visiting.

Top 20 Restaurants in Tabuk

1. Al Qaryah Al Turathia (Heritage Village)

  • Pros: Traditional Saudi cuisine, cultural atmosphere
  • Cons: Gets busy during weekends
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Families, cultural dining

2. Al Bustan Restaurant

  • Pros: Wide buffet, Arabian and international dishes
  • Cons: Quality varies on busy days
  • Price: $$-$$$
  • Best For: Family gatherings, business lunches

3. Royal Gulf Restaurant

  • Pros: Excellent seafood and grills
  • Cons: Limited vegetarian options
  • Price: $$-$$$
  • Best For: Seafood lovers, group dining

4. Al Basha Restaurant

  • Pros: Authentic Middle Eastern cuisine, large portions
  • Cons: Basic interiors
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Traditional meals, families

5. Fish Market Tabuk

  • Pros: Fresh catch cooked to order, affordable
  • Cons: No luxury experience
  • Price: $-$$
  • Best For: Seafood fans, casual meals

6. Al Andalus Restaurant

  • Pros: Mix of Arabic & Indian dishes, great service
  • Cons: Limited dessert options
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Group dining, casual meals

7. Steak House Tabuk

  • Pros: Juicy steaks, modern interiors
  • Cons: Pricey compared to local eateries
  • Price: $$$
  • Best For: Western food lovers, business dinners

8. Pizza Hut Tabuk

  • Pros: Reliable fast food, quick service
  • Cons: Not unique to Tabuk
  • Price: $
  • Best For: Quick bites, kids-friendly dining

9. Al Shami Restaurant

  • Pros: Delicious Syrian cuisine, affordable
  • Cons: Limited seafood options
  • Price: $-$$
  • Best For: Middle Eastern cuisine lovers

10. Bamboo Asian Restaurant

  • Pros: Authentic Asian dishes (Thai & Chinese)
  • Cons: Small dining area
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Asian cuisine fans, couples

11. Najd Village Restaurant

  • Pros: Traditional Najdi dishes, cultural ambiance
  • Cons: Menu is not very diverse
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Cultural dining, tourists

12. Layali Al Sham Restaurant

  • Pros: Shawarma & Arabic grills, good value for money
  • Cons: Gets crowded in evenings
  • Price: $-$$
  • Best For: Street food lovers, families

13. House of Donuts Tabuk

  • Pros: Fresh pastries, coffee, light snacks

  • Price: $
  • Best For: Coffee breaks, dessert lovers

14. La Venezia Italian Restaurant

  • Pros: Pizzas & pastas, cozy interior
  • Cons: More expensive than local eateries
  • Price: $$-$$$
  • Best For: Italian cuisine, couples

15. Green Garden Restaurant

  • Pros: Variety of Middle Eastern & Indian dishes
  • Cons: Service can be slow
  • Price: $$
  • Best For: Families, group meals

16. Al Manar Restaurant

  • Pros: Affordable, quick service
  • Cons: Limited luxury feel
  • Price: $
  • Best For: Students, budget dining

17. Tabuk Palace Restaurant

  • Pros: Buffet with international options
  • Cons: Quality depends on timing
  • Price: $$-$$$
  • Best For: Business events, large groups

18. Shawarma House

  • Pros: Famous for shawarma, fast service
  • Cons: Not for fine dining
  • Price: $
  • Best For: Quick meals, takeaways

19. Olive Garden Tabuk (International Chain)

  • Pros: Western-style Italian, consistent taste
  • Cons: Higher prices compared to locals
  • Price: $$$
  • Best For: Tourists, Italian food lovers

20. Sweet Land Café

  • Pros: Desserts, cakes, coffee
  • Cons: Limited savory dishes
  • Price: $-$$
  • Best For: Coffee lovers, dessert fans

Final Words

Tabuk offers a diverse food scene where tradition meets modern dining. From authentic Saudi and Middle Eastern kitchens to Western favorites, the restaurants in Tabuk cover every budget and taste. Whether you’re seeking luxury dining, a quick shawarma, or fresh seafood, this updated 2025 guide highlights the best options for your visit.

FAQs About Restaurants in Tabuk

Q1: What are the most famous restaurants in Tabuk?

Al Qaryah Al Turathia, Al Bustan, and Royal Gulf are among the most popular choices.

Q2: Are there affordable restaurants in Tabuk?

Yes, Al Manar, Shawarma House, and Fish Market offer budget-friendly meals.

Q3: Which restaurants in Tabuk are vegetarian-friendly?

Bamboo Asian, La Venezia, and Olive Garden offer vegetarian options.

Q4: Are there fine dining restaurants in Tabuk?

Yes, Steak House, Olive Garden, and Tabuk Palace provide upscale experiences.

Q5: What restaurants in Tabuk are best for families?

Al Bustan, Green Garden, and Najd Village are perfect for family dining.
