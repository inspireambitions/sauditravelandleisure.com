Top 20 Restaurants in Taif 2025 Updated

2025-08-26

Taif, nestled in the mountains of the Makkah Province, is famous for its cool climate, beautiful landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Along with sightseeing and shopping, the city offers a growing and diverse culinary scene. From traditional Saudi dishes to international cuisines, Taif has restaurants that cater to every taste. This guide covers the Top 20 Restaurants in Taif (2025 Updated), including pros, cons, price ranges, and dining highlights.

Best Restaurants in Taif

1. Al Romansiah Taif

Al Romansiah is a Saudi favorite serving Kabsa, Mandi, and other traditional dishes.

Pros:

  • Authentic Saudi flavors
  • Large portions
  • Family-friendly

Cons:

  • Casual dining setup
  • Service can be basic

Price Range: SAR 40 – 90 per person
Best For: Traditional Saudi food, family meals

2. Al Nawras Restaurant

A seafood restaurant offering fresh fish, shrimp, and grilled seafood specialties.

Pros:

  • Fresh seafood

  • Friendly staff

Cons:

  • Limited vegetarian options
  • Slightly expensive

Price Range: SAR 80 – 180 per person
Best For: Seafood lovers, date nights

FAQ – Restaurants in Taif


Q1: What is Taif famous for food-wise?

Taif is known for traditional Saudi dishes like Kabsa, Mandi, and grilled meats. It also has growing options for seafood, Italian, and international cuisines.

Q2: Are there family-friendly restaurants in Taif?

Yes, most restaurants, including Al Romansiah, Piatto, and Nando’s, provide family-friendly seating.

Q3: Can I find international cuisine in Taif?

Absolutely! Italian, Japanese, Lebanese, American, and fusion cuisines are available in restaurants like Piatto, Sushi Counter, and Lusin.

Q4: Are there budget-friendly dining options in Taif?

Yes, Al Romansiah, Al Romansiah Express, and Sayadiyah Express offer affordable meals.

Q5: What is the average dining cost in Taif?

Casual meals cost around SAR 40–100 per person. Fine dining usually ranges from SAR 150–300 per person.

Q6: Are there seafood restaurants in Taif?

Yes, Shrimp Anatomy, Al Nawras, and Sayadiyah Express are popular seafood destinations.
