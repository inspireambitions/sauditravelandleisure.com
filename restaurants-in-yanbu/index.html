Top 20 Must-Try Restaurants in Yanbu for Food Lovers

Updated August 27, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Looking for the best restaurants in Yanbu this 2025? Whether you’re a local or a tourist, Yanbu’s food scene offers everything—from traditional Saudi flavors to international cuisines.

Known as one of Saudi Arabia’s most beautiful coastal cities, Yanbu combines Red Sea views with mouthwatering dining experiences.

In this updated guide, we’ve shortlisted the Top 20 restaurants in Yanbu based on food quality, price, ambiance, and overall experience. Each spot has its pros and cons so you can easily decide where to eat next.

Top 20 Restaurants in Yanbu

  • Cons: Can be crowded on weekends
  • Price: $$$ (Mid to high range)

FAQs About Restaurants in Yanbu


Q1: What is the most famous restaurant in Yanbu?

Al Marsa Seafood Restaurant is among the most popular for its fresh seafood and Red Sea views.

Q2: Are there budget-friendly restaurants in Yanbu?

Yes, places like Pizza Inn, Alkhayal, and Al Manar Restaurant are affordable options.

Q3: Do restaurants in Yanbu offer vegetarian dishes?

Yes, Indian Palace and some international restaurants have vegetarian-friendly menus.

Q4: Which restaurants in Yanbu are best for families?

Al Ahlam, Al Safina, and Al Jood are family-friendly with wide menus and large seating.

Q5: Are there fine dining restaurants in Yanbu?

Yes, Steak House Yanbu, L’Azurde, and The Ocean Restaurant offer premium dining experiences.

Final Words

Yanbu offers a diverse mix of restaurants—from local Arabic spots to international fine dining. Whether you want affordable family meals, authentic seafood, or international flavors, there’s something for everyone.
