Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if you provide false age information?

If you give the wrong age on a Saudi work visa application, it can cause big problems. You could have your work visa application denied or lose your saudi work visa. You might even face legal trouble. It is important that all the information you put on your visa application is right. This helps you avoid issues with your work visa and future job chances.

Is the age limit the same for visa renewals as first-time applications?

For visa renewals, the age limit usually stays the same as it is for people applying for the first time. But, the rules can change depending on the job you have or the country you are from. You should always check the latest policies so there are no problems when you renew your visa.

Where can I confirm the current official age limit rules?

You can find out the latest and correct age limit rules for Saudi work visas by checking the official Saudi government websites, like the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development. You can also get current information by talking to official visa processing agencies.
