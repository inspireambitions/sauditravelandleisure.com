Dubai is more liberal than many of its regional neighbors but showing respect for local customs matters. Dress modestly in places of worship and when visiting public spaces that are not beach- or pool-specific. Avoid loud public displays of affection.

The UAE enforces strict laws around drugs; even small amounts can have severe penalties. Carry official prescriptions for any controlled medications and check permitted substances before travel.

Accessibility and Special Needs

Dubai invests in accessibility across public transport and major attractions, but older neighborhoods may have gaps. If mobility or accessibility is a concern, plan ahead: contact hotels and attractions to confirm facilities, and use ride services for door-to-door convenience.
