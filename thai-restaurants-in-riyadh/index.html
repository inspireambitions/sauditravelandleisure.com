7 Top Best Thai Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 26, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh is home to a diverse culinary scene where international flavors meet traditional Saudi hospitality. Among the global cuisines available, Thai food has carved out a special place with its balance of spicy, sour, sweet, and savory flavors. Whether you’re craving a comforting bowl of Tom Yum soup, flavorful Pad Thai, or a refreshing Som Tum salad, Riyadh offers plenty of authentic Thai dining experiences.

To help you explore, here are the 7 best Thai restaurants in Riyadh that promise an unforgettable journey into Thailand’s vibrant food culture.

Best Thai Restaurants in Riyadh

1. White Elephant Restaurant

One of Riyadh’s most famous Thai restaurants, White Elephant offers a truly authentic experience. Known for its traditional décor and extensive menu, it serves classics like Tom Yum Goong, Pad Thai, and Green Curry. The restaurant is popular among both locals and expats who crave genuine Thai flavors.

Must-Try: Tom Yum Soup, Pad Thai, Green Curry
Location: Olaya, Riyadh

White Elephant is as popular for Thai food lovers as Indian restaurants in Riyadh are for spice enthusiasts.

2. Golden Dragon

While Golden Dragon is primarily known as a pan-Asian restaurant, it has a strong Thai section on its menu. Guests can enjoy Thai curries, noodles, and seafood dishes in a fine-dining setting. The ambiance is elegant, making it ideal for family dinners and business meetings.

Must-Try: Thai Red Curry, Pad See Ew, Mango Sticky Rice
Location: Al Waha, Riyadh

Golden Dragon also serves Chinese dishes, similar to those you’d find in our guide to the best Chinese restaurants in Riyadh

3. Thai Thai Restaurant

A hidden gem for Thai food lovers, Thai Thai Restaurant offers authentic dishes at affordable prices. It’s a cozy spot perfect for casual dining, with flavorful curries, stir-fries, and seafood prepared with fresh herbs and spices.

Must-Try: Thai Basil Chicken, Som Tum Salad, Green Curry
Location: Al Malaz, Riyadh

4. Gulf Royal Chinese & Thai Restaurant

This restaurant blends Chinese and Thai cuisines, offering a wide variety of dishes. It’s particularly loved for its Thai soups and stir-fries. With a spacious dining hall and friendly service, it’s a good spot for family gatherings.

Must-Try: Tom Kha Gai, Thai Fried Rice, Panang Curry
Location: Sulaymaniyah, Riyadh

5. Smokin’ Thai

For those who love modern Thai dining, Smokin’ Thai is a trendy choice. It combines authentic flavors with a contemporary twist, offering everything from Pad Thai to Thai-style BBQ. The restaurant is popular among young diners looking for a stylish atmosphere.

Must-Try: Thai BBQ Skewers, Spicy Noodles, Red Curry
Location: King Fahd Road, Riyadh

6. Thai House Restaurant

A family-friendly spot, Thai House is known for its homely atmosphere and flavorful dishes. The menu features a wide variety of curries, seafood, and stir-fried favorites, all served in generous portions.

Must-Try: Thai Green Curry, Pad Kra Pao, Tom Yum Seafood
Location: Al Murabba, Riyadh

7. Royal Orchid

A well-established Asian restaurant, Royal Orchid offers both Chinese and Thai specialties. Its Thai menu is highly rated for authenticity, with an emphasis on fresh ingredients and bold flavors. The elegant setting also makes it suitable for formal dinners.

Must-Try: Thai Massaman Curry, Pineapple Fried Rice, Thai Spring Rolls
Location: Takhassusi Street, Riyadh

FAQ – Best Thai Restaurants in Riyadh


Are there authentic Thai restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes, many restaurants such as White Elephant and Thai Thai Restaurant serve traditional Thai dishes with authentic flavors.

Which Thai dish is most popular in Riyadh?

Pad Thai, Tom Yum Soup, and Thai Green Curry are among the most loved dishes.

Are Thai restaurants in Riyadh expensive?

It depends—casual spots like Thai Thai Restaurant are budget-friendly, while fine-dining places like Golden Dragon or Royal Orchid are pricier.

Do Thai restaurants in Riyadh serve vegetarian options?

Yes, most Thai restaurants offer vegetarian-friendly dishes like vegetable Pad Thai, Green Curry with tofu, and Som Tum salad.

Can I find Thai desserts in Riyadh?

Yes, Mango Sticky Rice and Thai-style coconut desserts are commonly available in authentic Thai restaurants.

Final Words

Thai food lovers in Riyadh have no shortage of options. From affordable local spots to fine-dining restaurants, these Thai eateries deliver everything from spicy curries to refreshing salads and sweet desserts. Whether you’re a long-time fan of Thai cuisine or trying it for the first time, these 7 best Thai restaurants in Riyadh will satisfy your cravings for authentic and flavorful dishes.
