8 Best Turkish Restaurants in Riyadh

Updated August 25, 2025 By Saudi Travel and Leisure Editorial Team Indexable Saudi guide

Riyadh has become a hub for international cuisine, and Turkish food is among the most loved. From grilled kebabs to meze and baklava, Turkish restaurants offer rich flavors and authentic experiences.

In this guide, we explore the 8 best Turkish restaurants in Riyadh. Whether you want casual dining, family meals, or fine dining, these restaurants bring the taste of Turkey to the capital.

Along with enjoying Turkish flavors, Riyadh also offers amazing options for the Japanese restaurants in Riyadh, best Indian restaurants in Riyadh, top Italian restaurants in Riyadh, French restaurants in Riyadh, and best Chinese restaurants in Riyadh for a full culinary journey.

Best Turkish Restaurants in Riyadh

1. Istanbul Restaurant

Istanbul Restaurant is one of the most popular Turkish restaurants in Riyadh. Known for its authentic kebabs, pide, and meze platters, it offers a warm and traditional atmosphere.

The restaurant focuses on fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Families and groups enjoy dining here because of the large portions and friendly service.

2. Marmara Restaurant

Marmara is famous for its classic Turkish cuisine, including grilled meats, Turkish breakfast, and desserts like baklava and künefe.

The restaurant combines modern interiors with traditional Turkish décor. It’s ideal for casual meals or special family dinners.

3. Sofra Turkish Restaurant

Sofra offers a mix of traditional and contemporary Turkish dishes. Signature items include lamb shish, mixed grills, and freshly baked Turkish bread.

The service is attentive, and the cozy environment makes it a perfect choice for both casual and romantic dinners.

4. Turkuaz Restaurant

Turkuaz is known for its authentic flavors and elegant ambiance. Guests can enjoy kebabs, seafood, and a variety of meze dishes.

The restaurant is perfect for family gatherings or business lunches. Its presentation and quality make it a top choice for Turkish cuisine lovers in Riyadh.

5. Divan Turkish Restaurant

Divan brings a modern Turkish dining experience to Riyadh. The menu features grilled meats, appetizers, and traditional desserts.

Its stylish décor and calm ambiance make it ideal for celebrations or dinner with friends. The restaurant is praised for both flavor and service.

6. Efes Turkish Restaurant

Efes specializes in classic Turkish dishes like kebabs, lamb chops, and meze platters.

The casual setting and reasonable prices make it popular with locals and expats alike. It’s perfect for a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner.

7. Sultan’s Turkish Restaurant

Sultan’s offers a mix of Turkish classics and contemporary dishes. Popular items include pide, lamb shish, and dolma.

The restaurant’s family-friendly environment and consistent quality make it a reliable spot for Turkish cuisine lovers.

8. Anatolia Restaurant

Anatolia focuses on authentic Turkish flavors with a modern twist. The menu features grilled meats, seafood, and traditional desserts like baklava and Turkish delight.

With a comfortable and elegant setting, Anatolia is ideal for casual dining, family meals, or romantic dinners.

FAQ About Turkish Restaurants in Riyadh

What is the best Turkish restaurant in Riyadh for authentic kebabs?

Istanbul Restaurant and Marmara are top choices for authentic Turkish kebabs, grilled meats, and traditional meze.  

Which Turkish restaurants in Riyadh are family-friendly?

Sofra, Turkuaz, and Sultan’s Turkish Restaurant are ideal for families. They offer spacious seating, casual vibes, and a variety of dishes.

Are there affordable Turkish restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes. Efes and Sofra provide authentic Turkish dishes at reasonable prices, making them perfect for casual dining

Which Turkish restaurant is best for groups or celebrations?

Turkuaz, Divan, and Anatolia are perfect for group dining and celebrations. Their spacious interiors and quality service make them ideal for gatherings.

Are there modern or contemporary Turkish restaurants in Riyadh?

Yes. Divan and Anatolia offer modern twists on traditional dishes, combining authentic flavors with stylish presentation.

Final Words

Riyadh has a growing number of international dining options, and Turkish cuisine is among the favorites. From kebabs to meze and desserts, the 8 best Turkish restaurants in Riyadh offer authentic and unforgettable dining experiences.
