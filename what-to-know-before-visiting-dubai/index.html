FAQ

Do I need a visa before I travel to Dubai?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Many countries can obtain a visa on arrival, but others must apply ahead of time. Check official government channels and airline information well before departure to confirm your status and prepare documents.

Is Dubai safe for solo travelers and families?

Yes. Dubai is generally very safe, with low violent crime rates and high-quality public services. Exercise normal travel precautionsâsecure your belongings, avoid risky areas late at night, and follow local laws and cultural norms.

What is the best way to get around the city?

Use a combination of metro, tram, and buses for cost-effective travel between major hubs. For door-to-door convenience, ride-hailing and taxis are reliable. Walking works well within pedestrianized districts, but distances between major areas usually require transit.

How should I dress and behave during Ramadan?

Dress modestly and avoid eating, drinking, or smoking in public during daylight hours. Many restaurants close or operate on restricted hours; plan evening dining and consider booking an iftar experience to observe local traditions respectfully.
