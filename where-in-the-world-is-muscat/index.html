FAQ

Is Muscat in the same country as Oman?

Yes. Muscat is the capital city of the Sultanate of Oman and functions as the countryâs administrative, economic and cultural center.

How far is Muscat from Dubai by air or road?

Flight time between Muscat and Dubai is roughly one hour; driving times vary widely depending on border procedures and chosen routes, but a coastal road drive normally takes several hours. Flights offer the fastest and simplest option for most travelers.

What is the best time of year to visit Muscat?

The most comfortable months are typically October through April, when temperatures are milder and outdoor exploration is easier. Summer months are very hot and humid, which limits extended outdoor activity.

Do I need to speak Arabic to get around Muscat?

No; English is widely spoken in tourist and urban service sectors, and many signs use English in addition to Arabic. Learning a few polite Arabic phrases is appreciated but not required.
