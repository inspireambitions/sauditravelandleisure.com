Baggage reclaim is centrally located; signage in English and Arabic clearly indicates carousel numbers. If you have fragile or special items, use the declared-baggage lane. For customs, familiarize yourself with allowances for currency and items that require declaration.

Vehicles can wait for up to 10 minutes free in the pickup/drop-off forecourt before fees apply. For longer waits, use short-term parking.
