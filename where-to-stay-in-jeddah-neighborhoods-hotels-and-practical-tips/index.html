When To Visit and How Seasons Affect Where You Stay

Jeddah is hot and humid much of the year. The most comfortable months are generally from November through March when temperatures are milder, which also draws more tourists and increases hotel demand. Summer brings higher humidity and fewer visitors; you may find better hotel deals but be prepared for intense heat. During Ramadan or the Hajj season, travel patterns shift and many restaurants and services operate on adjusted schedules; some travelers prefer to stay in larger hotels during Ramadan for consistent 24/7 services.
